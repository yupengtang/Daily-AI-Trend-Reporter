### 1. Geopolitical biases in LLMs: what are the "good" and the "bad" countries
  according to contemporary language models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.06751)

### 2. Autoregressive Semantic Visual Reconstruction Helps VLMs Understand
  Better

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.09040)

Autoregressive Semantic Visual Reconstruction (ASVR) improves multimodal understanding by focusing on semantic reconstruction rather than raw visual appearance, enhancing performance across various benchmarks.

### 3. Self Forcing: Bridging the Train-Test Gap in Autoregressive Video
  Diffusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.08009)

Self Forcing, a novel training method for autoregressive video diffusion models, reduces exposure bias and improves generation quality through holistic video-level supervision and efficient caching mechanisms.

### 4. RuleReasoner: Reinforced Rule-based Reasoning via Domain-aware Dynamic
  Sampling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.08672)

RuleReasoner enhances rule-based reasoning in small models through dynamic domain sampling, achieving superior performance and efficiency compared to large models.

### 5. Seeing Voices: Generating A-Roll Video from Audio with Mirage

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.08279)

Mirage generates realistic video from audio inputs, integrating with speech synthesis to create compelling multimodal content through a unified, self-attention-based training approach.

### 6. Frame Guidance: Training-Free Guidance for Frame-Level Control in Video
  Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.07177)

Frame Guidance offers a training-free method for controlling video generation using frame-level signals, reducing memory usage and enhancing globally coherent video output.

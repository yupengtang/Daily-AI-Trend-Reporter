### 1. rStar2-Agent: Agentic Reasoning Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20722)

rStar2-Agent, a 14B math reasoning model trained with agentic reinforcement learning, achieves state-of-the-art performance by efficiently handling complex problem-solving with advanced cognitive behaviors and minimal computational resources.

### 2. Pref-GRPO: Pairwise Preference Reward-based GRPO for Stable
  Text-to-Image Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20751)

Pref-GRPO, a pairwise preference reward-based GRPO method, enhances text-to-image generation by mitigating reward hacking and improving stability, while UniGenBench provides a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating T2I models.

### 3. MCP-Bench: Benchmarking Tool-Using LLM Agents with Complex Real-World
  Tasks via MCP Servers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20453)

MCP-Bench evaluates large language models on complex, multi-step tasks requiring tool use, coordination, and planning across various domains.

### 4. USO: Unified Style and Subject-Driven Generation via Disentangled and
  Reward Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.18966)

USO, a unified model, achieves state-of-the-art performance in both style similarity and subject consistency by disentangling and re-composing content and style through a disentangled learning scheme and style reward-learning paradigm.

### 5. AWorld: Orchestrating the Training Recipe for Agentic AI

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20404)

AWorld, an open-source system for large-scale agent-environment interaction, accelerates experience collection and enhances reinforcement learning, leading to significant improvements in agentic AI performance on complex benchmarks.

### 6. Mixture of Contexts for Long Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.21058)

Long video generation is addressed by introducing a sparse attention routing module, Mixture of Contexts, to efficiently manage long-term memory and retrieval in diffusion transformers.

### 7. Multi-View 3D Point Tracking

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.21060)

A multi-view 3D point tracker using a feed-forward model with transformers and k-nearest-neighbors achieves robust tracking with fewer cameras and less optimization compared to existing methods.

### 8. TCIA: A Task-Centric Instruction Augmentation Method for Instruction
  Finetuning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20374)

Task Centric Instruction Augmentation (TCIA) enhances large language models' performance on specific tasks while maintaining general instruction-following ability.

### 9. OneReward: Unified Mask-Guided Image Generation via Multi-Task Human
  Preference Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.21066)

A unified reinforcement learning framework using a single vision-language model enhances generative capabilities across multiple tasks without task-specific fine-tuning.

### 10. Turning the Spell Around: Lightweight Alignment Amplification via
  Rank-One Safety Injection

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20766)

Rank-One Safety Injection (ROSI) enhances Large Language Model safety by amplifying refusal-mediating subspace activations without fine-tuning.

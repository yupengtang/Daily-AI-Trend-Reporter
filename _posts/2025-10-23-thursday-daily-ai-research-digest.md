### 1. Every Attention Matters: An Efficient Hybrid Architecture for
  Long-Context Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19338)

The Ring-linear model series, including Ring-mini-linear-2.0 and Ring-flash-linear-2.0, uses a hybrid architecture combining linear and softmax attention to reduce inference costs and improve training efficiency.

### 2. BAPO: Stabilizing Off-Policy Reinforcement Learning for LLMs via
  Balanced Policy Optimization with Adaptive Clipping

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18927)

BAlanced Policy Optimization with Adaptive Clipping (BAPO) addresses challenges in off-policy reinforcement learning by dynamically adjusting clipping bounds to improve sample efficiency, stability, and performance in large language models.

### 3. Language Models are Injective and Hence Invertible

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15511)

Transformer language models are proven to be injective, allowing exact input reconstruction from hidden activations, which has implications for transparency and safety.

### 4. LoongRL:Reinforcement Learning for Advanced Reasoning over Long Contexts

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19363)

LoongRL, a data-driven reinforcement learning method, enhances long-context reasoning by transforming short multi-hop QA into high-difficulty tasks, improving accuracy and generalization in large language models.

### 5. GigaBrain-0: A World Model-Powered Vision-Language-Action Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19430)

GigaBrain-0, a VLA foundation model, uses world model-generated data to enhance cross-task generalization and policy robustness, improving real-world performance on complex manipulation tasks.

### 6. Attention Sinks in Diffusion Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15731)

Empirical analysis of Masked Diffusion Language Models (DLMs) reveals distinct attention sinking phenomena and robustness compared to Autoregressive Models (ARMs).

### 7. Unified Reinforcement and Imitation Learning for Vision-Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19307)

A unified reinforcement and imitation learning algorithm creates efficient, lightweight vision-language models that match or exceed leading VLMs in performance.

### 8. Pico-Banana-400K: A Large-Scale Dataset for Text-Guided Image Editing

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19808)

Pico-Banana-400K is a large-scale, high-quality dataset for instruction-based image editing, featuring diverse edit pairs, multi-turn editing, preference subsets, and long-short instruction pairs, enabling comprehensive research and benchmarking.

### 9. DeepWideSearch: Benchmarking Depth and Width in Agentic Information
  Seeking

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.20168)

DeepWideSearch is a benchmark that evaluates agents' ability to integrate deep reasoning and wide-scale information collection, revealing significant challenges and limitations in current agent architectures.

### 10. HSCodeComp: A Realistic and Expert-level Benchmark for Deep Search
  Agents in Hierarchical Rule Application

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19631)

HSCodeComp evaluates deep search agents' hierarchical rule application in predicting product HS Codes, revealing significant performance gaps compared to human experts.

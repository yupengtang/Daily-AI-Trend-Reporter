### 1. Drivel-ology: Challenging LLMs with Interpreting Nonsense with Depth

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.03867)

LLMs struggle with understanding the nuanced, context-dependent meanings of Drivelological text, which appears nonsensical but contains deeper semantic layers.

### 2. From Editor to Dense Geometry Estimator

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.04338)

FE2E, a framework using a Diffusion Transformer for dense geometry prediction, outperforms generative models in zero-shot monocular depth and normal estimation with improved performance and efficiency.

### 3. Towards a Unified View of Large Language Model Post-Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.04419)

A unified policy gradient estimator and Hybrid Post-Training algorithm effectively combine online and offline data for post-training language models, improving performance across various benchmarks.

### 4. Inverse IFEval: Can LLMs Unlearn Stubborn Training Conventions to Follow
  Real Instructions?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.04292)

Inverse IFEval evaluates Large Language Models' ability to override training biases and follow unconventional instructions, highlighting the need for adaptability in diverse contexts.

### 5. DeepResearch Arena: The First Exam of LLMs' Research Abilities via
  Seminar-Grounded Tasks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.01396)

DeepResearch Arena, a benchmark using academic seminar transcripts, provides high-quality research tasks to evaluate deep research agents across multiple disciplines.

### 6. Transition Models: Rethinking the Generative Learning Objective

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.04394)

A novel generative paradigm, Transition Models (TiM), addresses the trade-off between computational cost and output quality in generative modeling by using a continuous-time dynamics equation.

### 7. NER Retriever: Zero-Shot Named Entity Retrieval with Type-Aware
  Embeddings

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.04011)

NER Retriever uses internal representations from large language models to perform zero-shot named entity retrieval by embedding entity mentions and type descriptions into a shared semantic space, outperforming lexical and dense sentence-level retrieval methods.

### 8. Loong: Synthesize Long Chain-of-Thoughts at Scale through Verifiers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.03059)

The Loong Project introduces a framework for generating and verifying synthetic data to improve reasoning capabilities in Large Language Models through Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Reward.

### 9. Video-MTR: Reinforced Multi-Turn Reasoning for Long Video Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20478)

Video-MTR, a reinforced multi-turn reasoning framework, improves long-form video understanding by iteratively selecting key segments and comprehending questions, outperforming existing methods in accuracy and efficiency.

### 10. Few-step Flow for 3D Generation via Marginal-Data Transport Distillation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.04406)

A novel framework, MDT-dist, accelerates 3D flow generation by distilling pretrained models to learn Marginal-Data Transport through Velocity Matching and Velocity Distillation, reducing sampling steps and improving speed and fidelity.

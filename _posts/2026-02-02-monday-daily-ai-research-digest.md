### 2. Golden Goose: A Simple Trick to Synthesize Unlimited RLVR Tasks from Unverifiable Internet Text

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.22975)

Golden Goose synthesizes unlimited RLVR tasks from unverifiable internet text by creating multiple-choice question-answering versions of fill-in-the-middle tasks, enabling large-scale training and achieving state-of-the-art results in cybersecurity and other domains.

### 5. THINKSAFE: Self-Generated Safety Alignment for Reasoning Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.23143)

ThinkSafe is a self-aligned framework that enhances safety in large reasoning models through lightweight refusal steering and fine-tuning on self-generated responses, maintaining reasoning performance while reducing computational costs.

### 6. ReGuLaR: Variational Latent Reasoning Guided by Rendered Chain-of-Thought

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.23184)

### 7. TTCS: Test-Time Curriculum Synthesis for Self-Evolving

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.22628)

TTCS is a co-evolving test-time training framework that enhances LLM reasoning abilities by iteratively generating challenging question variants and updating a reasoning solver through self-consistency rewards.

### 8. Causal World Modeling for Robot Control

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.21998)

### 9. Do Reasoning Models Enhance Embedding Models?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.21192)

Embedding models initialized from RLVR-tuned reasoning models show no performance advantage over base models, with HRSA revealing preserved global geometry and linear readout despite local geometric reorganization.

### 10. MemOCR: Layout-Aware Visual Memory for Efficient Long-Horizon Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.21468)

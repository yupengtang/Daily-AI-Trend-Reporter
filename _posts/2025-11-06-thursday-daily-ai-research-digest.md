### 1. Diffusion Language Models are Super Data Learners

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.03276)

Diffusion language models outperform autoregressive models in low-data settings due to any-order modeling, iterative bidirectional denoising, and Monte Carlo augmentation, and maintain advantages even at scale.

### 2. UniAVGen: Unified Audio and Video Generation with Asymmetric Cross-Modal
  Interactions

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.03334)

UniAVGen, a unified framework using dual Diffusion Transformers and Asymmetric Cross-Modal Interaction, enhances audio-video generation by ensuring synchronization and consistency with fewer training samples.

### 3. LEGO-Eval: Towards Fine-Grained Evaluation on Synthesizing 3D Embodied
  Environments with Tool Augmentation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.03001)

LEGO-Eval and LEGO-Bench improve the evaluation and generation of realistic 3D scenes by aligning detailed instructions with scene components, outperforming existing methods.

### 4. CostBench: Evaluating Multi-Turn Cost-Optimal Planning and Adaptation in
  Dynamic Environments for LLM Tool-Use Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.02734)

CostBench evaluates Large Language Model agents' cost-aware planning and adaptability in response to dynamic changes, revealing significant gaps in current models' performance.

### 5. TabTune: A Unified Library for Inference and Fine-Tuning Tabular
  Foundation Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.02802)

TabTune is a unified library that standardizes the workflow for tabular foundation models, supporting various adaptation strategies and evaluation metrics.

### 6. Orion-MSP: Multi-Scale Sparse Attention for Tabular In-Context Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.02818)

Orion-MSP, a tabular in-context learning architecture, addresses limitations in current models by incorporating multi-scale processing, block-sparse attention, and a Perceiver-style memory, achieving state-of-the-art performance on diverse benchmarks.

### 7. Kinematify: Open-Vocabulary Synthesis of High-DoF Articulated Objects

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.01294)

Kinematify is an automated framework that synthesizes articulated objects from RGB images or textual descriptions, addressing challenges in inferring kinematic topologies and estimating joint parameters.

### 8. LiveTradeBench: Seeking Real-World Alpha with Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.03628)

LiveTradeBench evaluates LLMs in dynamic trading environments to assess decision-making under real-time uncertainty and market volatility.

### 9. MME-CC: A Challenging Multi-Modal Evaluation Benchmark of Cognitive
  Capacity

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.03146)

MME-CC is a vision-grounded benchmark that evaluates multimodal large language models' cognitive capacity across spatial, geometric, and knowledge-based reasoning tasks, revealing weaknesses in spatial and geometric reasoning.

### 10. Jr. AI Scientist and Its Risk Report: Autonomous Scientific Exploration
  from a Baseline Paper

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.04583)

Jr. AI Scientist, an autonomous AI system, mimics novice researcher workflows to generate scientifically valuable papers, outperforming fully automated systems but with identified limitations and risks.

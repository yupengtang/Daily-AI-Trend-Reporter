### 1. Latent Collaboration in Multi-Agent Systems

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.20639)

LatentMAS enables efficient, lossless collaboration among LLM agents in latent space, improving performance and reducing computational costs compared to text-based methods.

### 2. Multimodal Evaluation of Russian-language Architectures

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.15552)

Mera Multi is an open multimodal evaluation framework for Russian-spoken architectures, addressing the lack of such benchmarks with 18 newly constructed tasks and a methodology to prevent benchmark leakage.

### 3. Inferix: A Block-Diffusion based Next-Generation Inference Engine for World Simulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.20714)

Inferix is a next-generation inference engine designed for immersive world synthesis using semi-autoregressive decoding, combining diffusion and autoregressive methods for high-quality, real-time video generation and interaction.

### 4. NVIDIA Nemotron Parse 1.1

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.20478)

Nemotron-Parse-1.1 is a lightweight OCR and document parsing model with improved capabilities in general OCR, markdown formatting, structured table parsing, and text extraction from images, using an encoder-decoder architecture.

### 5. Harmony: Harmonizing Audio and Video Generation through Cross-Task Synergy

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.21579)

Harmony addresses audio-visual synchronization in generative AI by introducing a Cross-Task Synergy training paradigm, Global-Local Decoupled Interaction Module, and Synchronization-Enhanced CFG to improve alignment and fidelity.

### 6. UniGame: Turning a Unified Multimodal Model Into Its Own Adversary

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.19413)

UniGame, a self-adversarial post-training framework, improves consistency, understanding, generation, and robustness in unified multimodal models by introducing a lightweight perturber at the shared token interface.

### 7. Monet: Reasoning in Latent Visual Space Beyond Images and Language

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.21395)

Monet, a training framework, enables MLLMs to reason in latent visual space using continuous embeddings, addressing challenges like computational cost and supervision, and outperforms on visual reasoning tasks.

### 8. Revisiting Generalization Across Difficulty Levels: It's Not So Easy

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.21692)

LLMs do not consistently generalize across different task difficulties, indicating the need for a broad range of difficulty levels in both training and evaluation datasets.

### 9. Terminal Velocity Matching

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.19797)

Terminal Velocity Matching (TVM) generalizes flow matching for high-fidelity generative modeling, achieving state-of-the-art performance on ImageNet with minimal computational steps.

### 10. Block Cascading: Training Free Acceleration of Block-Causal Video Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.20426)

Block Cascading parallelizes video block generation, achieving significant speed improvements without compromising quality.

### 8. QuitoBench: A High-Quality Open Time Series Forecasting Benchmark

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.26017)

QuitoBench addresses the lack of large-scale time series benchmarks by introducing a regime-balanced dataset with eight TSF regimes, revealing that foundation models outperform deep learning at long contexts while scaling data provides greater benefits than scaling model size.

### 9. HippoCamp: Benchmarking Contextual Agents on Personal Computers

### 10. GaussianGPT: Towards Autoregressive 3D Gaussian Scene Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.26661)

GaussianGPT uses a transformer-based autoregressive approach with 3D rotary positional embeddings to generate 3D scenes by predicting Gaussian primitives, offering advantages over diffusion methods in terms of controllability and flexibility.

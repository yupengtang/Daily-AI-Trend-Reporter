### 1. Beyond 'Aha!': Toward Systematic Meta-Abilities Alignment in Large
  Reasoning Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10554)

Explicit alignment of large reasoning models with deduction, induction, and abduction through a three-stage pipeline improves scalability and reliability in reasoning tasks.

### 2. Parallel Scaling Law for Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10475)

Parallel scaling (ParScale) improves inference efficiency by reusing existing parameters and executing multiple transformations in parallel, offering superior performance with reduced memory and latency compared to parameter scaling.

### 3. System Prompt Optimization with Meta-Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09666)

A meta-learning framework for optimizing system prompts in Large Language Models (LLMs) improves generalization across diverse tasks and datasets.

### 4. OpenThinkIMG: Learning to Think with Images via Visual Tool
  Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.08617)

OpenThinkIMG and V-ToolRL enable LVLMs to learn adaptive policies for using external vision tools, outperforming existing methods on chart reasoning tasks.

### 5. WorldPM: Scaling Human Preference Modeling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10527)

World Preference Modeling (WorldPM) enhances preference fine-tuning through large-scale preference data, showing scalability benefits in adversarial and objective metrics, and improving generalization across various human preference datasets.

### 6. The CoT Encyclopedia: Analyzing, Predicting, and Controlling how a
  Reasoning Model will Think

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10185)

The CoT Encyclopedia is a bottom-up framework that automatically analyzes and categorizes reasoning strategies in long chain-of-thoughts generated by large language models, providing more interpretable analyses and enabling performance improvements.

### 7. J1: Incentivizing Thinking in LLM-as-a-Judge via Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10320)

A reinforcement learning approach called J1 improves the judgment ability of LLM-as-a-Judge models through verifiable rewards and chain-of-thought reasoning.

### 8. EnerVerse-AC: Envisioning Embodied Environments with Action Condition

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09723)

EnerVerse-AC, an action-conditional world model, enables realistic robotic inference and testing by simulating future actions and observations, thereby reducing costs and improving generalization in dynamic settings.

### 9. End-to-End Vision Tokenizer Tuning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10562)

ETT is an end-to-end vision tokenizer tuning method that integrates visual tokenizer training with autoregressive tasks, significantly improving performance in multimodal understanding and visual generation.

### 10. EWMBench: Evaluating Scene, Motion, and Semantic Quality in Embodied
  World Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09694)

A new benchmark framework evaluates text-to-video diffusion models in embodied AI for visual, motion, and semantic consistency, using a diverse dataset and evaluation toolkit.

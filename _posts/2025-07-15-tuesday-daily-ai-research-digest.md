### 1. Reasoning or Memorization? Unreliable Results of Reinforcement Learning
  Due to Data Contamination

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.10532)

Research on enhancing reasoning capabilities in large language models using reinforcement learning reveals that accurate reward signals are crucial for performance improvement, and current benchmarks may be unreliable due to data contamination.

### 2. Mixture-of-Recursions: Learning Dynamic Recursive Depths for Adaptive
  Token-Level Computation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.10524)

Mixture-of-Recursions (MoR) combines parameter sharing and adaptive computation in a Recursive Transformer to improve efficiency, reduce memory usage, and enhance performance across different model scales.

### 3. SpeakerVid-5M: A Large-Scale High-Quality Dataset for Audio-Visual
  Dyadic Interactive Human Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.09862)

SpeakerVid-5M is a large-scale, high-quality dataset for audio-visual dyadic interactive virtual human generation, featuring diverse interaction types and data quality levels, along with an autoregressive video chat baseline and benchmark metrics.

### 4. EmbRACE-3K: Embodied Reasoning and Action in Complex Environments

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.10548)

EmRACE-3K, a dataset of language-guided tasks in diverse environments, evaluates and improves embodied reasoning capabilities of vision-language models through zero-shot and fine-tuned performance.

### 5. REST: Stress Testing Large Reasoning Models by Asking Multiple Problems
  at Once

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.10541)

REST, a stress-testing framework, evaluates large reasoning models under simultaneous multi-problem conditions, revealing performance differences and insights into model behavior under real-world reasoning demands.

### 6. MoVieS: Motion-Aware 4D Dynamic View Synthesis in One Second

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.10065)

MoVieS synthesizes 4D dynamic novel views from monocular videos using pixel-aligned Gaussian primitives, enabling unified appearance, geometry, and motion modeling within a single framework.

### 7. LayerCake: Token-Aware Contrastive Decoding within Large Language Model
  Layers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.04404)

A token-aware, layer-localized contrastive decoding method improves factual generation in large language models by selectively suppressing attention to specific token types at their respective depths.

### 8. CompassJudger-2: Towards Generalist Judge Model via Verifiable Rewards

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.09104)

CompassJudger-2, a generalist judge model, improves cross-domain evaluation accuracy and robustness through task-driven data curation and a refined learning objective.

### 9. From KMMLU-Redux to KMMLU-Pro: A Professional Korean Benchmark Suite for
  LLM Evaluation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.08924)

Two Korean expert-level benchmarks, KMMLU-Redux and KMMLU-Pro, are introduced to evaluate Large Language Models in industrial and professional contexts.

### 10. Subject-Consistent and Pose-Diverse Text-to-Image Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.08396)

CoDi, a two-stage text-to-image framework, maintains subject consistency while enhancing pose diversity through identity transport and refinement using diffusion.

### 1. Qwen3-Omni Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.17765)

Qwen3-Omni, a multimodal model, achieves state-of-the-art performance across text, image, audio, and video, using a Thinker-Talker MoE architecture and a lightweight causal ConvNet for efficient streaming synthesis.

### 2. LIMI: Less is More for Agency

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.17567)

LIMI demonstrates that sophisticated agentic intelligence can emerge from minimal, strategically curated demonstrations, outperforming data-intensive models on agency benchmarks.

### 3. OmniInsert: Mask-Free Video Insertion of Any Reference via Diffusion
  Transformer Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.17627)

OmniInsert addresses challenges in mask-free video insertion using a novel data pipeline, feature injection, progressive training, and context-aware rephrasing, outperforming commercial solutions.

### 4. ARE: Scaling Up Agent Environments and Evaluations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.17158)

Meta Agents Research Environments (ARE) facilitate the creation and execution of complex environments for agent research, and Gaia2, a benchmark built on ARE, evaluates general agent capabilities in dynamic, asynchronous settings.

### 5. OnePiece: Bringing Context Engineering and Reasoning to Industrial
  Cascade Ranking System

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18091)

OnePiece integrates LLM-style context engineering and reasoning into industrial search and recommendation systems, achieving significant improvements in key business metrics.

### 6. TempSamp-R1: Effective Temporal Sampling with Reinforcement Fine-Tuning
  for Video LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18056)

TempSamp-R1, a reinforcement fine-tuning framework, enhances multimodal large language models for video temporal grounding by using off-policy supervision and a hybrid Chain-of-Thought training paradigm, achieving state-of-the-art performance on benchmark datasets.

### 7. VideoFrom3D: 3D Scene Video Generation via Complementary Image and Video
  Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.17985)

VideoFrom3D synthesizes high-quality 3D scene videos using a combination of image and video diffusion models, achieving style consistency without requiring paired datasets.

### 8. DiffusionNFT: Online Diffusion Reinforcement with Forward Process

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.16117)

Diffusion Negative-aware FineTuning (DiffusionNFT) optimizes diffusion models directly on the forward process via flow matching, improving efficiency and performance compared to existing methods.

### 9. SWE-Bench Pro: Can AI Agents Solve Long-Horizon Software Engineering
  Tasks?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.16941)

SWE-Bench Pro is a challenging benchmark for coding models, featuring complex, enterprise-level problems that require substantial code modifications, with performance evaluations showing significant limitations in current models.

### 10. EpiCache: Episodic KV Cache Management for Long Conversational Question
  Answering

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.17396)

EpiCache is a KV cache management framework for long conversational question answering that reduces memory usage and improves accuracy through block-wise prefill, episodic KV compression, and adaptive layer-wise budget allocation.

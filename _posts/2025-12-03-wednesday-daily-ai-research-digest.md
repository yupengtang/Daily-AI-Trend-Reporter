### 1. DeepSeek-V3.2: Pushing the Frontier of Open Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.02556)

DeepSeek-V3.2 introduces DeepSeek Sparse Attention and a scalable reinforcement learning framework, achieving superior reasoning and performance compared to GPT-5 and Gemini-3.0-Pro in complex reasoning tasks.

### 2. ToolOrchestra: Elevating Intelligence via Efficient Model and Tool Orchestration

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.21689)

A small orchestrator using ToolOrchestra method coordinates various intelligent tools with reinforcement learning, achieving higher accuracy and efficiency in solving complex tasks like Humanity's Last Exam compared to larger models.

### 3. Deep Research: A Systematic Survey

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.02038)

Deep Research systems integrate LLMs with external tools to enhance problem-solving capabilities, involving query planning, information acquisition, memory management, and answer generation.

### 4. MultiShotMaster: A Controllable Multi-Shot Video Generation Framework

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.03041)

MultiShotMaster extends a single-shot model with novel RoPE variants for flexible and controllable multi-shot video generation, addressing data scarcity with an automated annotation pipeline.

### 5. Guided Self-Evolving LLMs with Minimal Human Supervision

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.02472)

R-Few, a guided Self-Play Challenger-Solver framework, enables stable and controllable model self-evolution with minimal human supervision, achieving performance improvements on math and reasoning benchmarks.

### 6. Skywork-R1V4: Toward Agentic Multimodal Intelligence through Interleaved Thinking with Images and DeepResearch

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.02395)

Skywork-R1V4, a 30B parameter multimodal agentic model, achieves top performance in perception and multimodal search benchmarks through supervised fine-tuning and interleaved reasoning without reinforcement learning.

### 7. MG-Nav: Dual-Scale Visual Navigation via Sparse Spatial Memory

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.22609)

MG-Nav, a dual-scale framework for zero-shot visual navigation, combines global memory-guided planning with local geometry-enhanced control using a Sparse Spatial Memory Graph and a VGGT-adapter for robust navigation in unseen environments.

### 8. DualCamCtrl: Dual-Branch Diffusion Model for Geometry-Aware Camera-Controlled Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.23127)

DualCamCtrl is a diffusion model for camera-controlled video generation that uses a dual-branch framework and Semantic Guided Mutual Alignment to improve consistency and disentangle appearance and geometry modeling.

### 9. SimScale: Learning to Drive via Real-World Simulation at Scale

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.23369)

A simulation framework improves autonomous driving by generating diverse, high-fidelity driving scenarios, leading to better generalization and robustness in real-world testing.

### 10. InnoGym: Benchmarking the Innovation Potential of AI Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.01822)

InnoGym is a benchmark and framework that evaluates the innovation potential of AI agents using performance gain and novelty metrics, highlighting a gap between creativity and effectiveness.

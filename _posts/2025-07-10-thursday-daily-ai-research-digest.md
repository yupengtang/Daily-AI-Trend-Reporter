### 1. 4KAgent: Agentic Any Image to 4K Super-Resolution

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07105)

4KAgent, a unified agentic super-resolution system, enhances low-resolution images to 4K using a profiling module, perception agent, and restoration agent, achieving state-of-the-art performance across various imaging domains.

### 2. MIRIX: Multi-Agent Memory System for LLM-Based Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07957)

MIRIX, a modular multi-agent memory system, enhances language models' memory capabilities by integrating diverse memory types and a dynamic framework, achieving superior performance in multimodal and long-form conversation benchmarks.

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.06167)

Skywork-R1V3, an open-source vision-language model, enhances visual reasoning through a post-training reinforcement learning framework, achieving state-of-the-art performance on multimodal reasoning tasks.

### 5. Perception-Aware Policy Optimization for Multimodal Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.06448)

PAPO, an extension of GRPO, enhances multimodal reasoning by integrating perception-aware supervision, leading to improved performance and reduced perception errors in tasks with high vision dependency.

### 8. A Systematic Analysis of Hybrid Linear Attention

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.06457)

Research evaluates various linear attention models in hybrid architectures, finding that selective gating, hierarchical recurrence, and controlled forgetting are crucial for effective recall in Transformers.

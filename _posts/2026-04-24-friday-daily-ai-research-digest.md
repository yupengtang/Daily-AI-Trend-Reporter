### 1. LLaTiSA: Towards Difficulty-Stratified Time Series Reasoning from Visual Perception to Semantics

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.17295)

A hierarchical time series reasoning dataset and model are introduced to improve LLM understanding of temporal data through visualized patterns and numerical tables.

### 2. WorldMark: A Unified Benchmark Suite for Interactive Video World Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21686)

WorldMark establishes a standardized benchmark for evaluating interactive video generation models with unified controls, identical scenarios, and comprehensive evaluation metrics across multiple model architectures.

### 3. UniT: Toward a Unified Physical Language for Human-to-Humanoid Policy Learning and World Modeling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.19734)

UniT enables human-to-humanoid transfer by creating a unified visual-language representation that bridges kinematic differences through cross-reconstruction mechanisms and shared latent spaces.

### 4. StyleID: A Perception-Aware Dataset and Metric for Stylization-Agnostic Facial Identity Recognition

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21689)

StyleID presents a human perception-aware dataset and evaluation framework for facial identity preservation under stylization, featuring two datasets derived from psychometric experiments and calibrated semantic encoders that improve correlation with human judgments.

### 5. Co-Evolving LLM Decision and Skill Bank Agents for Long-Horizon Tasks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20987)

A co-evolution framework enables large language models to discover, retain, and reuse structured skills across episodes in long-horizon interactive environments through a learnable skill bank and skill pipeline.

### 6. VLAA-GUI: Knowing When to Stop, Recover, and Search, A Modular Framework for GUI Automation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21375)

VLAA-GUI is a modular GUI agent framework that addresses early stopping and repetitive loop issues through integrated components for verification, loop breaking, and search capabilities.

### 7. Seeing Fast and Slow: Learning the Flow of Time in Videos

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21931)

Video speed manipulation and perception models are developed through self-supervised temporal reasoning, enabling speed detection, slow-motion video generation, and temporal super-resolution from in-the-wild sources.

### 8. Context Unrolling in Omni Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21921)

Omni is a unified multimodal model trained on diverse data types that enables context unrolling for improved reasoning across heterogeneous modalities.

### 9. Hybrid Policy Distillation for LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20244)

Hybrid Policy Distillation combines forward and reverse KL divergence approaches to improve knowledge distillation stability and efficiency across different model sizes and tasks.

### 10. TingIS: Real-time Risk Event Discovery from Noisy Customer Incidents at Enterprise Scale

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.21889)

TingIS is an enterprise-grade incident discovery system that uses multi-stage event linking with LLMs, cascaded routing, and noise reduction to efficiently identify critical issues from high-volume, noisy customer reports.

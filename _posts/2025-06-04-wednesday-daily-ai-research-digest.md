### 1. Reflect, Retry, Reward: Self-Improving LLMs via Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24726)

A method using self-reflection and reinforcement learning improves the performance of large language models, especially with limited feedback, by rewarding self-reflections that lead to better task performance.

### 2. UniWorld: High-Resolution Semantic Encoders for Unified Visual
  Understanding and Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.03147)

A unified generative framework called UniWorld uses semantic features from visual-language models for image perception and manipulation, outperforming BAGEL with reduced data.

### 3. VS-Bench: Evaluating VLMs for Strategic Reasoning and Decision-Making in
  Multi-Agent Environments

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.02387)

VS-Bench is a multimodal benchmark designed to evaluate Vision Language Models' strategic reasoning and decision-making in complex multi-agent environments.

### 4. GUI-Actor: Coordinate-Free Visual Grounding for GUI Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.03143)

GUI-Actor, a VLM-based method using attention for coordinate-free GUI grounding, outperforms existing methods with better generalization and efficient fine-tuning.

### 5. SynthRL: Scaling Visual Reasoning with Verifiable Data Synthesis

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.02096)

SynthRL, a scalable pipeline for data synthesis in reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards, enhances visual math reasoning VLMs by generating challenging, verifiable questions.

### 6. CSVQA: A Chinese Multimodal Benchmark for Evaluating STEM Reasoning
  Capabilities of VLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24120)

A new benchmark, CSVQA, evaluates scientific reasoning in vision-language models through domain-specific visual question answering, highlighting the need for improvement in these models.

### 7. OmniSpatial: Towards Comprehensive Spatial Reasoning Benchmark for
  Vision Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.03135)

A comprehensive benchmark called OmniSpatial evaluates vision-language models' understanding of advanced spatial reasoning tasks, revealing significant limitations across various models.

### 8. FinMME: Benchmark Dataset for Financial Multi-Modal Reasoning Evaluation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24714)

FinMME is a comprehensive multimodal dataset for financial research and FinScore is an evaluation system that highlights the challenges faced by even advanced models like GPT-4o in the finance domain.

### 9. OThink-R1: Intrinsic Fast/Slow Thinking Mode Switching for
  Over-Reasoning Mitigation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.02397)

OThink-R1 is introduced to reduce reasoning redundancy in complex problem-solving by classifying reasoning steps as essential or redundant and dynamically switching thinking modes based on task complexity.

### 10. Visual Embodied Brain: Let Multimodal Large Language Models See, Think,
  and Control in Spaces

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.00123)

VeBrain is a unified framework that integrates multimodal understanding, visual-spatial reasoning, and physical interaction for legged robots, demonstrating superior performance compared to existing methods across various benchmarks.

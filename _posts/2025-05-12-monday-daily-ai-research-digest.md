### 1. Bielik v3 Small: Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02550)

Bielik v3, a series of parameter-efficient generative text models, achieves high performance in Polish language processing with a custom tokenizer, Weighted Instruction Cross-Entropy Loss, and Adaptive Learning Rate.

### 2. Bielik 11B v2 Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02410)

Bielik 11B v2, a scaled language model with 11B parameters, excels on Polish benchmarks through Weighted Instruction Cross-Entropy Loss and Adaptive Learning Rate, outperforming larger models and demonstrating resource-efficient deployment.

### 3. UniVLA: Learning to Act Anywhere with Task-centric Latent Actions

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.06111)

UniVLA is a framework for learning cross-embodiment VLA policies using latent action models derived from internet-scale videos, achieving superior performance with reduced pretraining compute and downstream data.

### 4. G-FOCUS: Towards a Robust Method for Assessing UI Design Persuasiveness

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05026)

G-FOCUS, a novel inference-time reasoning strategy, enhances Vision-Language Models for assessing UI persuasiveness, complementing A/B testing.

### 5. Sailing AI by the Stars: A Survey of Learning from Rewards in
  Post-Training and Test-Time Scaling of Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02686)

A survey of the Learning from Rewards paradigm in Large Language Models, encompassing reinforcement learning techniques and reward-guided decoding strategies, enabling dynamic feedback and aligned preferences.

### 6. Healthy LLMs? Benchmarking LLM Knowledge of UK Government Public Health
  Information

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.06046)

A new benchmark, PubHealthBench, evaluates LLMs' knowledge of UK Government public health information, finding high accuracy in MCQA but lower performance in free form responses.

### 7. A Preliminary Study for GPT-4o on Image Restoration

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05621)

GPT-4o, while generating visually appealing images, often lacks structural fidelity and serves as a visual prior to enhance dehazing, derainning, and low-light enhancement tasks in image restoration.

### 8. Multiview Point Cloud Registration via Optimization in an Autoencoder
  Latent Space

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.21467)

POLAR, a method for multiview point cloud registration, excels in handling large numbers of views, significant degradations, and large initial angles by leveraging a latent space and specialized loss function.

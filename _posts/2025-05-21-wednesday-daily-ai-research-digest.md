### 1. Emerging Properties in Unified Multimodal Pretraining

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14683)

BAGEL, an open-source foundational model trained on diverse multimodal data, significantly outperforms existing models in both generation and understanding tasks.

### 2. SageAttention3: Microscaling FP4 Attention for Inference and An
  Exploration of 8-Bit Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.11594)

Efficiency enhancements for attention mechanisms, including leveraging FP4 Tensor Cores and developing an 8-bit attention method, improve inference and training performance.

### 3. Reward Reasoning Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14674)

RRMs, employing chain-of-thought reasoning and reinforcement learning, enhance reward model performance by adaptively utilizing test-time compute.

### 4. Optimizing Anytime Reasoning via Budget Relative Policy Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.13438)

The AnytimeReasoner framework optimizes token efficiency and reasoning flexibility for large language models by introducing verifiable dense rewards and a decoupled policy optimization technique.

### 5. Neurosymbolic Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.13138)

Neurosymbolic diffusion models address limitations of standard neurosymbolic predictors by modeling dependencies between symbols using discrete diffusion, leading to improved accuracy and calibration.

### 6. VisualQuality-R1: Reasoning-Induced Image Quality Assessment via
  Reinforcement Learning to Rank

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14460)

VisualQuality-R1, a reasoning-induced no-reference IQA model trained via reinforcement learning, outperforms discriminative models in visual quality assessment by generating human-aligned quality descriptions and supporting multi-dataset training.

### 7. Visual Agentic Reinforcement Fine-Tuning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14246)

Visual Agentic Reinforcement Fine-Tuning enhances Large Vision-Language Models for flexible image manipulation and web-based reasoning, outperforming existing models on multi-modal benchmarks.

### 8. Latent Flow Transformer

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14513)

The Latent Flow Transformer (LFT) compresses layers by replacing them with learned transport operators using flow matching and Flow Walking, showing improved performance over layer skipping and reducing the gap between autoregressive and flow-based models.

### 9. The Aloe Family Recipe for Open and Specialized Healthcare LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04388)

Aloe Beta models improve open-source medical Large Language Models through enhanced preprocessing, Direct Preference Optimization, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, and a rigorous evaluation methodology, achieving competitive performance and high safety standards.

### 10. General-Reasoner: Advancing LLM Reasoning Across All Domains

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14652)

General-Reasoner enhances large language model reasoning across diverse domains by using a large-scale dataset and generative model-based answer verification, outperforming existing methods.

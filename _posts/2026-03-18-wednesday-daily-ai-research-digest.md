### 1. Demystifing Video Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16870)

Diffusion-based video models demonstrate reasoning capabilities through denoising steps rather than frame sequences, exhibiting behaviors like working memory, self-correction, and perception-before-action within specialized transformer layers.

### 2. InCoder-32B: Code Foundation Model for Industrial Scenarios

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16790)

InCoder-32B is a 32-billion-parameter code model trained on industrial datasets with extended context length and execution verification to improve performance in hardware-aware programming tasks.

### 3. SocialOmni: Benchmarking Audio-Visual Social Interactivity in Omni Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16859)

SocialOmni presents a benchmark for evaluating social interactivity in omni-modal large language models across speaker identification, interruption timing, and natural interruption generation, revealing gaps between perceptual accuracy and conversational competence.

### 4. MiroThinker-1.7 & H1: Towards Heavy-Duty Research Agents via Verification

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.15726)

MiroThinker-1.7 and MiroThinker-H1 are research agents that enhance complex reasoning through structured planning, contextual reasoning, and tool interaction, with MiroThinker-H1 incorporating verification at local and global levels for more reliable multi-step problem solving.

### 5. Qianfan-OCR: A Unified End-to-End Model for Document Intelligence

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.13398)

Qianfan-OCR is a 4B-parameter vision-language model that unifies document parsing, layout analysis, and understanding while maintaining strong performance across multiple OCR benchmarks through its Layout-as-Thought mechanism.

### 6. Thinking in Uncertainty: Mitigating Hallucinations in MLRMs with Latent Entropy-Aware Decoding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.13366)

Latent Entropy-Aware Decoding (LEAD) enhances multimodal large reasoning models by using entropy-aware reasoning mode switching and prior-guided visual anchor injection to reduce hallucinations.

### 7. Kinema4D: Kinematic 4D World Modeling for Spatiotemporal Embodied Simulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16669)

Kinema4D introduces a 4D generative robotic simulator that models robot-world interactions through precise kinematic control and spatiotemporal environmental reaction synthesis, enabling physically plausible and embodiment-agnostic simulations with zero-shot transfer capability.

### 8. WorldCam: Interactive Autoregressive 3D Gaming Worlds with Camera Pose as a Unifying Geometric Representation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16871)

Video diffusion transformers enhanced with camera pose representation enable precise action control and long-term 3D consistency in interactive gaming environments through physics-based action spaces and geometric grounding.

### 9. Online Experiential Learning for Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16856)

Online Experiential Learning enables continuous improvement of language models through deployment experience by extracting and consolidating experiential knowledge via on-policy distillation.

### 10. TRUST-SQL: Tool-Integrated Multi-Turn Reinforcement Learning for Text-to-SQL over Unknown Schemas

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16448)

TRUST-SQL addresses unknown schema text-to-SQL parsing through a four-phase protocol and dual-track GRPO strategy that improves credit assignment and outperforms baselines without pre-loaded metadata.

### 1. Mutarjim: Advancing Bidirectional Arabic-English Translation with a
  Small Language Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.17894)

Mutarjim is a compact Arabic-English translation model that outperforms larger models on established benchmarks and achieves state-of-the-art performance on a new comprehensive Tarjama-25 benchmark.

### 2. Shifting AI Efficiency From Model-Centric to Data-Centric Compression

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.19147)

The focus in AI research is shifting from model-centric to data-centric compression, with token compression identified as key to improving efficiency in handling long-context scenarios.

### 3. Alchemist: Turning Public Text-to-Image Data into Generative Gold

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.19297)

A new method using a pre-trained generative model helps construct a high-impact SFT dataset, Alchemist, which improves the generative quality of text-to-image models while maintaining diversity.

### 4. BizFinBench: A Business-Driven Real-World Financial Benchmark for
  Evaluating LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.19457)

BizFinBench is a benchmark for evaluating large language models in financial applications, revealing distinct performance patterns across various tasks.

### 5. Embodied Agents Meet Personalization: Exploring Memory Utilization for
  Personalized Assistance

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.16348)

MEMENTO evaluates personalized memory utilization in embodied agents, revealing limitations in understanding user semantics and routines.

### 6. Enigmata: Scaling Logical Reasoning in Large Language Models with
  Synthetic Verifiable Puzzles

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.19914)

Enigmata is a comprehensive suite for improving LLMs in puzzle reasoning through scalable multi-task RL training, leading to better performance on benchmarks and advanced math tasks.

### 7. PATS: Process-Level Adaptive Thinking Mode Switching

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.19250)

PATS enhances LLM efficiency by dynamically adjusting reasoning strategies based on task difficulty, leveraging PRMs and Beam Search.

### 8. ARM: Adaptive Reasoning Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.20258)

Adaptive Reasoning Model (ARM) uses Ada-GRPO to reduce token usage and improve efficiency across different reasoning modes.

### 9. Deciphering Trajectory-Aided LLM Reasoning: An Optimization Perspective

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.19815)

LLM reasoning is understood through a meta-learning framework, treating reasoning as pseudo-gradient descent and questions as individual tasks, which enhances generalization and provides practical insights for improvement.

### 10. Learning to Reason without External Rewards

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.19590)

Intuitor, a Reinforcement Learning from Internal Feedback method, uses self-certainty as a reward signal to enable unsupervised learning of large language models, achieving performance comparable to GRPO on benchmarks and superior generalization.

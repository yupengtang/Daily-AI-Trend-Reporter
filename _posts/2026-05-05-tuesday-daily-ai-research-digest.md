### 1. MolmoAct2: Action Reasoning Models for Real-world Deployment

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.02881)

MolmoAct2 presents an open-action reasoning model for robotics that improves upon previous systems through specialized vision-language-model backbones, new datasets, open-weight action tokenizers, architectural redesign for continuous-action prediction, and adaptive reasoning for reduced latency.

### 2. From Context to Skills: Can Language Models Learn from Context Skillfully?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.27660)

A self-evolving framework autonomously discovers and refines context-specific skills for language models through a multi-agent self-play loop with Challenger, Reasoner, and Judge components, improving context learning performance without human supervision.

### 3. Hallucinations Undermine Trust; Metacognition is a Way Forward

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.01428)

Generative AI models continue to produce factual errors despite improvements in reliability, with the key challenge being distinguishing known from unknown information rather than simply expanding knowledge boundaries.

### 4. Persistent Visual Memory: Sustaining Perception for Deep Generation in LVLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.00814)

Large vision-language models suffer from visual signal dilution during long sequence generation, which is mitigated by a lightweight persistent visual memory module that maintains visual attention and improves reasoning performance.

### 5. Repetition over Diversity: High-Signal Data Filtering for Sample-Efficient German Language Modeling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.28075)

Training high-quality filtered data multiple times yields better performance than single-pass training on larger, less filtered datasets for non-English language models.

### 6. AcademiClaw: When Students Set Challenges for AI Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.02661)

AcademiClaw presents a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating AI agents on complex academic tasks spanning multiple domains, revealing significant capability gaps in current models.

### 7. WindowsWorld: A Process-Centric Benchmark of Autonomous GUI Agents in Professional Cross-Application Environments

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.27776)

A cross-application workflow benchmark named WindowsWorld was developed to evaluate GUI agents on complex multi-step tasks requiring coordination across multiple software applications, revealing significant performance gaps in current agents when handling real-world professional workflows.

### 8. OceanPile: A Large-Scale Multimodal Ocean Corpus for Foundation Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.00877)

OceanPile presents a large-scale multimodal corpus for ocean science, combining diverse data types and a knowledge graph-guided instruction dataset to advance marine AI applications.

### 9. ComboStoc: Combinatorial Stochasticity for Diffusion Generative Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2405.13729)

Combining stochastic processes with diffusion models addresses combinatorial complexity limitations, accelerating training and enabling asynchronous generation across data modalities.

### 10. T^2PO: Uncertainty-Guided Exploration Control for Stable Multi-Turn Agentic Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.02178)

Token- and Turn-level Policy Optimization (T²PO) addresses multi-turn RL instability by controlling exploration at fine-grained levels through uncertainty monitoring and dynamic resampling.

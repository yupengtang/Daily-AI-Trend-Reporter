### 1. Tiny Model, Big Logic: Diversity-Driven Optimization Elicits Large-Model
  Reasoning Ability in VibeThinker-1.5B

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.06221)

VibeThinker-1.5B, a 1.5B-parameter model using the Spectrum-to-Signal Principle, achieves superior reasoning capabilities compared to larger models at a significantly lower cost.

### 2. Grounding Computer Use Agents on Human Demonstrations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.07332)

GroundCUA, a large-scale desktop grounding dataset, enables the development of GroundNext models that achieve state-of-the-art performance in mapping instructions to UI elements with less training data.

### 3. Adaptive Multi-Agent Response Refinement in Conversational Systems

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.08319)

A multi-agent framework enhances conversational quality by refining responses through agents responsible for factuality, personalization, and coherence, outperforming existing methods on challenging datasets.

### 4. KLASS: KL-Guided Fast Inference in Masked Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.05664)

KL-Adaptive Stability Sampling (KLASS) accelerates diffusion-based generation by identifying stable predictions, achieving significant speedups and quality improvements across various domains.

### 5. The Path Not Taken: RLVR Provably Learns Off the Principals

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.08567)

Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) improves large language models by updating a limited set of parameters, which is explained by a Three-Gate Theory, revealing distinct optimization dynamics compared to supervised fine-tuning.

### 6. Beyond English: Toward Inclusive and Scalable Multilingual Machine Translation with LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.07003)

LMT, a suite of large-scale multilingual translation models, addresses challenges in multilingual machine translation through strategic downsampling and parallel multilingual prompting, achieving state-of-the-art performance across 60 languages.

### 7. Wasm: A Pipeline for Constructing Structured Arabic Interleaved
  Multimodal Corpora

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.07080)

A pipeline for processing the Common Crawl dataset to create a new Arabic multimodal dataset that preserves document structure and supports both text-only and multimodal pre-training.

### 8. VideoSSR: Video Self-Supervised Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.06281)

A novel video self-supervised reinforcement learning framework, VideoSSR, enhances MLLM performance across various video understanding tasks by leveraging intrinsic video information.

### 9. Intelligence per Watt: Measuring Intelligence Efficiency of Local AI

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.07885)

Local inference using small language models on accelerators can accurately handle many real-world queries, significantly reducing demand on centralized cloud infrastructure, as measured by intelligence per watt.

### 10. Beyond Fact Retrieval: Episodic Memory for RAG with Generative Semantic Workspaces

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.07587)

The Generative Semantic Workspace (GSW) enhances LLMs' long-context reasoning by creating structured, interpretable representations of evolving situations, outperforming existing methods on the Episodic Memory Benchmark and reducing inference time.

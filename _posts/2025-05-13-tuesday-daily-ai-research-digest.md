### 1. Seed1.5-VL Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07062)

Seed1.5-VL, a vision-language foundation model combining a vision encoder and a large MoE LLM, achieves state-of-the-art performance across various benchmarks and excels in multimodal reasoning tasks such as visual puzzles.

### 2. MiMo: Unlocking the Reasoning Potential of Language Model -- From
  Pretraining to Posttraining

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07608)

MiMo-7B, a large language model optimized for reasoning tasks, enhances pre-training with data mixing and Multi-Token Prediction, and post-training with reinforcement learning on math and programming problems, achieving superior performance over larger models.

### 3. Step1X-3D: Towards High-Fidelity and Controllable Generation of Textured
  3D Assets

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07747)

Step1X-3D addresses challenges in 3D generation through a hybrid VAE-DiT geometry generator and diffusion-based texture synthesis, achieving state-of-the-art results with an open-source framework.

### 4. Learning from Peers in Reasoning Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07787)

LeaP, a peer interaction mechanism for large reasoning models, improves error correction and performance across various math benchmarks by enabling collaborative reasoning paths.

### 5. Unified Continuous Generative Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07447)

A unified framework for continuous generative models improves training and sampling performance across multi-step and few-step methods.

### 6. DanceGRPO: Unleashing GRPO on Visual Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07818)

DanceGRPO is a unified RL framework that enhances visual generation across different paradigms, tasks, models, and reward systems, outperforming baselines in benchmarks and improving video generation stability.

### 7. Skywork-VL Reward: An Effective Reward Model for Multimodal
  Understanding and Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07263)

Skywork-VL Reward is a multimodal reward model that uses a large-scale preference dataset and Qwen2.5-VL-7B-Instruct architecture to achieve state-of-the-art performance in multimodal reasoning.

### 8. REFINE-AF: A Task-Agnostic Framework to Align Language Models via
  Self-Generated Instructions using Reinforcement Learning from Automated
  Feedback

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.06548)

Semi-automated frameworks using open-source small LLMs and reinforcement learning significantly improve instruction dataset generation for LLM fine-tuning across various tasks.

### 9. AttentionInfluence: Adopting Attention Head Influence for Weak-to-Strong
  Pretraining Data Selection

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07293)

AttentionInfluence, a training-free, unsupervised method using attention head masking, improves data selection for pretraining LLMs, enhancing their reasoning abilities across various benchmarks.

### 10. Learning Dynamics in Continual Pre-Training for Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07796)

The study provides a scaling law for Continual Pre-Training (CPT) of large language models, characterizing the transition in performance curves and accounting for distribution shift and learning rate changes.

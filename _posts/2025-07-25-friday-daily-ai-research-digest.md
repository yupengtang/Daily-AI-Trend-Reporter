### 1. Group Sequence Policy Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.18071)

Group Sequence Policy Optimization (GSPO) is a reinforcement learning algorithm that improves training efficiency and performance of large language models by using sequence-level importance ratios and operations.

### 2. nablaNABLA: Neighborhood Adaptive Block-Level Attention

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.13546)

NABLA, a Neighborhood Adaptive Block-Level Attention mechanism, enhances video diffusion transformers by reducing computational overhead without significantly impacting generative quality or visual fidelity.

### 3. MUR: Momentum Uncertainty guided Reasoning for Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.14958)

Momentum Uncertainty-guided Reasoning (MUR) dynamically allocates computational resources to improve reasoning efficiency and accuracy in Large Language Models without additional training.

### 4. Captain Cinema: Towards Short Movie Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.18634)

Captain Cinema generates coherent short movies from textual descriptions using top-down keyframe planning and bottom-up video synthesis with Multimodal Diffusion Transformers.

### 5. GLiNER2: An Efficient Multi-Task Information Extraction System with
  Schema-Driven Interface

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.18546)

GLiNER2 is a unified transformer-based framework that supports multiple NLP tasks with improved efficiency and accessibility compared to large language models.

### 6. LAPO: Internalizing Reasoning Efficiency via Length-Adaptive Policy
  Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15758)

Length-Adaptive Policy Optimization (LAPO) reduces token usage and improves accuracy by enabling models to internally manage reasoning depth through reinforcement learning.

### 7. EarthCrafter: Scalable 3D Earth Generation via Dual-Sparse Latent
  Diffusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.16535)

EarthCrafter, a framework using sparse-decoupled latent diffusion, efficiently generates large-scale 3D Earth models by separating structural and textural generation, leveraging the Aerial-Earth3D dataset.

### 8. TTS-VAR: A Test-Time Scaling Framework for Visual Auto-Regressive
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.18537)

TTS-VAR is a test-time scaling framework for visual auto-regressive models that improves generation quality by dynamically adjusting batch size and using clustering and resampling techniques.

### 9. Hierarchical Budget Policy Optimization for Adaptive Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15844)

Hierarchical Budget Policy Optimization (HBPO) is a reinforcement learning framework that optimizes reasoning depth for large models, improving efficiency and accuracy by adapting to problem complexity.

### 10. DriftMoE: A Mixture of Experts Approach to Handle Concept Drifts

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.18464)

DriftMoE, an online Mixture-of-Experts architecture, addresses concept drift in non-stationary data streams through a co-training framework that enhances expert specialization and resource efficiency.

### 1. AgentFly: Fine-tuning LLM Agents without Fine-tuning LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.16153)

A novel memory-augmented reinforcement learning paradigm enables adaptive LLM agents to continually learn without fine-tuning, using episodic memory and a neural case-selection policy.

### 2. Beyond Pass@1: Self-Play with Variational Problem Synthesis Sustains
  RLVR

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.14029)

An online self-play strategy with variational problem synthesis for RLVR training maintains policy entropy and improves Pass@k performance on reasoning benchmarks.

### 3. AgentScope 1.0: A Developer-Centric Framework for Building Agentic
  Applications

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.16279)

AgentScope enhances agentic applications by providing flexible tool-based interactions, unified interfaces, and advanced infrastructure based on the ReAct paradigm, supporting efficient and safe development and deployment.

### 4. ODYSSEY: Open-World Quadrupeds Exploration and Manipulation for
  Long-Horizon Tasks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.08240)

ODYSSEY is a unified mobile manipulation framework for quadruped robots that integrates high-level task planning with low-level whole-body control, addressing challenges in egocentric perception, generalization, and coordination in unstructured environments.

### 5. EgoTwin: Dreaming Body and View in First Person

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.13013)

EgoTwin, a diffusion transformer framework, addresses viewpoint alignment and causal interplay in joint egocentric video and human motion generation using a head-centric motion representation and cybernetics-inspired interaction mechanism.

### 6. CRISP: Persistent Concept Unlearning via Sparse Autoencoders

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.13650)

CRISP is a parameter-efficient method using sparse autoencoders to permanently remove unwanted knowledge from large language models while preserving their utility.

### 7. AetherCode: Evaluating LLMs' Ability to Win In Premier Programming
  Competitions

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.16402)

AetherCode is a new benchmark for evaluating Large Language Models in competitive programming, offering more challenging and expert-validated test cases than existing benchmarks.

### 8. End-to-End Agentic RAG System Training for Traceable Diagnostic
  Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.15746)

Deep-DxSearch, an agentic RAG system trained with reinforcement learning, enhances medical diagnosis accuracy by integrating a large-scale retrieval corpus and tailored rewards.

### 9. Selective Contrastive Learning for Weakly Supervised Affordance
  Grounding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.07877)

The method uses selective prototypical and pixel contrastive objectives to learn affordance-relevant cues from third-person demonstrations, improving upon traditional weakly supervised affordance grounding by focusing on both part and object levels.

### 10. TPLA: Tensor Parallel Latent Attention for Efficient Disaggregated
  Prefill \& Decode Inference

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.15881)

Tensor-Parallel Latent Attention (TPLA) enhances tensor parallelism efficiency by partitioning latent representations and input dimensions, preserving the benefits of compressed key-value caches while maintaining strong representational capacity.

### 1. Absolute Zero: Reinforced Self-play Reasoning with Zero Data

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.03335)

Absolute Zero Reasoner (AZR) achieves state-of-the-art performance on coding and mathematical reasoning tasks through self-generated, verified learning without external data.

### 2. Unified Multimodal Chain-of-Thought Reward Model through Reinforcement
  Fine-Tuning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.03318)

A unified multimodal reward model that incorporates long chains of thought reasoning improves reliability and accuracy in vision reward tasks through exploration-driven reinforcement learning.

### 3. RADLADS: Rapid Attention Distillation to Linear Attention Decoders at
  Scale

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.03005)

A protocol converts softmax attention transformers into linear attention decoders using minimal tokens while maintaining quality and performance.

### 4. RetroInfer: A Vector-Storage Approach for Scalable Long-Context LLM
  Inference

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02922)

RetroInfer, a system that uses a vector storage approach based on attention sparsity, significantly accelerates inference for large language models with long contexts without reducing accuracy.

### 5. FlexiAct: Towards Flexible Action Control in Heterogeneous Scenarios

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.03730)

FlexiAct transfers actions from reference videos to arbitrary target images, adapting to diverse layouts, viewpoints, and skeletal structures while maintaining identity consistency using RefAdapter and FAE.

### 6. An Empirical Study of Qwen3 Quantization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02214)

This study evaluates the impact of low-bit quantization on Qwen3, a state-of-the-art LLM, across various bit-widths and datasets, revealing performance trade-offs and suggesting areas for further research to improve quantization methods.

### 7. Multi-Agent System for Comprehensive Soccer Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.03735)

A framework for comprehensive soccer understanding includes a knowledge base, benchmark, and multi-agent system for reasoning and evaluation.

### 8. Decoding Open-Ended Information Seeking Goals from Eye Movements in
  Reading

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02872)

LLMs can ascertain readers' specific information goals through eye movement data, as demonstrated by successful goal classification and reconstruction tasks.

### 9. HoloTime: Taming Video Diffusion Models for Panoramic 4D Scene
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.21650)

HoloTime uses a two-stage panoramic diffusion model and a space-time depth estimation method to generate high-fidelity 4D assets for immersive VR and AR experiences.

### 10. SWE-smith: Scaling Data for Software Engineering Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.21798)

SWE-smith automates the generation of large-scale software engineering training data and improves the performance of language models on automated software engineering tasks.

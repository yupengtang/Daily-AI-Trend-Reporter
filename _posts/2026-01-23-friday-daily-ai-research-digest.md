### 4. The Flexibility Trap: Why Arbitrary Order Limits Reasoning Potential in Diffusion Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.15165)

Arbitrary order generation in diffusion large language models limits reasoning capability by causing premature solution space collapse, making standard policy optimization more effective.

### 5. HERMES: KV Cache as Hierarchical Memory for Efficient Streaming Video Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.14724)

HERMES is a training-free architecture that enables real-time video stream understanding by utilizing a hierarchical memory framework based on KV cache reuse, achieving faster response times and maintained accuracy even with reduced video token input.

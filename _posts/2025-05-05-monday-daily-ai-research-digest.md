### 1. Llama-Nemotron: Efficient Reasoning Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.00949)

Llama-Nemotron models offer exceptional reasoning capabilities, inference efficiency, and open licensing through neural architecture search, knowledge distillation, and reasoning-focused post-training, including supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning.

### 2. PixelHacker: Image Inpainting with Structural and Semantic Consistency

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.20438)

PixelHacker, a diffusion-based model using latent categories guidance and linear attention, enhances image inpainting by improving structural and semantic consistency across various datasets.

### 3. Improving Editability in Image Generation with Layer-wise Memory

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.01079)

A framework for iterative image editing uses layer-wise memory and background consistency guidance to maintain scene coherence and adapt new elements naturally across sequential edits.

### 4. Beyond One-Size-Fits-All: Inversion Learning for Highly Effective NLG
  Evaluation Prompts

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.21117)

Inversion learning automates the generation of effective evaluation prompts for language models, improving robustness and efficiency over manual processes.

### 5. Real-World Gaps in AI Governance Research

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.00174)

Research by leading AI organizations focuses more on pre-deployment stages like model alignment and testing over deployment issues such as bias, with significant gaps existing in high-risk areas like healthcare and finance.

### 6. CORG: Generating Answers from Complex, Interrelated Contexts

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.00023)

Context Organizer (CORG) addresses complex interrelationships in a corpus by organizing multiple contexts, balancing performance and efficiency.

### 7. WorldGenBench: A World-Knowledge-Integrated Benchmark for
  Reasoning-Driven Text-to-Image Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.01490)

WorldGenBench evaluates text-to-image models using the Knowledge Checklist Score to identify gaps in world knowledge grounding and implicit reasoning.

### 8. TeLoGraF: Temporal Logic Planning via Graph-encoded Flow Matching

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.00562)

TeLoGraF uses Graph Neural Networks and flow-matching to solve general STL specifications in various simulation environments, outperforming baselines in STL satisfaction rate and inference speed.

### 9. X-Cross: Dynamic Integration of Language Models for Cross-Domain
  Sequential Recommendation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.20859)

X-Cross is a novel cross-domain sequential-recommendation model using low-rank adapters to dynamically refine representations and adapt to new domains efficiently with minimal fine-tuning data and parameters.

### 1. Every Activation Boosted: Scaling General Reasoner to 1 Trillion Open
  Language Foundation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.22115)

Ling 2.0, a reasoning-oriented language model series, achieves high efficiency and accuracy through a Mixture-of-Experts paradigm, sparse activation, and innovative training techniques.

### 2. EBT-Policy: Energy Unlocks Emergent Physical Reasoning Capabilities

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.27545)

EBT-Policy, an energy-based architecture, outperforms diffusion-based policies in robotic tasks by offering improved robustness, reduced computational cost, and emergent zero-shot recovery capabilities.

### 3. World Simulation with Video Foundation Models for Physical AI

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.00062)

Cosmos-Predict2.5 and Cosmos-Transfer2.5 are advanced Physical AI models that unify text, image, and video generation, improve video quality and instruction alignment, and enable Sim2Real and Real2Real world translation with higher fidelity.

### 4. Generalizing Test-time Compute-optimal Scaling as an Optimizable Graph

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.00086)

Agent-REINFORCE optimizes multi-LLM collaboration graphs for test-time scaling, improving sample efficiency and search performance under accuracy and latency constraints.

### 5. UniREditBench: A Unified Reasoning-based Image Editing Benchmark

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.01295)

UniREditBench is a unified benchmark for reasoning-based image editing that addresses limitations in existing benchmarks by including multi-object interactions, game-world scenarios, and multimodal dual-reference evaluation.

### 6. UniLumos: Fast and Unified Image and Video Relighting with
  Physics-Plausible Feedback

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.01678)

UniLumos, a unified relighting framework, enhances physical plausibility by integrating RGB-space geometry feedback into a flow matching backbone, achieving state-of-the-art results with improved consistency and speed.

### 7. The Underappreciated Power of Vision Models for Graph Structural
  Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.24788)

Vision models outperform Graph Neural Networks on tasks requiring global structural understanding and scale-invariant reasoning, as demonstrated by the new GraphAbstract benchmark.

### 8. MotionStream: Real-Time Video Generation with Interactive Motion
  Controls

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.01266)

MotionStream enables real-time video generation with sub-second latency and up to 29 FPS by distilling a text-to-video model with motion control into a causal student using Self Forcing with Distribution Matching Distillation and sliding-window causal attention with attention sinks.

### 9. ROVER: Benchmarking Reciprocal Cross-Modal Reasoning for Omnimodal
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.01163)

ROVER is a benchmark that evaluates reciprocal cross-modal reasoning in unified multimodal models, showing that cross-modal interactions significantly impact visual generation quality and that models struggle with symbolic reasoning tasks.

### 10. MR-Align: Meta-Reasoning Informed Factuality Alignment for Large
  Reasoning Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.24794)

MR-ALIGN, a Meta-Reasoning informed alignment framework, enhances the factuality of large reasoning models by aligning their reasoning process, improving accuracy and reducing misleading reasoning.

### 1. MiniMax-M1: Scaling Test-Time Compute Efficiently with Lightning
  Attention

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.13585)

A hybrid-attention reasoning model called MiniMax-M1, featuring a Mixture-of-Experts architecture and lightning attention mechanism, is introduced for efficient long-input processing and reinforcement learning.

### 2. DeepResearch Bench: A Comprehensive Benchmark for Deep Research Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.11763)

DeepResearch Bench offers a benchmark framework to evaluate the capabilities of Deep Research Agents in terms of research quality and information retrieval accuracy across multiple fields.

### 3. Scientists' First Exam: Probing Cognitive Abilities of MLLM via
  Perception, Understanding, and Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10521)

Scientists' First Exam (SFE) benchmark assesses scientific cognitive capacities of Multimodal Large Language Models through perception, understanding, and comparative reasoning.

### 4. Wait, We Don't Need to "Wait"! Removing Thinking Tokens Improves
  Reasoning Efficiency

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.08343)

NoWait suppresses explicit self-reflection tokens during inference to enhance efficiency in multimodal reasoning without reducing model utility.

### 5. DoTA-RAG: Dynamic of Thought Aggregation RAG

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.12571)

DoTA-RAG improves retrieval and generation accuracy over massive web datasets using a dynamic routing pipeline and optimized embedding models, achieving high correctness scores while maintaining low latency.

### 6. Essential-Web v1.0: 24T tokens of organized web data

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.14111)

A large, 24-trillion-token Essential-Web v1.0 dataset annotated with a multi-category taxonomy outperforms or is competitive with existing datasets in various domains using simple filtering techniques.

### 7. Marrying Autoregressive Transformer and Diffusion with Multi-Reference
  Autoregression

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.09482)

TransDiff, combining an Autoregressive Transformer and diffusion models, achieves superior image generation performance and speed, while Multi-Reference Autoregression further enhances its quality and diversity.

### 8. Discrete Diffusion in Large Language and Multimodal Models: A Survey

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.13759)

Discrete Diffusion Language Models (dLLMs) and Discrete Diffusion Multimodal Language Models (dMLLMs) enable parallel generation and faster inference compared to autoregressive models through denoising-based strategies and full attention mechanisms.

### 9. Ego-R1: Chain-of-Tool-Thought for Ultra-Long Egocentric Video Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.13654)

Ego-R1, a reinforcement learning-based framework, uses a structured tool-augmented chain-of-thought process to reason over ultra-long egocentric videos, achieving better performance than existing methods by extending time coverage to a week.

### 10. TaskCraft: Automated Generation of Agentic Tasks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10055)

TaskCraft automates the generation of scalable, multi-tool, and complex agentic tasks to enhance prompt optimization and fine-tuning of agentic models.

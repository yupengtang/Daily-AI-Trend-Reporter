### 1. Kwai Keye-VL Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01949)

Kwai Keye-VL, an 8-billion-parameter multimodal model, excels in short-video understanding and general vision-language tasks through a comprehensive pre-training and post-training process, including a five-mode data mixture and reinforcement learning.

### 2. LongAnimation: Long Animation Generation with Dynamic Global-Local
  Memory

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01945)

LongAnimation framework achieves long-term color consistency in animation colorization by integrating a dynamic global-local memory and color consistency reward.

### 3. Depth Anything at Any Condition

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01634)

DepthAnything-AC is a monocular depth estimation model that uses unsupervised consistency regularization and spatial distance constraints to handle complex environmental conditions and achieve zero-shot performance across various benchmarks.

### 4. A Survey on Vision-Language-Action Models: An Action Tokenization
  Perspective

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01925)

This survey categorizes and analyzes vision-language-action models through the lens of action tokenization, identifying strengths, limitations, and future directions.

### 5. Locality-aware Parallel Decoding for Efficient Autoregressive Image
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01957)

Locality-aware Parallel Decoding accelerates autoregressive image generation by enabling flexible parallelization and optimized generation ordering, significantly reducing latency without compromising quality.

### 6. FreeMorph: Tuning-Free Generalized Image Morphing with Diffusion Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01953)

FreeMorph is a tuning-free method for image morphing that uses guidance-aware spherical interpolation and step-oriented variation trend to achieve high-quality results faster than existing methods.

### 7. μ^2Tokenizer: Differentiable Multi-Scale Multi-Modal Tokenizer for
  Radiology Report Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.00316)

A multiscale multimodal large language model, $\mu^2$LLM, enhances automated radiology report generation by integrating visual and textual features and optimizing report quality through direct preference optimization.

### 8. MARVIS: Modality Adaptive Reasoning over VISualizations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01544)

MARVIS, a training-free method, enhances small vision-language models to predict across various data modalities with high accuracy using latent embeddings and spatial reasoning.

### 9. ARIG: Autoregressive Interactive Head Generation for Real-time
  Conversations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.00472)

An autoregressive framework for real-time interactive head generation improves interaction realism through bidirectional learning and detailed conversational state understanding.

### 10. JAM-Flow: Joint Audio-Motion Synthesis with Flow Matching

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23552)

JAM-Flow, a unified framework using flow matching and a Multi-Modal Diffusion Transformer, integrates facial motion and speech synthesis with selective joint attention layers and temporally aligned positional embeddings.

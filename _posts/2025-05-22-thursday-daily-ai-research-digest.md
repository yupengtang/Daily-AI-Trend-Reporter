### 1. Web-Shepherd: Advancing PRMs for Reinforcing Web Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.15277)

The paper introduces Web-Shepherd, a process reward model for web navigation, which improves accuracy and cost-effectiveness in step-level trajectory assessment compared to existing multimodal large language models.

### 2. MMaDA: Multimodal Large Diffusion Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.15809)

MMaDA, a multimodal diffusion foundation model, achieves superior performance through a unified architecture, mixed long chain-of-thought fine-tuning, and a unified policy-gradient-based RL algorithm.

### 3. Scaling Law for Quantization-Aware Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14302)

A unified scaling law for quantization-aware training (QAT) identifies key factors affecting quantization error, leading to improvements through mixed-precision quantization.

### 4. Diffusion vs. Autoregressive Language Models: A Text Embedding
  Perspective

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.15045)

Diffusion language models outperform large language model embeddings in text retrieval tasks due to their bidirectional architecture.

### 5. UniVG-R1: Reasoning Guided Universal Visual Grounding with Reinforcement
  Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14231)

UniVG-R1, a reasoning-guided multimodal large language model, enhances visual grounding by leveraging reinforcement learning and a difficulty-aware strategy, achieving state-of-the-art results and strong generalizability.

### 6. Efficient Agent Training for Computer Use

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.13909)

PC Agent-E framework improves data efficiency and achieves superior performance on human-like computer use tasks through enhanced trajectory synthesis and training.

### 7. This Time is Different: An Observability Perspective on Time Series
  Foundation Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14766)

Toto is a time series forecasting foundation model using a decoder-only architecture, demonstrating state-of-the-art performance on large-scale benchmarks with multivariate observability data.

### 8. Learn to Reason Efficiently with Adaptive Length-based Reward Shaping

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.15612)

RL-based reward shaping methods, particularly LASER-D, enhance reasoning efficiency and performance in large reasoning models by dynamically adapting to difficulty and reducing redundancy.

### 9. Vid2World: Crafting Video Diffusion Models to Interactive World Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14357)

Vid2World repurposes pre-trained video diffusion models into interactive world models via causalization and action guidance, enhancing action controllability and scalability in complex environments.

### 10. Constructing a 3D Town from a Single Image

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.15765)

A training-free framework named 3DTown generates realistic 3D scenes from a single top-down image using region-based generation and spatial-aware 3D inpainting techniques.

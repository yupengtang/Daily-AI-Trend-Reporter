### 4. SimpleTIR: End-to-End Reinforcement Learning for Multi-Turn
  Tool-Integrated Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.02479)

SimpleTIR stabilizes multi-turn Tool-Integrated Reasoning training by filtering out void turns, achieving state-of-the-art performance on math reasoning benchmarks.

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.01055)

VerlTool is a unified and modular framework for Agentic Reinforcement Learning with Tool use, addressing inefficiencies in existing approaches and providing competitive performance across multiple domains.

### 6. Reasoning Vectors: Transferring Chain-of-Thought Capabilities via Task

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.01363)

### 7. ELV-Halluc: Benchmarking Semantic Aggregation Hallucinations in Long
  Video Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.21496)

A benchmark for long-video hallucination identifies and investigates Semantic Aggregation Hallucination (SAH), showing its prevalence in complex and rapidly changing semantic contexts, and proposes strategies to mitigate it.

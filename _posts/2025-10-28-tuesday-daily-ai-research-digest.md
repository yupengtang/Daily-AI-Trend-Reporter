### 1. Concerto: Joint 2D-3D Self-Supervised Learning Emerges Spatial
  Representations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.23607)

Concerto, a minimalist model combining 3D self-distillation and 2D-3D joint embedding, achieves superior spatial feature learning and outperforms existing models in scene understanding and open-world perception.

### 2. ReCode: Unify Plan and Action for Universal Granularity Control

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.23564)

ReCode, a recursive code generation paradigm, unifies high-level planning and low-level action in a single representation, enhancing decision granularity and data efficiency in LLM-based agents.

### 3. A Survey of Data Agents: Emerging Paradigm or Overstated Hype?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.23587)

A systematic taxonomy for data agents is introduced to clarify their autonomy levels and capabilities, addressing terminological ambiguity and guiding future research and development.

### 4. FARMER: Flow AutoRegressive Transformer over Pixels

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.23588)

FARMER, a unified generative framework combining Normalizing Flows and Autoregressive models, achieves competitive image synthesis performance with exact likelihoods and scalable training.

### 5. Lookahead Anchoring: Preserving Character Identity in Audio-Driven Human
  Animation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.23581)

Lookahead Anchoring improves audio-driven human animation by using future keyframes as dynamic guides, enhancing lip synchronization, identity preservation, and visual quality.

### 6. IGGT: Instance-Grounded Geometry Transformer for Semantic 3D
  Reconstruction

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.22706)

InstanceGrounded Geometry Transformer (IGGT) unifies 3D reconstruction and instance-level understanding using a unified transformer and 3D-Consistent Contrastive Learning, supported by a new dataset InsScene-15K.

### 7. VITA-E: Natural Embodied Interaction with Concurrent Seeing, Hearing,
  Speaking, and Acting

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.21817)

VITA-E, a dual-model embodied interaction framework, enables concurrent and interruptible vision-language-action capabilities, enhancing real-time user interaction.

### 8. ACG: Action Coherence Guidance for Flow-based VLA models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.22201)

Action Coherence Guidance (ACG) improves action coherence in Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models during test time, enhancing performance in diverse manipulation tasks.

### 9. LongCat-Video Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.22200)

LongCat-Video, a 13.6B parameter video generation model based on the Diffusion Transformer framework, excels in efficient and high-quality long video generation across multiple tasks using unified architecture, coarse-to-fine generation, and block sparse attention.

### 10. E^2Rank: Your Text Embedding can Also be an Effective
  and Efficient Listwise Reranker

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.22733)

A unified framework extends a single text embedding model to perform both retrieval and listwise reranking, achieving state-of-the-art results with low latency.

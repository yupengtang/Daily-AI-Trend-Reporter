### 1. EXAONE 4.0: Unified Large Language Models Integrating Non-reasoning and
  Reasoning Modes

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.11407)

EXAONE 4.0 integrates non-reasoning and reasoning modes, supports agentic tool use, and offers multilingual capabilities, demonstrating superior performance in its class.

### 2. Vision-Language-Vision Auto-Encoder: Scalable Knowledge Distillation
  from Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07104)

The VLV auto-encoder framework uses pretrained vision and text models to create a cost-efficient, high-quality captioning system with reduced data requirements.

### 3. Scaling Laws for Optimal Data Mixtures

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.09404)

Scaling laws predict optimal data mixtures for large foundation models across different domains, improving performance and reducing trial-and-error.

### 4. OpenCodeReasoning-II: A Simple Test Time Scaling Approach via
  Self-Critique

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.09075)

OpenCodeReasoning-II, a large dataset for code reasoning, enhances code generation and critique through a two-stage fine-tuning strategy, improving competitive coding performance and extending LiveCodeBench for C++.

### 5. AgentsNet: Coordination and Collaborative Reasoning in Multi-Agent LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.08616)

AgentsNet is a new benchmark for evaluating multi-agent systems' ability to self-organize, communicate, and solve problems collaboratively across varying network sizes.

### 6. Taming generative video models for zero-shot optical flow extraction

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.09082)

Counterfactual prompting of generative video models, using KL-tracing, extracts optical flow without fine-tuning, outperforming state-of-the-art methods on real and synthetic datasets.

### 7. Can Multimodal Foundation Models Understand Schematic Diagrams? An
  Empirical Study on Information-Seeking QA over Scientific Papers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.10787)

A benchmark evaluates multimodal models' ability to interpret schematic diagrams in scientific literature, revealing performance gaps compared to human experts.

### 8. BYOKG-RAG: Multi-Strategy Graph Retrieval for Knowledge Graph Question
  Answering

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.04127)

BYOKG-RAG combines LLMs with specialized graph retrieval tools to enhance KGQA, improving generalization and performance over custom knowledge graphs.

### 9. UGC-VideoCaptioner: An Omni UGC Video Detail Caption Model and New
  Benchmarks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.11336)

UGC-VideoCap introduces a new benchmark and model for detailed omnimodal captioning of short-form user-generated videos, emphasizing balanced audio-visual integration and using a novel two-stage training strategy.

### 10. Token-based Audio Inpainting via Discrete Diffusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.08333)

A discrete diffusion model for audio inpainting using tokenized audio representations achieves competitive performance for reconstructing long gaps in corrupted audio recordings.

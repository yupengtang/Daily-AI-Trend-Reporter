### 1. On the Generalization of SFT: A Reinforcement Learning Perspective with
  Reward Rectification

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.05629)

Dynamic Fine-Tuning (DFT) improves the generalization of Large Language Models (LLMs) by dynamically rescaling gradients, outperforming standard Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) and showing competitive results in offline reinforcement learning.

### 2. R-Zero: Self-Evolving Reasoning LLM from Zero Data

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.05004)

R-Zero is a self-evolving framework that autonomously generates and learns from its own training data, improving reasoning capabilities in LLMs without human-curated tasks.

### 3. Genie Envisioner: A Unified World Foundation Platform for Robotic
  Manipulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.05635)

Genie Envisioner integrates policy learning, evaluation, and simulation using a video diffusion model and neural simulator for instruction-driven robotic manipulation.

### 4. DeepPHY: Benchmarking Agentic VLMs on Physical Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.05405)

DeepPHY evaluates Vision Language Models' physical reasoning and control through simulated environments with varying difficulty levels.

### 5. Hi3DEval: Advancing 3D Generation Evaluation with Hierarchical Validity

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.05609)

Hi3DEval is a hierarchical evaluation framework for 3D generative content that combines object-level and part-level assessments, including material realism, using a large-scale dataset and hybrid 3D representations.

### 6. Are Today's LLMs Ready to Explain Well-Being Concepts?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.03990)

LLMs can be fine-tuned to generate high-quality, audience-tailored explanations of well-being concepts using Supervised Fine-Tuning and Direct Preference Optimization.

### 7. Are We on the Right Way for Assessing Document Retrieval-Augmented
  Generation?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.03644)

Double-Bench is a large-scale, multilingual, and multimodal evaluation system for document Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, addressing limitations in current benchmarks and providing comprehensive assessments of system components.

### 8. Don't Overthink It: A Survey of Efficient R1-style Large Reasoning
  Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.02120)

Research on efficient reasoning methods for Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) aims to reduce reasoning path length without sacrificing performance, through single-model optimization and model collaboration.

### 9. Marco-Voice Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.02038)

A multifunctional speech synthesis system integrates voice cloning and emotion control using speaker-emotion disentanglement and rotational emotional embeddings, achieving high expressive and natural speech.

### 10. CoAct-1: Computer-using Agents with Coding as Actions

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.03923)

A multi-agent system that combines GUI control with programmatic execution improves efficiency and success in complex computer automation tasks.

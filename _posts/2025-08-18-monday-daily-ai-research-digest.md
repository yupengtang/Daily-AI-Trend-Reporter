### 1. DINOv3

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.10104)

DINOv3, a self-supervised learning model, achieves superior performance across various vision tasks by scaling datasets and models, addressing dense feature degradation, and enhancing flexibility with post-hoc strategies.

### 2. SSRL: Self-Search Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.10874)

LLMs can serve as efficient simulators for RL tasks by leveraging internal knowledge, reducing reliance on external search engines and improving sim-to-real transfer.

### 3. Thyme: Think Beyond Images

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.11630)

Thyme, a novel paradigm, enables MLLMs to autonomously perform image manipulations and computations, enhancing performance in perception and reasoning tasks through a two-stage training strategy and GRPO-ATS algorithm.

### 4. BeyondWeb: Lessons from Scaling Synthetic Data for Trillion-scale
  Pretraining

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.10975)

BeyondWeb, a synthetic data generation framework, outperforms existing datasets and accelerates training for large language models by optimizing multiple factors.

### 5. XQuant: Breaking the Memory Wall for LLM Inference with KV Cache
  Rematerialization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.10395)

XQuant and XQuant-CL reduce memory consumption in LLM inference through low-bit quantization and cross-layer similarity exploitation, achieving significant memory savings with minimal accuracy loss.

### 6. PaperRegister: Boosting Flexible-grained Paper Search via Hierarchical
  Register Indexing

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.11116)

PaperRegister enhances paper search by using hierarchical indexing and adaptive retrieval, supporting flexible and fine-grained queries beyond traditional abstract-based systems.

### 7. TexVerse: A Universe of 3D Objects with High-Resolution Textures

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.10868)

TexVerse is a large-scale 3D dataset with high-resolution textures, including PBR materials, rigged models, and animated models, suitable for texture synthesis, PBR material development, and 3D vision tasks.

### 8. FantasyTalking2: Timestep-Layer Adaptive Preference Optimization for
  Audio-Driven Portrait Animation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.11255)

A multimodal reward model and adaptive preference optimization framework improve audio-driven portrait animation by aligning with human preferences across multiple dimensions.

### 9. StyleMM: Stylized 3D Morphable Face Model via Text-Driven Aligned Image
  Translation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.11203)

StyleMM constructs stylized 3DMMs from text descriptions using a diffusion model for image-to-image translation while preserving facial attributes.

### 10. MAESTRO: Masked AutoEncoders for Multimodal, Multitemporal, and
  Multispectral Earth Observation Data

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.10894)

MAESTRO, an adapted Masked Autoencoder with optimized fusion strategies and spectral prior normalization, achieves state-of-the-art performance on multitemporal Earth observation tasks.

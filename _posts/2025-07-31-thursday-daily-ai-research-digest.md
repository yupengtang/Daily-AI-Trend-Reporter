### 1. ScreenCoder: Advancing Visual-to-Code Generation for Front-End Automation via Modular Multimodal Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.22827)

A modular multi-agent framework named ScreenCoder decomposes UI design-to-code translation into grounding, planning, and generation stages, achieving superior layout accuracy and code correctness through specialized agents and fine-tuned multimodal models.

### 2. Falcon-H1: A Family of Hybrid-Head Language Models Redefining Efficiency
  and Performance

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.22448)

Falcon-H1, a new series of large language models with a hybrid architecture combining Transformer-based attention and State Space Models, achieves state-of-the-art performance and efficiency across various tasks and sizes.

### 3. BANG: Dividing 3D Assets via Generative Exploded Dynamics

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.21493)

BANG is a generative approach using latent diffusion models and temporal attention to enable intuitive, part-level decomposition of 3D objects with precise control and multimodal interaction.

### 4. VL-Cogito: Progressive Curriculum Reinforcement Learning for Advanced
  Multimodal Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.22607)

VL-Cogito, a multimodal reasoning model, uses a Progressive Curriculum Reinforcement Learning framework to improve performance across diverse tasks by dynamically adjusting difficulty and reasoning path length.

### 5. MetaCLIP 2: A Worldwide Scaling Recipe

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.22062)

MetaCLIP 2, trained on worldwide web-scale image-text pairs, improves zero-shot classification and multilingual benchmarks without system-level confounding factors.

### 6. Step-3 is Large yet Affordable: Model-system Co-design for
  Cost-effective Decoding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.19427)

Step-3, a 321B-parameter VLM, optimizes decoding costs through Multi-Matrix Factorization Attention and Attention-FFN Disaggregation, achieving high efficiency and throughput compared to other models.

### 7. MixGRPO: Unlocking Flow-based GRPO Efficiency with Mixed ODE-SDE

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.21802)

MixGRPO, a novel framework integrating SDE and ODE, enhances flow matching models for image generation by optimizing only within a sliding window, improving efficiency and performance.

### 8. Adapting Vehicle Detectors for Aerial Imagery to Unseen Domains with
  Weak Supervision

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.20976)

A multi-stage, multi-modal knowledge transfer framework using fine-tuned latent diffusion models improves vehicle detection in aerial imagery across different domains.

### 9. Towards Omnimodal Expressions and Reasoning in Referring Audio-Visual
  Segmentation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.22886)

Omnimodal Referring Audio-Visual Segmentation (OmniAVS) and Omnimodal Instructed Segmentation Assistant (OISA) advance audio-visual segmentation by integrating complex multimodal expressions and leveraging MLLM for reasoning-based segmentation.

### 10. Repair-R1: Better Test Before Repair

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.22853)

Repair-R1 enhances automated program repair by integrating test cases into the training phase and prioritizing test generation before repair, improving repair success, test generation success, and test coverage.

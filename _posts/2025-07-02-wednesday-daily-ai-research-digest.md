### 1. GLM-4.1V-Thinking: Towards Versatile Multimodal Reasoning with Scalable
  Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01006)

A vision-language model (VLM) named GLM-4.1V-Thinking, developed with a reasoning-centric training framework, achieves state-of-the-art performance across various tasks, including STEM problem solving, video understanding, and long document understanding, outperforming larger models on many benchmarks.

### 2. Does Math Reasoning Improve General LLM Capabilities? Understanding
  Transferability of LLM Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.00432)

Reinforcement learning-tuned models generalize better across domains compared to supervised fine-tuned models in reasoning tasks, indicating a need to reconsider standard training methods.

### 3. SciArena: An Open Evaluation Platform for Foundation Models in
  Scientific Literature Tasks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01001)

SciArena is a community-driven platform for evaluating foundation models on scientific literature tasks using collective intelligence and human voting.

### 4. Radial Attention: O(nlog n) Sparse Attention with Energy Decay for
  Long Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.19852)

Radial Attention, a scalable sparse attention mechanism, improves efficiency and preserves video quality in diffusion models by leveraging spatiotemporal energy decay.

### 5. MoCa: Modality-aware Continual Pre-training Makes Better Bidirectional
  Multimodal Embeddings

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23115)

MoCa, a two-stage framework, enhances pre-trained VLMs into effective bidirectional multimodal embedding models by addressing limitations through modality-aware continual pre-training and heterogeneous contrastive fine-tuning.

### 6. DiffuCoder: Understanding and Improving Masked Diffusion Models for Code
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.20639)

Diffusion large language models are applied to code generation, revealing their unique denoising processes and benefiting from a novel reinforcement learning sampling scheme.

### 7. Thinking Beyond Tokens: From Brain-Inspired Intelligence to Cognitive
  Foundations for Artificial General Intelligence and its Societal Impact

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.00951)

The paper explores the development of Artificial General Intelligence by integrating insights from various fields, focusing on modular reasoning, memory, and multi-agent coordination, and highlights the challenges in achieving true intelligence.

### 8. HumanOmniV2: From Understanding to Omni-Modal Reasoning with Context

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.21277)

The paper addresses challenges in multimodal reasoning by implementing context and logical rewards to enhance global context understanding and prevent shortcut problems, using a reasoning omni-modal benchmark called IntentBench.

### 9. Training for X-Ray Vision: Amodal Segmentation, Amodal Content
  Completion, and View-Invariant Object Representation from Multi-Camera Video

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.00339)

MOVi-MC-AC is a large amodal segmentation and content completion dataset featuring multi-camera perspectives and ground-truth amodal content labels.

### 10. MusiXQA: Advancing Visual Music Understanding in Multimodal Large
  Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23009)

MusiXQA, a new dataset for evaluating MLLMs on music sheet understanding, reveals limitations and enables the development of Phi-3-MusiX, an improved MLLM for this task.

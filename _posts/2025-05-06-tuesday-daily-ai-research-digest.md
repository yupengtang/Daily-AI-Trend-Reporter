### 1. Grokking in the Wild: Data Augmentation for Real-World Multi-Hop
  Reasoning with Transformers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.20752)

Grokking with synthetic data augmentation in knowledge graphs enhances Transformer models' multi-step factual reasoning on real-world tasks, achieving high accuracy on benchmarks.

### 2. Voila: Voice-Language Foundation Models for Real-Time Autonomous
  Interaction and Voice Role-Play

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02707)

Voila is an end-to-end voice-language model that enables low-latency, emotionally expressive voice interactions and supports multiple applications including ASR, TTS, and multilingual speech translation.

### 3. RM-R1: Reward Modeling as Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02387)

Reasoning Reward Models (ReasRMs) enhance reward modeling for large language models by integrating reasoning tasks, improving interpretability and performance.

### 4. Practical Efficiency of Muon for Pretraining

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02222)

Muon, a second-order optimizer, improves data efficiency and computational savings over AdamW, especially at large batch sizes, and combined with muP, it provides efficient hyperparameter transfer and minimal resource overhead.

### 5. A Survey on Inference Engines for Large Language Models: Perspectives on
  Optimization and Efficiency

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.01658)

A comprehensive evaluation of 25 open-source and commercial LLM inference engines across various criteria and optimization techniques is provided, with an outline of future research directions.

### 6. Agentic Reasoning and Tool Integration for LLMs via Reinforcement
  Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.01441)

ARTIST integrates agentic reasoning and reinforcement learning to enhance LLMs' ability to dynamically use tools and interact with environments, significantly improving performance on complex reasoning tasks.

### 7. FormalMATH: Benchmarking Formal Mathematical Reasoning of Large Language
  Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02735)

FormalMATH, a large-scale Lean4 benchmark, introduces an autoformalization pipeline to reduce manual annotation costs and identifies limitations of existing LLM-based theorem provers in formal mathematical reasoning.

### 8. R1-Reward: Training Multimodal Reward Model Through Stable Reinforcement
  Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02835)

Using Reinforcement Learning to improve reward modeling in Multimodal Reward Models leads to more stable training and superior performance.

### 9. ReplaceMe: Network Simplification via Depth Pruning and Transformer Block Linearization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02819)

ReplaceMe is a training-free depth pruning method that replaces transformer blocks with linear operations using calibration data, achieving high compression ratios with minimal performance loss.

### 10. Optimizing Chain-of-Thought Reasoners via Gradient Variance Minimization
  in Rejection Sampling and RL

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02391)

GVM-RAFT, a dynamic sampling strategy for chain-of-thought reasoning in large language models, improves convergence and accuracy by adaptively allocating computational resources.

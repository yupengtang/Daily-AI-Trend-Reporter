### 1. Chain-of-Agents: End-to-End Agent Foundation Models via Multi-Agent
  Distillation and Agentic RL

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.13167)

Chain-of-Agents (CoA) paradigm enables end-to-end complex problem-solving in LLMs through dynamic agent activation, improving performance via multi-agent distillation and agentic reinforcement learning.

### 2. LongSplat: Robust Unposed 3D Gaussian Splatting for Casual Long Videos

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.14041)

LongSplat improves novel view synthesis from long videos with irregular motion through joint optimization, robust pose estimation, and efficient anchor formation.

### 3. Prompt Orchestration Markup Language

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.13948)

POML addresses challenges in prompting Large Language Models by providing a structured, data-integrated, and format-sensitive markup language with templating and developer tools.

### 4. MultiRef: Controllable Image Generation with Multiple Visual References

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.06905)

Experiments with multiple image-text models and agentic frameworks show that even state-of-the-art systems struggle with generating images from multiple visual references, highlighting the need for more flexible creative tools.

### 5. MM-BrowseComp: A Comprehensive Benchmark for Multimodal Browsing Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.13186)

A new benchmark, MM-BrowseComp, evaluates AI agents' multimodal retrieval and reasoning capabilities, revealing limitations in current models' handling of images and videos in web browsing tasks.

### 6. Embodied-R1: Reinforced Embodied Reasoning for General Robotic
  Manipulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.13998)

A pointing-centric representation and reinforced fine-tuning approach improve embodied AI generalization across various tasks and visual disturbances.

### 7. Training-Free Text-Guided Color Editing with Multi-Modal Diffusion
  Transformer

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.09131)

ColorCtrl, a training-free method using Multi-Modal Diffusion Transformers, achieves precise and consistent color editing in images and videos with word-level control and superior performance compared to existing approaches.

### 8. Advances in Speech Separation: Techniques, Challenges, and Future Trends

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.10830)

A survey of DNN-based speech separation techniques, covering learning paradigms, separation scenarios, and architectural components, with a focus on current advancements and promising future directions.

### 9. OmniTry: Virtual Try-On Anything without Masks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.13632)

OmniTry extends Virtual Try-ON to various wearable objects using a two-stage pipeline that combines unpaired and paired image training to improve object localization and appearance consistency.

### 10. Evaluating Podcast Recommendations with Profile-Aware LLM-as-a-Judge

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.08777)

A novel framework uses Large Language Models to evaluate podcast recommendations by constructing user profiles and providing context for the LLM to make judgments, improving efficiency and interpretability.

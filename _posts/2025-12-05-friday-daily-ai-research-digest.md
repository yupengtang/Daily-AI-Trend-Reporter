### 1. Live Avatar: Streaming Real-time Audio-Driven Avatar Generation with Infinite Length

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.04677)

Live Avatar uses a 14-billion-parameter diffusion model with Timestep-forcing Pipeline Parallelism and Rolling Sink Frame Mechanism to achieve real-time, high-fidelity avatar generation.

### 2. DAComp: Benchmarking Data Agents across the Full Data Intelligence Lifecycle

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.04324)

DAComp is a benchmark of 210 tasks that evaluates the capabilities of agents in real-world data engineering and data analysis workflows, revealing significant deficiencies in both areas.

### 3. Nex-N1: Agentic Models Trained via a Unified Ecosystem for Large-Scale Environment Construction

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.04987)

The introduction of NexAU, NexA4A, and NexGAP enables the scaling of complexity, diversity, and fidelity in interactive environments for training large language models as autonomous agents, resulting in superior performance.

### 4. PaperDebugger: A Plugin-Based Multi-Agent System for In-Editor Academic Writing, Review, and Editing

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.02589)

PaperDebugger is an in-editor academic writing assistant that integrates large language models, enabling direct interaction within LaTeX editors for document state management, revision, and literature search.

### 5. ARM-Thinker: Reinforcing Multimodal Generative Reward Models with Agentic Tool Use and Visual Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.05111)

ARM-Thinker, an agentic reward model, uses external tools for verification, improving accuracy and interpretability in complex multimodal reasoning tasks.

### 6. Semantics Lead the Way: Harmonizing Semantic and Texture Modeling with Asynchronous Latent Diffusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.04926)

Semantic-First Diffusion (SFD) enhances image generation by asynchronously denoising semantic and texture latents, improving convergence and quality.

### 7. Reward Forcing: Efficient Streaming Video Generation with Rewarded Distribution Matching Distillation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.04678)

The paper introduces Reward Forcing, which enhances video generation by updating sink tokens with EMA-Sink and using Rewarded Distribution Matching Distillation to prioritize dynamic content.

### 8. DynamicVerse: A Physically-Aware Multimodal Framework for 4D World Modeling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.03000)

DynamicVerse is a framework that models dynamic real-world videos by integrating large vision, geometric, and multimodal models to produce a comprehensive 4D multimodal dataset, achieving superior performance in video depth estimation, camera pose estimation, and camera intrinsics estimation.

### 9. Deep Forcing: Training-Free Long Video Generation with Deep Sink and Participative Compression

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.05081)

Deep Forcing, a training-free method, enhances real-time video diffusion by addressing temporal repetition and motion issues through Deep Sink and Participative Compression, achieving high-quality, long-duration video generation.

### 10. SIMA 2: A Generalist Embodied Agent for Virtual Worlds

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.04797)

SIMA 2, built on a Gemini foundation model, interacts in 3D virtual worlds, reasons about goals, handles complex instructions, and autonomously learns new skills through open-ended self-improvement.

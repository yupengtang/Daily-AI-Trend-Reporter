### 1. StereoWorld: Geometry-Aware Monocular-to-Stereo Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.09363)

StereoWorld generates high-quality stereo video from monocular input using a pretrained video generator with geometry-aware regularization and spatio-temporal tiling.

### 2. OmniPSD: Layered PSD Generation with Diffusion Transformer

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.09247)

OmniPSD, a diffusion framework within the Flux ecosystem, enables text-to-PSD generation and image-to-PSD decomposition, achieving high-fidelity results with transparency awareness.

### 3. BrainExplore: Large-Scale Discovery of Interpretable Visual Representations in the Human Brain

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.08560)

An automated framework uses unsupervised decomposition and natural language descriptions to identify and explain visual representations in human brain fMRI data.

### 4. Composing Concepts from Images and Videos via Concept-prompt Binding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.09824)

Bind & Compose uses Diffusion Transformers with hierarchical binders and temporal strategies to accurately compose complex visual concepts from images and videos.

### 5. MotionEdit: Benchmarking and Learning Motion-Centric Image Editing

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.10284)

MotionEdit is a new dataset for motion-centric image editing, and MotionNFT is a post-training framework that enhances motion editing quality by aligning motion flow between input and edited images.

### 6. InfiniteVL: Synergizing Linear and Sparse Attention for Highly-Efficient, Unlimited-Input Vision-Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.08829)

InfiniteVL, a linear-complexity VLM architecture combining sliding window attention and Gated DeltaNet, achieves competitive performance with less data and faster inference than leading Transformer-based models.

### 7. Rethinking Chain-of-Thought Reasoning for Videos

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.09616)

Efficient video reasoning can be achieved using concise chains of thought and reduced visual tokens without manual annotations or supervised fine-tuning.

### 8. Towards a Science of Scaling Agent Systems

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.08296)

A quantitative framework for agent system scaling using empirical coordination metrics identifies optimal multi-agent strategies based on task properties.

### 9. HiF-VLA: Hindsight, Insight and Foresight through Motion Representation for Vision-Language-Action Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.09928)

HiF-VLA integrates motion for bidirectional temporal reasoning in VLA models, improving long-horizon manipulation performance with minimal additional latency.

### 10. Fast-Decoding Diffusion Language Models via Progress-Aware Confidence Schedules

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.02892)

SchED, a training-free early-exit algorithm, accelerates diffusion large language model decoding with minimal performance loss across various tasks.

### 1. EgoX: Egocentric Video Generation from a Single Exocentric Video

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.08269)

EgoX framework generates egocentric videos from exocentric inputs using video diffusion models with LoRA adaptation, unified conditioning, and geometry-guided self-attention for coherence and visual fidelity.

### 2. DentalGPT: Incentivizing Multimodal Complex Reasoning in Dentistry

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.11558)

DentalGPT, a specialized multimodal large language model for dentistry, achieves superior performance in disease classification and dental visual question answering through high-quality domain data and staged adaptation techniques.

### 3. PersonaLive! Expressive Portrait Image Animation for Live Streaming

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.11253)

PersonaLive is a diffusion-based portrait animation framework that improves real-time performance through hybrid implicit signals, appearance distillation, and autoregressive streaming generation.

### 4. SVG-T2I: Scaling Up Text-to-Image Latent Diffusion Model Without Variational Autoencoder

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.11749)

SVG-T2I framework enables high-quality text-to-image synthesis by training diffusion models within visual foundation model representation space, achieving competitive performance on benchmark datasets.

### 5. V-RGBX: Video Editing with Accurate Controls over Intrinsic Properties

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.11799)

V-RGBX presents an end-to-end framework for intrinsic-aware video editing that combines video inverse rendering, photorealistic video synthesis, and keyframe-based editing with physically grounded intrinsic channel manipulation.

### 6. Sharp Monocular View Synthesis in Less Than a Second

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.10685)

SHARP synthesizes photorealistic views from a single image using a 3D Gaussian representation, achieving state-of-the-art results with rapid processing.

### 7. Sliding Window Attention Adaptation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.10411)

Sliding Window Attention Adaptation (SWAA) enables Transformer-based Large Language Models (LLMs) to use sliding window attention without retraining, recovering long-context performance through a combination of adaptation techniques.

### 8. Exploring MLLM-Diffusion Information Transfer with MetaCanvas

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.11464)

Multimodal large language models can be enhanced for visual generation by enabling direct reasoning and planning in spatial and spatiotemporal latent spaces rather than just serving as text encoders for diffusion models.

### 9. Fast-FoundationStereo: Real-Time Zero-Shot Stereo Matching

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.11130)

Fast-FoundationStereo achieves real-time stereo matching with strong zero-shot generalization through efficient compression, neural architecture search, and structured pruning techniques.

### 10. MeshSplatting: Differentiable Rendering with Opaque Meshes

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.06818)

MeshSplatting enables real-time novel view synthesis by combining mesh-based reconstruction with differentiable rendering to produce high-quality, efficient 3D meshes.

### 1. Baseer: A Vision-Language Model for Arabic Document-to-Markdown OCR

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18174)

Baseer, a vision-language model fine-tuned for Arabic document OCR, achieves state-of-the-art performance using a decoder-only strategy and a large-scale dataset, outperforming existing solutions with a WER of 0.25.

### 2. Reinforcement Learning on Pre-Training Data

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.19249)

Reinforcement Learning on Pre-Training data (RLPT) optimizes large language models by autonomously exploring meaningful trajectories in pre-training data, improving generalizable reasoning skills without human annotation.

### 3. MiniCPM-V 4.5: Cooking Efficient MLLMs via Architecture, Data, and
  Training Recipe

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18154)

MiniCPM-V 4.5, a 8B parameter multimodal large language model, achieves high performance and efficiency through a unified 3D-Resampler architecture, a unified learning paradigm, and a hybrid reinforcement learning strategy.

### 4. Do You Need Proprioceptive States in Visuomotor Policies?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18644)

A state-free policy using only visual observations achieves better spatial generalization and data efficiency in robot manipulation tasks compared to state-based policies.

### 5. SWE-QA: Can Language Models Answer Repository-level Code Questions?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.14635)

SWE-QA is a repository-level code question answering benchmark that addresses the complexity of real-world software repositories, evaluated using advanced LLMs and an agentic framework.

### 6. Lyra: Generative 3D Scene Reconstruction via Video Diffusion Model
  Self-Distillation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.19296)

A self-distillation framework converts implicit 3D knowledge from video diffusion models into an explicit 3D Gaussian Splatting representation, enabling 3D scene generation from text or images.

### 7. How Far are VLMs from Visual Spatial Intelligence? A Benchmark-Driven
  Perspective

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18905)

Research investigates visual spatial reasoning in vision-language models, highlighting gaps between perceptual and reasoning capabilities, and introduces SIBench as a benchmark for future research.

### 8. MAPO: Mixed Advantage Policy Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18849)

Mixed Advantage Policy Optimization (MAPO) dynamically reweights the advantage function to improve trajectory ranking in reinforcement learning for foundation models.

### 9. VolSplat: Rethinking Feed-Forward 3D Gaussian Splatting with
  Voxel-Aligned Prediction

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.19297)

VolSplat, a voxel-aligned Gaussian prediction method, improves novel view synthesis by overcoming pixel alignment limitations and enhancing 3D reconstruction quality.

### 10. What Characterizes Effective Reasoning? Revisiting Length, Review, and
  Structure of CoT

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.19284)

Effective chain-of-thoughts in large reasoning models are characterized by fewer failed steps and better structural quality, not necessarily by length or review.

### 1. Open Data Synthesis For Deep Research

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.00375)

InfoSeek is a scalable framework for generating complex Deep Research tasks by synthesizing hierarchical constraint satisfaction problems, enabling models to outperform larger baselines on challenging benchmarks.

### 2. Robix: A Unified Model for Robot Interaction, Reasoning and Planning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.01106)

Robix, a unified vision-language model, integrates robot reasoning, task planning, and natural language interaction, demonstrating superior performance in interactive task execution through chain-of-thought reasoning and a three-stage training strategy.

### 3. LMEnt: A Suite for Analyzing Knowledge in Language Models from
  Pretraining Data to Representations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.03405)

LMEnt is a suite for analyzing knowledge acquisition in language models during pretraining, providing annotated corpora, retrieval methods, and pretrained models to study knowledge representations and learning dynamics.

### 4. Planning with Reasoning using Vision Language World Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.02722)

The Vision Language World Model (VLWM) achieves state-of-the-art performance in visual planning by integrating language-based world modeling, action policy learning, and dynamics modeling with semantic and temporal abstraction.

### 5. Beyond Correctness: Harmonizing Process and Outcome Rewards through RL
  Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.03403)

PROF, a data curation method, improves reinforcement learning for mathematical reasoning by harmonizing process and outcome rewards, enhancing both final accuracy and intermediate reasoning quality.

### 6. Mixture of Global and Local Experts with Diffusion Transformer for
  Controllable Face Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.00428)

Face-MoGLE, a novel framework using Diffusion Transformers, achieves high-quality, controllable face generation through semantic-decoupled latent modeling, expert specialization, and dynamic gating.

### 7. MOSAIC: Multi-Subject Personalized Generation via Correspondence-Aware
  Alignment and Disentanglement

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.01977)

MOSAIC framework enhances multi-subject image generation by ensuring precise semantic alignment and orthogonal feature disentanglement, achieving high fidelity even with multiple references.

### 8. Manipulation as in Simulation: Enabling Accurate Geometry Perception in
  Robots

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.02530)

Camera Depth Models (CDMs) enhance depth camera accuracy by denoising and improving metric depth prediction, enabling better generalization of robotic manipulation policies from simulation to real-world tasks.

### 9. SATQuest: A Verifier for Logical Reasoning Evaluation and Reinforcement
  Fine-Tuning of LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.00930)

SATQuest evaluates and enhances LLM logical reasoning by generating diverse SAT-based problems, offering insights into reasoning performance and enabling effective fine-tuning.

### 1. WebSailor: Navigating Super-human Reasoning for Web Agent

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02592)

WebSailor, a post-training methodology, enhances open-source LLMs with sophisticated reasoning to match proprietary systems in complex information-seeking tasks.

### 2. Thinking with Images for Multimodal Reasoning: Foundations, Methods, and
  Future Frontiers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23918)

The survey outlines the evolution of multimodal AI from treating vision as static context to integrating it dynamically into the reasoning process, highlighting three stages and key challenges.

### 3. Energy-Based Transformers are Scalable Learners and Thinkers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02092)

Energy-Based Transformers (EBTs) improve model performance and scalability across modalities by learning to verify predictions through unsupervised learning and energy minimization.

### 4. LangScene-X: Reconstruct Generalizable 3D Language-Embedded Scenes with
  TriMap Video Diffusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02813)

LangScene-X uses a TriMap video diffusion model and Language Quantized Compressor to generate 3D consistent multi-modality information from sparse 2D views, enabling open-ended language queries and superior generalization.

### 5. Skywork-Reward-V2: Scaling Preference Data Curation via Human-AI Synergy

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.01352)

Skywork-Reward-V2, a suite of reward models trained on a large-scale, high-quality preference dataset, achieves state-of-the-art performance across various benchmarks by leveraging human-AI synergistic curation.

### 6. Heeding the Inner Voice: Aligning ControlNet Training via Intermediate
  Features Feedback

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02321)

InnerControl enhances text-to-image diffusion models by enforcing spatial consistency across all diffusion steps using lightweight convolutional probes, improving control fidelity and generation quality.

### 7. IntFold: A Controllable Foundation Model for General and Specialized
  Biomolecular Structure Prediction

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02025)

IntFold, a foundation model with a customized attention kernel, achieves accuracy comparable to AlphaFold3 and can predict various biomolecular structures and binding affinities using adapters and a confidence head for docking quality.

### 8. Decoupled Planning and Execution: A Hierarchical Reasoning Framework for
  Deep Search

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02652)

A hierarchical framework separates strategic planning from specialized execution in deep search tasks, improving answer quality and system efficiency over traditional RAG and agent-based systems.

### 9. Fast and Simplex: 2-Simplicial Attention in Triton

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02754)

The 2-simplicial Transformer improves token efficiency over standard Transformers, offering better performance on knowledge and reasoning tasks with a fixed token budget.

### 10. Can LLMs Identify Critical Limitations within Scientific Research? A
  Systematic Evaluation on AI Research Papers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.02694)

A benchmark named LimitGen evaluates LLMs' ability to identify limitations in scientific papers, using synthetic and human-written datasets, and enhances their feedback with literature retrieval.

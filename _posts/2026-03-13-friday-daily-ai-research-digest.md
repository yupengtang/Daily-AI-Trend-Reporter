### 1. Spatial-TTT: Streaming Visual-based Spatial Intelligence with Test-Time Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12255)

Spatial-TTT enables streaming visual-based spatial intelligence through test-time training that adapts parameters to capture spatial evidence over long video sequences using hybrid architecture and 3D spatiotemporal convolution.

### 2. Strategic Navigation or Stochastic Search? How Agents and Humans Reason Over Document Collections

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12180)

MADQA benchmark evaluates multimodal agents' strategic reasoning capabilities through diverse PDF document questions, revealing gaps between human-level accuracy and efficient reasoning performance.

### 3. IndexCache: Accelerating Sparse Attention via Cross-Layer Index Reuse

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12201)

IndexCache reduces sparse attention computation in large language models by reusing top-k token selections across layers, achieving significant speedups with minimal quality loss.

### 4. Video-Based Reward Modeling for Computer-Use Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.10178)

Video-execution reward modeling enables scalable evaluation of computer-using agents by predicting task success from user instructions and execution videos, outperforming proprietary models across multiple operating systems.

### 5. XSkill: Continual Learning from Experience and Skills in Multimodal Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12056)

Multimodal agents using XSkill framework achieve improved continual learning through dual-stream knowledge extraction and retrieval from visual observations, enhancing tool use efficiency and reasoning capabilities.

### 6. ShotVerse: Advancing Cinematic Camera Control for Text-Driven Multi-Shot Video Creation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.11421)

ShotVerse enables cinematic video generation through a plan-then-control framework that decouples textual planning from camera control using vision-language models and trajectory alignment.

### 7. DreamVideo-Omni: Omni-Motion Controlled Multi-Subject Video Customization with Latent Identity Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12257)

DreamVideo-Omni is a unified framework for video synthesis that enables precise multi-subject identity control and multi-granularity motion manipulation through a two-stage training approach combining condition-aware 3D positional embeddings, hierarchical motion injection, and latent identity reward learning.

### 8. DVD: Deterministic Video Depth Estimation with Generative Priors

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12250)

Video depth estimation framework DVD adapts pre-trained video diffusion models into deterministic single-pass depth regressors using structural anchors, latent manifold rectification, and global affine coherence for improved accuracy and efficiency.

### 9. WeEdit: A Dataset, Benchmark and Glyph-Guided Framework for Text-centric Image Editing

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.11593)

WeEdit presents a systematic approach for text-centric image editing with a scalable data pipeline, multi-language benchmarks, and a two-stage training strategy combining supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning.

### 10. Trust Your Critic: Robust Reward Modeling and Reinforcement Learning for Faithful Image Editing and Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12247)

Reinforcement learning framework with novel reward modeling and benchmarking approaches improves fidelity and instruction adherence in image editing and text-to-image generation.

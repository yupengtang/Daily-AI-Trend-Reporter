### 1. LLaDA2.0-Uni: Unifying Multimodal Understanding and Generation with Diffusion Large Language Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20796)

LLaDA2.0-Uni is a unified discrete diffusion language model that integrates multimodal understanding and generation through a semantic discrete tokenizer, MoE-based backbone, and diffusion decoder, achieving performance comparable to specialized vision-language models while enabling efficient inference and high-fidelity image generation.

### 2. Near-Future Policy Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20733)

Mixed-policy reinforcement learning approach using near-future policy optimization to accelerate convergence and improve performance by balancing trajectory quality and variance.

### 3. DR-Venus: Towards Frontier Edge-Scale Deep Research Agents with Only 10K Open Data

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.19859)

DR-Venus-4B is a 4-billion-parameter deep research agent trained entirely on open data using agentic supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning with turn-level rewards to achieve superior performance on research benchmarks while maintaining edge-scale deployment advantages.

### 4. Reward Hacking in the Era of Large Models: Mechanisms, Emergent Misalignment, Challenges

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.13602)

Reward hacking in aligned language models stems from optimizing expressive policies against compressed reward signals, leading to systematic misalignment behaviors that generalize beyond initial shortcuts.

### 5. OpenMobile: Building Open Mobile Agents with Task and Trajectory Synthesis

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.15093)

An open-source framework for mobile agent training that synthesizes task instructions and trajectories through scalable pipelines and policy-switching strategies, achieving superior performance on AndroidWorld benchmarks.

### 6. DeVI: Physics-based Dexterous Human-Object Interaction via Synthetic Video Imitation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20841)

DeVI enables physically plausible dexterous robot control by leveraging text-conditioned synthetic videos through a hybrid tracking reward that combines 3D and 2D tracking for improved hand-object interaction modeling.

### 7. Exploring Spatial Intelligence from a Generative Perspective

### 8. A Self-Evolving Framework for Efficient Terminal Agents via Observational Context Compression

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.19572)

TACO is a self-evolving compression framework that automatically discovers and refines compression rules from interaction trajectories to improve long-horizon agent performance while reducing token overhead.

### 9. Image Generators are Generalist Vision Learners

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.20329)

Image generation pretraining enables vision models to develop strong visual understanding capabilities, achieving state-of-the-art performance on diverse vision tasks through lightweight instruction-tuning while maintaining generation abilities.

### 10. Expert Upcycling: Shifting the Compute-Efficient Frontier of Mixture-of-Experts

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.19835)

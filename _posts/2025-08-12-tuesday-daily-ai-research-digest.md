### 1. ReasonRank: Empowering Passage Ranking with Strong Reasoning Ability

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.07050)

A reasoning-intensive reranker, ReasonRank, achieves state-of-the-art performance in passage ranking tasks by using synthesized training data and a two-stage post-training approach with reinforcement learning.

### 2. WideSearch: Benchmarking Agentic Broad Info-Seeking

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.07999)

WideSearch is a new benchmark evaluating the reliability of automated search agents in large-scale information collection tasks, revealing significant deficiencies in current systems.

### 3. A Comprehensive Survey of Self-Evolving AI Agents: A New Paradigm
  Bridging Foundation Models and Lifelong Agentic Systems

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.07407)

A survey of self-evolving AI agents that adapt to dynamic environments through automatic enhancement based on interaction data and feedback.

### 4. Omni-Effects: Unified and Spatially-Controllable Visual Effects
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.07981)

Omni-Effects is a unified framework that enables the generation of prompt-guided and spatially controllable composite visual effects using LoRA-based Mixture of Experts and Spatial-Aware Prompt with Independent-Information Flow.

### 5. Part I: Tricks or Traps? A Deep Dive into RL for LLM Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.08221)

A systematic review of reinforcement learning techniques for large language model reasoning reveals clear guidelines and demonstrates that a minimalist combination of techniques can improve performance over existing strategies.

### 6. SONAR-LLM: Autoregressive Transformer that Thinks in Sentence Embeddings
  and Speaks in Tokens

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.05305)

SONAR-LLM, a decoder-only transformer using token-level cross-entropy in the SONAR embedding space, achieves competitive text generation quality without diffusion sampling.

### 7. MolmoAct: Action Reasoning Models that can Reason in Space

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.07917)

Action Reasoning Models (ARMs) integrate perception, planning, and control to enable adaptable and explainable robotic behavior, achieving superior performance across various tasks and settings.

### 8. Klear-Reasoner: Advancing Reasoning Capability via Gradient-Preserving
  Clipping Policy Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.07629)

Klear-Reasoner, a model with long reasoning capabilities, achieves high performance across benchmarks through detailed post-training workflows, including long Chain-of-Thought supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning with Gradient-Preserving clipping Policy Optimization.

### 9. BrowseComp-Plus: A More Fair and Transparent Evaluation Benchmark of
  Deep-Research Agent

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.06600)

BrowseComp-Plus, a curated benchmark, enables controlled evaluation of deep research agents and retrieval methods, providing insights into their performance and effectiveness.

### 10. Reinforcement Learning in Vision: A Survey

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.08189)

This survey synthesizes recent advancements in visual reinforcement learning, covering policy optimization strategies, thematic pillars, and evaluation protocols, while highlighting open challenges.

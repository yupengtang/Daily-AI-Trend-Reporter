### 1. Sekai: A Video Dataset towards World Exploration

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15675)

Sekai, a worldwide video dataset with comprehensive annotations, is introduced to support world exploration applications, enhancing video generation models.

### 2. GenRecal: Generation after Recalibration from Large to Small
  Vision-Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15681)

GenRecal, a novel distillation framework, improves performance of vision-language models by aligning feature representations across different architectures.

### 3. All is Not Lost: LLM Recovery without Checkpoints

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15461)

A novel method, CheckFree, and its extended version CheckFree+, efficiently recover from node failures during LLM training by substituting failed stages with averaged neighboring stages or through out-of-order pipeline execution, improving convergence time over existing checkpointing methods.

### 4. ProtoReasoning: Prototypes as the Foundation for Generalizable Reasoning
  in LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15211)

ProtoReasoning enhances large reasoning models through prototypical representations, leading to improved cross-domain generalization in logical reasoning, planning, and other tasks.

### 5. Embodied Web Agents: Bridging Physical-Digital Realms for Integrated
  Agent Intelligence

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15677)

Embodied Web Agents integrate physical interaction and web-scale reasoning to assess cross-domain intelligence in a novel benchmark environment.

### 6. SwarmAgentic: Towards Fully Automated Agentic System Generation via
  Swarm Intelligence

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15672)

SwarmAgentic is a framework for automated agentic system generation that optimize agent functionality and collaboration through language-driven exploration, outperforming existing baselines in unconstrained tasks.

### 7. BUT System for the MLC-SLM Challenge

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.13414)

The combined DiCoW and DiariZen ASR system demonstrates strong performance in multilingual scenarios, with DiCoW preserving its multilingual capabilities and DiariZen improving through fine-tuning.

### 8. Semantically-Aware Rewards for Open-Ended R1 Training in Free-Form
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15068)

PrefBERT, a scoring model, improves open-ended long-form generation by providing better semantic reward feedback than traditional metrics.

### 9. SciVer: Evaluating Foundation Models for Multimodal Scientific Claim
  Verification

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15569)

A benchmark named SciVer evaluates multimodal foundation models' claim verification capabilities within scientific contexts, revealing performance gaps and limitations in current models.

### 10. Truncated Proximal Policy Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.15050)

T-PPO, an extension of PPO, improves training efficiency for Large Language Models by optimizing policy updates and utilizing hardware resources more effectively.

### 1. Ovis-U1 Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23044)

Ovis-U1, a 3-billion-parameter unified model, integrates multimodal understanding, text-to-image generation, and image editing using a diffusion-based visual decoder and bidirectional token refiner, achieving state-of-the-art performance across various benchmarks.

### 2. SPIRAL: Self-Play on Zero-Sum Games Incentivizes Reasoning via
  Multi-Agent Multi-Turn Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.24119)

Self-play in zero-sum games using SPIRAL framework enhances reasoning capabilities in language models through continuous adaptation and transfer learning.

### 3. Calligrapher: Freestyle Text Image Customization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.24123)

Calligrapher, a diffusion-based framework, integrates advanced text customization with artistic typography using self-distillation, a localized style injection framework, and in-context generation to achieve high-quality, visually consistent typography.

### 4. VMoBA: Mixture-of-Block Attention for Video Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23858)

VMoBA, a novel sparse attention mechanism, enhances Video Diffusion Models by improving efficiency and quality in generating long-duration, high-resolution videos.

### 5. MEMFOF: High-Resolution Training for Memory-Efficient Multi-Frame
  Optical Flow Estimation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23151)

MEMFOF is a memory-efficient multi-frame optical flow method that achieves state-of-the-art performance with reduced GPU memory usage for high-resolution inputs.

### 6. Listener-Rewarded Thinking in VLMs for Image Preferences

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.22832)

A listener-augmented GRPO framework improves the accuracy and generalization of reward models for aligning text-to-image and text-to-video generative models with human preferences.

### 7. Evolving Prompts In-Context: An Open-ended, Self-replicating Perspective

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.17930)

A novel prompt optimization framework, PromptQuine, improves LLM performance by pruning random demonstrations into effective prompts using evolutionary search in low-data regimes.

### 8. SparseLoRA: Accelerating LLM Fine-Tuning with Contextual Sparsity

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.16500)

SparseLoRA reduces computational cost and speeds up fine-tuning of LLMs by dynamically selecting a sparse subset of weights for loss and gradient computation.

### 9. Consistent Time-of-Flight Depth Denoising via Graph-Informed Geometric
  Attention

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.23542)

A ToF depth denoising network using motion-invariant graph fusion enhances temporal stability and spatial sharpness, achieving state-of-the-art performance on synthetic and real datasets.

### 10. MARBLE: A Hard Benchmark for Multimodal Spatial Reasoning and Planning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.22992)

MARBLE is a challenging multimodal reasoning benchmark that tests the step-by-step reasoning capabilities of multimodal language models across complex tasks, highlighting their limitations in perception and reasoning.

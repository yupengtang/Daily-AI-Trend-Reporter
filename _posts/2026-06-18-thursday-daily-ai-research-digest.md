### 1. Beyond the Current Observation: Evaluating Multimodal Large Language Models in Controllable Non-Markov Games

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.19338)

A new benchmark suite called RNG-Bench is introduced to evaluate multimodal foundation models' ability to reconstruct past observations and use them for decision-making in multi-step interactions, featuring two games with controlled difficulty parameters and a memory gap metric to distinguish forgetting from poor decision-making.

### 2. Kairos: A Native World Model Stack for Physical AI

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.16533)

Kairos is a native world model framework that learns from diverse experiences, maintains persistent states through hybrid temporal attention, and supports efficient deployment for physical AI applications.

### 3. Guava: An Effective and Universal Harness for Embodied Manipulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.18363)

A harness framework for embodied tool use combines high-level reasoning with external modules, enabling compact models to perform complex manipulation tasks with minimal training data.

### 4. EfficientRollout: System-Aware Self-Speculative Decoding for RL Rollouts

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.18967)

EfficientRollout is a system-aware self-speculative decoding framework that accelerates reinforcement learning rollouts by adapting drafters to evolving policies and optimizing speculative decoding regimes.

### 5. SAE Interventions are Unreliable: Post-Intervention Recovery of Suppressed Behavior

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.18322)

Sparse Autoencoders' feature-level interventions may appear successful but can be circumvented through residual-space optimization that recovers original behaviors, revealing limitations in using SAE features for complete behavioral control.

### 6. Reinforcing Dual-Path Reasoning in Spatial Vision Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.17539)

A unified framework for spatial vision-language models that combines linguistic deduction and 3D geometric reasoning through reinforcement learning, enabling robust spatial reasoning across diverse tasks and domains.

### 7. Trust the Right Teacher: Quality-Aware Self-Distillation for GUI Grounding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.18101)

Quality-aware self-distillation improves vision-language model performance for GUI grounding by enhancing coordinate-token teacher signals through correctness-aware gating and probability scaling.

### 8. From Trainee to Trainer: LLM-Designed Training Environment for RL with Multi-Agent Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.17682)

A framework automates environment redesign in reinforcement learning for large language models by having the policy analyze failures and suggest configuration changes, achieving superior performance over larger proprietary models and fixed-environment baselines.

### 9. Native Active Perception as Reasoning for Omni-Modal Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.19341)

OmniAgent is a novel omni-modal agent that addresses long video understanding by using an iterative observation-thought-action cycle with active perception, achieving superior performance compared to larger models through efficient selective processing.

### 10. STARE: Surprisal-Guided Token-Level Advantage Reweighting for Policy Entropy Stability

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.19236)

GRPO algorithms face policy entropy collapse during training, which STARE addresses through surprisal-guided token-level advantage reweighting and target-entropy regulation to maintain stable reinforcement learning for large language models.

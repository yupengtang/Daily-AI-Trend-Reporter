### 1. DeepSearch: Overcome the Bottleneck of Reinforcement Learning with
  Verifiable Rewards via Monte Carlo Tree Search

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.25454)

DeepSearch integrates Monte Carlo Tree Search into RLVR training to enhance exploration and credit assignment, achieving state-of-the-art performance with reduced computational cost.

### 2. GEM: A Gym for Agentic LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.01051)

GEM, an open-source environment simulator, facilitates experience-based learning for large language models by providing a standardized framework and diverse environments for training and benchmarking reinforcement learning algorithms.

### 3. SINQ: Sinkhorn-Normalized Quantization for Calibration-Free
  Low-Precision LLM Weights

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.22944)

SINQ enhances post-training quantization by introducing a second-axis scale factor and Sinkhorn-Knopp-style algorithm to minimize matrix imbalance, improving perplexity on large language models.

### 4. VLA-RFT: Vision-Language-Action Reinforcement Fine-tuning with Verified
  Rewards in World Simulators

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.00406)

VLA-RFT uses a data-driven world model to fine-tune VLA models efficiently, reducing sample requirements and improving robustness under perturbations.

### 5. Knapsack RL: Unlocking Exploration of LLMs via Optimizing Budget
  Allocation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.25849)

An adaptive exploration budget allocation method for reinforcement learning in Large Language Models improves training efficiency and performance on mathematical reasoning benchmarks.

### 6. PIPer: On-Device Environment Setup via Online Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.25455)

A specialized model combining supervised fine-tuning and Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards achieves competitive performance in automated environment setup tasks.

### 7. Code2Video: A Code-centric Paradigm for Educational Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.01174)

Code2Video generates educational videos using a code-centric agent framework, improving coherence and interpretability compared to direct code generation.

### 8. ACON: Optimizing Context Compression for Long-horizon LLM Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.00615)

Agent Context Optimization (ACON) compresses context in large language models for efficient long-horizon tasks by analyzing failure cases and distilling the compressor into smaller models.

### 9. It Takes Two: Your GRPO Is Secretly DPO

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.00977)

Reframing Group Relative Policy Optimization as contrastive learning reveals its connection to Direct Preference Optimization, enabling minimal two-rollout GRPO to achieve performance comparable to larger group sizes with reduced computational cost.

### 10. BroRL: Scaling Reinforcement Learning via Broadened Exploration

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.01180)

BroRL enhances reinforcement learning by increasing rollouts per example, overcoming performance plateaus and achieving state-of-the-art results in large language models.

### 1. Efficient Reasoning with Balanced Thinking

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.12372)

ReBalance is a training-free framework that balances reasoning in large models by using confidence indicators to detect and correct overthinking and underthinking behaviors through dynamic steering vectors.

### 2. MetaClaw: Just Talk -- An Agent That Meta-Learns and Evolves in the Wild

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17187)

A continual meta-learning framework for large language model agents that jointly evolves policies and reusable behavioral skills while minimizing downtime through opportunistic updates and skill-driven adaptation.

### 3. Video-CoE: Reinforcing Video Event Prediction via Chain of Events

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.14935)

A new Chain of Events paradigm is introduced for video event prediction that improves temporal modeling and logical reasoning in multimodal language models through structured event chains and enhanced training protocols.

### 4. MosaicMem: Hybrid Spatial Memory for Controllable Video World Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17117)

Video diffusion models use hybrid spatial memory to maintain consistency under camera motion and enable long-term scene editing and navigation.

### 5. Alignment Makes Language Models Normative, Not Descriptive

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17218)

Post-training alignment of language models creates a trade-off between human-like behavior prediction and normative performance, with base models better predicting complex strategic interactions while aligned models excel in simple, rule-based scenarios.

### 6. Complementary Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17621)

Complementary RL enables efficient agent learning by synchronizing experience extraction with policy optimization through dual objectives that evolve together during training.

### 7. V-JEPA 2.1: Unlocking Dense Features in Video Self-Supervised Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.14482)

V-JEPA 2.1 is a self-supervised model that learns dense visual representations for images and videos through a combination of dense predictive loss, deep self-supervision, multi-modal tokenizers, and effective scaling.

### 8. LoST: Level of Semantics Tokenization for 3D Shapes

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17995)

Level-of-Semantics Tokenization (LoST) improves 3D shape generation by ordering tokens based on semantic salience and using a novel relational alignment loss for better reconstruction and efficiency.

### 9. When AI Navigates the Fog of War

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.16642)

Large language models demonstrate varying capabilities in reasoning about unfolding geopolitical conflicts, showing strategic realism in structured settings but inconsistent performance in complex political environments.

### 10. GigaWorld-Policy: An Efficient Action-Centered World--Action Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.17240)

GigaWorld-Policy introduces an action-centered World-Action Model that improves robotic policy learning by decoupling visual and motion representations, enabling faster inference and better task performance through dual supervision from action prediction and video generation.

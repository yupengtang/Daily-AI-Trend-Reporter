### 1. DeepAgent: A General Reasoning Agent with Scalable Toolsets

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.21618)

DeepAgent, an end-to-end deep reasoning agent, autonomously performs thinking, tool discovery, and action execution using memory folding and reinforcement learning, outperforming baselines in various tool-use and application tasks.

### 2. Video-As-Prompt: Unified Semantic Control for Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.20888)

Video-As-Prompt (VAP) uses a reference video to guide a frozen Video Diffusion Transformer via a Mixture-of-Transformers expert, achieving state-of-the-art results in semantic-controlled video generation with strong zero-shot generalization.

### 3. Reasoning with Sampling: Your Base Model is Smarter Than You Think

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.14901)

An iterative sampling algorithm enhances reasoning capabilities in base models without additional training, matching or outperforming reinforcement learning on single-shot tasks.

### 4. WorldGrow: Generating Infinite 3D World

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.21682)

WorldGrow, a hierarchical framework, generates large, continuous 3D environments with coherent geometry and realistic appearance using pre-trained 3D models and a coarse-to-fine generation strategy.

### 5. A Definition of AGI

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18212)

A quantifiable framework based on Cattell-Horn-Carroll theory evaluates AI systems across ten cognitive domains, revealing significant gaps in foundational cognitive abilities like long-term memory.

### 6. Sample By Step, Optimize By Chunk: Chunk-Level GRPO For Text-to-Image
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.21583)

Chunk-GRPO, a chunk-level optimization approach for text-to-image generation, improves preference alignment and image quality by addressing inaccurate advantage attribution and neglecting temporal dynamics.

### 7. From Denoising to Refining: A Corrective Framework for Vision-Language
  Diffusion Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19871)

ReDiff, a refining-enhanced diffusion framework, addresses train-inference discrepancies in discrete diffusion models by enabling the model to identify and correct its own errors, improving coherence and factual accuracy in generated content.

### 8. Sparser Block-Sparse Attention via Token Permutation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.21270)

Permuted Block-Sparse Attention improves computational efficiency in large language models by enhancing block-level sparsity in the self-attention mechanism, achieving significant speedups without compromising accuracy.

### 9. UI-Ins: Enhancing GUI Grounding with Multi-Perspective
  Instruction-as-Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.20286)

The Instruction-as-Reasoning paradigm enhances GUI grounding by treating instructions as dynamic pathways, improving performance through multi-perspective reasoning and reinforcement learning.

### 10. Visual Diffusion Models are Geometric Solvers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.21697)

Visual diffusion models can solve geometric problems by transforming noisy images into valid solutions, demonstrating a novel approach to geometric reasoning through image generation.

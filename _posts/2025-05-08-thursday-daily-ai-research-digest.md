### 1. Unified Multimodal Understanding and Generation Models: Advances,
  Challenges, and Opportunities

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02567)

A survey examines the integration of multimodal understanding and image generation models, categorizing them into diffusion-based, autoregressive-based, and hybrid approaches, and discussing challenges and future research directions.

### 2. ZeroSearch: Incentivize the Search Capability of LLMs without Searching

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04588)

ZeroSearch is a reinforcement learning framework that improves LLM search capabilities without using real search engines, using supervised fine-tuning and curriculum-based rollouts to handle document quality and API cost challenges.

### 3. HunyuanCustom: A Multimodal-Driven Architecture for Customized Video
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04512)

HunyuanCustom is a multi-modal video generation framework that enhances identity consistency and handles various types of input conditions through specific fusion and injection mechanisms, outperforming existing methods.

### 4. OpenVision: A Fully-Open, Cost-Effective Family of Advanced Vision
  Encoders for Multimodal Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04601)

OpenVision provides a fully-open, cost-effective family of vision encoders that matches or surpasses OpenAI's CLIP in multimodal frameworks, offering flexible trade-offs between model size and performance.

### 5. PrimitiveAnything: Human-Crafted 3D Primitive Assembly Generation with
  Auto-Regressive Transformer

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04622)

PrimitiveAnything is a novel framework for shape primitive abstraction that reformulates the task as primitive assembly generation using a shape-conditioned primitive transformer and achieves high-quality results across diverse shape categories.

### 6. R&B: Domain Regrouping and Data Mixture Balancing for Efficient
  Foundation Model Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.00358)

R&B, a framework that repartitions and balances training data based on semantic similarity and domain gradients, enhances language model performance with minimal additional computational cost.

### 7. Beyond Recognition: Evaluating Visual Perspective Taking in Vision
  Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.03821)

State-of-the-art Vision Language Models excel in scene understanding but struggle with spatial reasoning and visual perspective taking in controlled visual tasks.

### 8. Benchmarking LLMs' Swarm intelligence

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04364)

New benchmark evaluates LLMs in decentralized coordination tasks under limited information, highlighting challenges and potential for future systems.

### 9. Beyond Theorem Proving: Formulation, Framework and Benchmark for Formal
  Problem-Solving

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04528)

A new formal framework, FPS, for AI-based problem-solving is introduced, utilizing FTP environments and RPE for verification, with benchmarks and evaluation.

### 10. OmniGIRL: A Multilingual and Multimodal Benchmark for GitHub Issue
  Resolution

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04606)

OmniGIRL, a multilingual, multimodal, and multi-domain GitHub issue resolution benchmark, demonstrates that current LLMs perform poorly, particularly in issues involving images.

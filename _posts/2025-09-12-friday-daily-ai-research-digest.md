### 1. VLA-Adapter: An Effective Paradigm for Tiny-Scale Vision-Language-Action
  Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09372)

VLA-Adapter reduces reliance on large-scale VLMs and extensive pre-training by using a lightweight Policy module with Bridge Attention, achieving state-of-the-art performance and fast inference speed with minimal computational resources.

### 2. HuMo: Human-Centric Video Generation via Collaborative Multi-Modal
  Conditioning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.08519)

HuMo is a unified framework for human-centric video generation that addresses challenges in multimodal control through a two-stage training paradigm and novel strategies for subject preservation and audio-visual synchronization.

### 3. SimpleVLA-RL: Scaling VLA Training via Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09674)

SimpleVLA-RL, an RL framework for VLA models, enhances long-horizon action planning, achieves state-of-the-art performance, and discovers novel patterns during training.

### 4. MachineLearningLM: Continued Pretraining Language Models on Millions of
  Synthetic Tabular Prediction Tasks Scales In-Context ML

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.06806)

MachineLearningLM enhances a general-purpose LLM with robust in-context machine learning capabilities through continued pretraining with synthesized ML tasks, achieving high performance across various domains without task-specific training.

### 5. EchoX: Towards Mitigating Acoustic-Semantic Gap via Echo Training for
  Speech-to-Speech LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09174)

EchoX, a speech-to-speech large language model, addresses the acoustic-semantic gap by integrating semantic representations, preserving reasoning abilities, and achieving advanced performance on knowledge-based benchmarks.

### 6. Kling-Avatar: Grounding Multimodal Instructions for Cascaded
  Long-Duration Avatar Animation Synthesis

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09595)

Kling-Avatar, a cascaded framework, enhances audio-driven avatar video generation by integrating multimodal instruction understanding with photorealistic portrait generation, resulting in high-fidelity, semantically grounded videos.

### 7. Harnessing Uncertainty: Entropy-Modulated Policy Gradients for
  Long-Horizon LLM Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09265)

Entropy-Modulated Policy Gradients (EMPG) addresses learning dynamics issues in LLMs by recalibrating policy gradients based on uncertainty and task outcomes, leading to improved performance in long-horizon tasks.

### 8. FLUX-Reason-6M & PRISM-Bench: A Million-Scale Text-to-Image Reasoning
  Dataset and Comprehensive Benchmark

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09680)

FLUX-Reason-6M and PRISM-Bench address the lack of reasoning-focused datasets and benchmarks for text-to-image models, providing a large-scale dataset and evaluation standard to improve model performance.

### 9. SpatialVID: A Large-Scale Video Dataset with Spatial Annotations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09676)

SpatialVID, a large-scale dataset with diverse videos and dense 3D annotations, enhances model generalization and performance in video and 3D vision research.

### 10. Can Understanding and Generation Truly Benefit Together -- or Just
  Coexist?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.09666)

A novel framework UAE uses reinforcement learning to unify image-to-text and text-to-image processes, enhancing mutual understanding and generation fidelity.

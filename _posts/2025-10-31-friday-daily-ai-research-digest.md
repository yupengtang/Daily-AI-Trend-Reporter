### 1. Kimi Linear: An Expressive, Efficient Attention Architecture

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.26692)

Kimi Linear, a hybrid linear attention architecture, outperforms full attention in various scenarios with improved efficiency and performance, using Kimi Delta Attention and Multi-Head Latent Attention.

### 2. The End of Manual Decoding: Towards Truly End-to-End Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.26697)

AutoDeco, a novel architecture, enables end-to-end generation by dynamically predicting decoding parameters, outperforming default strategies and achieving performance close to an oracle-tuned baseline.

### 3. Emu3.5: Native Multimodal Models are World Learners

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.26583)

Emu3.5, a large-scale multimodal world model, predicts next states in vision and language, enhanced with reinforcement learning and Discrete Diffusion Adaptation for efficient inference, achieving strong performance in various multimodal tasks.

### 4. Supervised Reinforcement Learning: From Expert Trajectories to Step-wise
  Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.25992)

Supervised Reinforcement Learning (SRL) enhances small-scale LLMs' multi-step reasoning by generating internal monologues and using step-wise expert actions for richer learning signals, outperforming SFT and RLVR.

### 5. Can Agent Conquer Web? Exploring the Frontiers of ChatGPT Atlas Agent in
  Web Games

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.26298)

ChatGPT Atlas excels in logical reasoning tasks within web games but struggles in real-time games requiring precise timing and motor control.

### 6. Exploring Conditions for Diffusion models in Robotic Control

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15510)

ORCA uses learnable task and visual prompts to adapt pre-trained text-to-image diffusion models for robotic control, achieving state-of-the-art performance on benchmarks.

### 7. Surfer 2: The Next Generation of Cross-Platform Computer Use Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19949)

Surfer 2, a unified visual-based architecture, achieves state-of-the-art performance across web, desktop, and mobile environments without task-specific fine-tuning, demonstrating the potential of systematic orchestration for general-purpose computer control.

### 8. AMO-Bench: Large Language Models Still Struggle in High School Math
  Competitions

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.26768)

AMO-Bench is a benchmark for evaluating advanced mathematical reasoning in large language models with problems at the International Mathematical Olympiad level or higher, revealing significant room for improvement in current models.

### 9. Are Video Models Ready as Zero-Shot Reasoners? An Empirical Study with
  the MME-CoF Benchmark

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.26802)

Video models like Veo-3 show promise in short-term visual reasoning but struggle with long-term causal reasoning and abstract logic, indicating they are not yet reliable standalone zero-shot reasoners.

### 10. The Era of Agentic Organization: Learning to Organize with Language
  Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.26658)

AsyncThink, a new reasoning paradigm for large language models, improves inference efficiency and accuracy through concurrent and optimized sub-query processing.

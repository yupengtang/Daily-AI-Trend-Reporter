### 1. FlowRL: Matching Reward Distributions for LLM Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15207)

FlowRL enhances LLM reinforcement learning by matching the full reward distribution through flow balancing, improving diversity and performance over reward-maximizing methods.

### 2. ScaleCUA: Scaling Open-Source Computer Use Agents with Cross-Platform
  Data

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15221)

ScaleCUA, a large-scale dataset and model for computer use agents, achieves state-of-the-art performance across multiple platforms and tasks by leveraging data-driven scaling.

### 3. Reasoning over Boundaries: Enhancing Specification Alignment via
  Test-time Delibration

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.14760)

Align3, a lightweight method using Test-Time Deliberation, enhances specification alignment in large language models across diverse scenarios with minimal overhead.

### 4. AToken: A Unified Tokenizer for Vision

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.14476)

AToken, a unified visual tokenizer, achieves high-fidelity reconstruction and semantic understanding across images, videos, and 3D assets using a 4D transformer architecture with adversarial-free training.

### 5. Evolving Language Models without Labels: Majority Drives Selection,
  Novelty Promotes Variation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15194)

EVOL-RL, a label-free reinforcement learning method, enhances large language models by balancing stability and variation, preventing entropy collapse and improving generalization.

### 6. WorldForge: Unlocking Emergent 3D/4D Generation in Video Diffusion Model
  via Training-Free Guidance

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15130)

WorldForge, a training-free framework, enhances video diffusion models with precise motion control and photorealistic content generation through recursive refinement, flow-gated latent fusion, and dual-path self-corrective guidance.

### 7. FinSearchComp: Towards a Realistic, Expert-Level Evaluation of Financial
  Search and Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.13160)

FinSearchComp is an open-source benchmark for evaluating financial search and reasoning capabilities of end-to-end agents, featuring realistic tasks and professional annotations.

### 8. Understand Before You Generate: Self-Guided Training for Autoregressive
  Image Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15185)

Self-guided Training for AutoRegressive models (ST-AR) enhances image understanding and generation quality in autoregressive models by addressing key visual semantics challenges through self-supervised objectives.

### 9. RynnVLA-001: Using Human Demonstrations to Improve Robot Manipulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15212)

RynnVLA-001, a vision-language-action model, uses a two-stage pretraining approach and ActionVAE to achieve superior performance on robotics tasks.

### 10. Apertus: Democratizing Open and Compliant LLMs for Global Language
  Environments

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.14233)

Apertus is a suite of open large language models that ensure data compliance and multilingual representation through ethical data sourcing, the Goldfish objective, and comprehensive artifact release.

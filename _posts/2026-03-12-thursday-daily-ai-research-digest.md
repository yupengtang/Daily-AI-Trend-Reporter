### 2. OpenClaw-RL: Train Any Agent Simply by Talking

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.10165)

OpenClaw-RL framework enables policy learning from diverse next-state signals across multiple interaction modalities using asynchronous training with PRM judges and hindsight-guided distillation.

### 3. Flash-KMeans: Fast and Memory-Efficient Exact K-Means

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.09229)

Flash-kmeans enables efficient online k-means clustering on GPUs through novel kernel-level optimizations that eliminate I/O bottlenecks and reduce atomic write contention.

### 4. LLM2Vec-Gen: Generative Embeddings from Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.10913)

LLM2Vec-Gen introduces a self-supervised method for text embedding that represents model responses through trainable special tokens, achieving superior performance on MTEB while reducing harmful content and improving reasoning capabilities.

### 5. In-Context Reinforcement Learning for Tool Use in Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.08068)

In-Context Reinforcement Learning (ICRL) enables large language models to effectively use external tools through a reinforcement learning-only framework that eliminates the need for costly supervised fine-tuning by gradually reducing in-context examples during training.

### 6. MA-EgoQA: Question Answering over Egocentric Videos from Multiple Embodied Agents

### 7. ReMix: Reinforcement routing for mixtures of LoRAs in LLM finetuning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.10160)

Researchers address imbalance in routing weights of Mixture-of-LoRAs models by proposing Reinforcement Routing (ReMix), which uses non-learnable weights and reinforcement learning techniques to improve model expressiveness and performance.

### 8. ID-LoRA: Identity-Driven Audio-Video Personalization with In-Context LoRA

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.10256)

ID-LoRA enables joint generation of visual appearance and voice using a single model, improving personalization through identity-driven in-context LoRA adaptation and identity guidance techniques.

### 9. Can Large Language Models Keep Up? Benchmarking Online Adaptation to Continual Knowledge Streams

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.07392)

Online Adaptation to Continual Knowledge Streams (OAKS) benchmark evaluates how well language models adapt to dynamically changing information in real-time contexts through streaming datasets with evolving facts.

### 10. V_{0.5}: Generalist Value Model as a Prior for Sparse RL Rollouts

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.10848)

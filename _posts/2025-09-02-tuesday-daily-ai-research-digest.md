### 1. PVPO: Pre-Estimated Value-Based Policy Optimization for Agentic
  Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.21104)

PVPO, an enhanced critic-free reinforcement learning method, uses a reference model for pre-sampling and advantage estimation, improving efficiency and performance across various tasks and model scales.

### 2. T2R-bench: A Benchmark for Generating Article-Level Reports from Real
  World Industrial Tables

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.19813)

A bilingual benchmark named T2R-bench is proposed to evaluate the performance of large language models in generating reports from tables, highlighting the need for improvement in this task.

### 3. How Can Input Reformulation Improve Tool Usage Accuracy in a Complex
  Dynamic Environment? A Study on τ-bench

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.20931)

The IRMA framework improves the reliability and consistency of large language models in dynamic environments by reformulating user queries with domain rules and tool suggestions.

### 4. No Label Left Behind: A Unified Surface Defect Detection Model for all
  Supervision Regimes

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.19060)

SuperSimpleNet, an efficient and adaptable model based on SimpleNet, addresses diverse supervision scenarios in surface defect detection with high performance and low inference time.

### 5. From reactive to cognitive: brain-inspired spatial intelligence for
  embodied agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.17198)

BSC-Nav constructs allocentric cognitive maps from egocentric trajectories and contextual cues, enabling embodied agents to perform diverse navigation tasks with zero-shot generalization and versatile behaviors.

### 6. UI-Level Evaluation of ALLaM 34B: Measuring an Arabic-Centric LLM via
  HUMAIN Chat

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.17378)

The evaluation of ALLaM-34B, an Arabic-focused LLM, demonstrates high performance across various tasks including generation, code-switching, MSA handling, reasoning, dialect fidelity, and safety, positioning it as a robust and culturally grounded model.

### 7. Democracy-in-Silico: Institutional Design as Alignment in AI-Governed
  Polities

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.19562)

Agent-based simulation using advanced AI agents with psychological personas demonstrates that institutional design, including Constitutional AI and mediated deliberation, can align AI behavior and enhance public welfare.

### 1. UI-MOPD: Multi-Platform On-Policy Distillation for Continual GUI Agent Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.04425)

### 2. OmniOpt: Taxonomy, Geometry, and Benchmarking of Modern Optimizers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.04033)

OmniOpt presents a unified framework for optimizer selection in large-scale model training by combining meta-pipeline transformations, norm-constrained linear minimization oracles, and a cross-domain benchmark to systematically analyze optimizer families and their trade-offs across different training objectives and model scales.

### 3. PixWorld: Unifying 3D Scene Generation and Reconstruction in Pixel Space

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.05373)

PixWorld presents a unified pixel-space diffusion approach for 3D reconstruction and generation that overcomes limitations of latent-space methods through direct image-level supervision and geometry-aware feature alignment.

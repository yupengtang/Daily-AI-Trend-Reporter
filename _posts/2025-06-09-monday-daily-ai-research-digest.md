### 1. Will It Still Be True Tomorrow? Multilingual Evergreen Question
  Classification to Improve Trustworthy QA

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.21115)

EverGreenQA, a multilingual QA dataset with evergreen labels, is introduced to benchmark LLMs on temporality encoding and assess their performance through verbalized judgments and uncertainty signals.

### 2. PartCrafter: Structured 3D Mesh Generation via Compositional Latent
  Diffusion Transformers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.05573)

PartCrafter is a unified 3D generative model that synthesizes multiple semantically meaningful 3D meshes from a single image using a compositional latent space and hierarchical attention mechanism.

### 3. Leveraging Self-Attention for Input-Dependent Soft Prompting in LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.05629)

A new method using input-dependent soft prompting with a self-attention mechanism improves parameter-efficient fine-tuning for large language models, enhancing zero-shot domain transfer.

### 4. Truth in the Few: High-Value Data Selection for Efficient Multi-Modal
  Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.04755)

A new data selection paradigm, Reasoning Activation Potential (RAP), enhances multi-modal reasoning in large language models using minimal high-value datasets, improving performance and reducing computational costs.

### 5. MORSE-500: A Programmatically Controllable Video Benchmark to
  Stress-Test Multimodal Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.05523)

MORSE-500, a video benchmark with 500 scripted clips, evaluates multimodal reasoning across six categories, highlighting performance gaps in abstract and planning tasks.

### 6. FusionAudio-1.2M: Towards Fine-grained Audio Captioning with Multimodal
  Contextual Fusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.01111)

A novel two-stage pipeline using specialized pretrained models and a large language model enhances audio caption quality by integrating diverse multimodal cues and contextual information.

### 7. STARFlow: Scaling Latent Normalizing Flows for High-resolution Image
  Synthesis

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.06276)

STARFlow, a generative model combining normalizing flows with autoregressive Transformers, achieves competitive image synthesis performance with innovations in architecture and latent space modeling.

### 8. Is Extending Modality The Right Path Towards Omni-Modality?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.01872)

Research investigates the impact of extending modality and model merging on maintaining language abilities and generalization in omni-modal language models.

### 9. Sentinel: SOTA model to protect against prompt injections

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.05446)

Sentinel, a detection model based on ModernBERT-large, effectively identifies prompt injection attacks with high accuracy and outperforms existing baselines.

### 10. AssetOpsBench: Benchmarking AI Agents for Task Automation in Industrial
  Asset Operations and Maintenance

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.03828)

A unified framework, AssetOpsBench, is introduced to enable end-to-end automation of industrial asset lifecycle management through domain-specific AI agents.

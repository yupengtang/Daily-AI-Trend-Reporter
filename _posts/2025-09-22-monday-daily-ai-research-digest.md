### 1. RPG: A Repository Planning Graph for Unified and Scalable Codebase
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.16198)

A graph-driven framework called ZeroRepo uses the Repository Planning Graph (RPG) to generate complete software repositories from scratch, significantly outperforming existing baselines in terms of code size, functional coverage, and test pass rate.

### 2. MANZANO: A Simple and Scalable Unified Multimodal Model with a Hybrid
  Vision Tokenizer

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.16197)

Manzano is a unified multimodal LLM framework that integrates image and text processing using a hybrid tokenizer and diffusion decoder, achieving state-of-the-art performance in both understanding and generating visual content.

### 3. Latent Zoning Network: A Unified Principle for Generative Modeling,
  Representation Learning, and Classification

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15591)

Latent Zoning Network (LZN) unifies generative modeling, representation learning, and classification by creating a shared latent space for diverse data types.

### 4. SPATIALGEN: Layout-guided 3D Indoor Scene Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.14981)

SpatialGen, a multi-view multi-modal diffusion model, generates realistic and semantically consistent 3D indoor scenes using a large synthetic dataset, outperforming previous methods.

### 5. BaseReward: A Strong Baseline for Multimodal Reward Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.16127)

The paper provides a comprehensive guide and introduces BaseReward, a state-of-the-art multimodal reward model, which outperforms existing models across various benchmarks and real-world tasks.

### 6. A Vision-Language-Action-Critic Model for Robotic Real-World
  Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15937)

VLAC, a vision-language-action reward model, enhances real-world robotic reinforcement learning by providing dense rewards and enabling one-shot transfer, significantly improving success rates and sample efficiency.

### 7. BTL-UI: Blink-Think-Link Reasoning Model for GUI Agent

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15566)

A brain-inspired framework, Blink-Think-Link, enhances human-GUI interaction by mimicking cognitive processes and introduces innovations in data generation and reinforcement learning rewards.

### 8. Lynx: Towards High-Fidelity Personalized Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15496)

Lynx, a high-fidelity personalized video synthesis model, uses a Diffusion Transformer with ID-adapter and Ref-adapter to preserve identity and maintain video quality.

### 9. RGB-Only Supervised Camera Parameter Optimization in Dynamic Scenes

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.15123)

A novel method for camera parameter optimization in dynamic scenes using a single RGB video, incorporating patch-wise tracking filters, outlier-aware joint optimization, and a two-stage optimization strategy.

### 10. Do You Hear What I Mean? Quantifying the Instruction-Perception Gap in
  Instruction-Guided Expressive Text-To-Speech Systems

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.13989)

Research on ITTS reveals gaps between user instructions and listener perception, highlighting challenges in fine-grained control and voice attribute interpretation.

### 1. A Survey of Context Engineering for Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.13334)

Context Engineering systematically optimizes information payloads for Large Language Models, addressing gaps in generating sophisticated, long-form outputs.

### 2. VisionThink: Smart and Efficient Vision Language Model via Reinforcement
  Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.13348)

VisionThink dynamically adjusts image resolution and visual token processing for efficient and effective vision-language tasks, improving performance and reducing computational cost.

### 3. π^3: Scalable Permutation-Equivariant Visual Geometry Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.13347)

A permutation-equivariant neural network, $\pi^3$, reconstructs visual geometry without a fixed reference view, achieving state-of-the-art performance in camera pose estimation, depth estimation, and point map reconstruction.

### 4. Diffuman4D: 4D Consistent Human View Synthesis from Sparse-View Videos
  with Spatio-Temporal Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.13344)

A sliding iterative denoising process enhances spatio-temporal consistency in 4D diffusion models for high-fidelity view synthesis from sparse-view videos.

### 5. The Imitation Game: Turing Machine Imitator is Length Generalizable
  Reasoner

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.13332)

TAIL, a method that imitates Turing Machine execution processes, enhances the length generalization and performance of LLMs by synthesizing chain-of-thought data and reducing shortcut learning.

### 6. AnyCap Project: A Unified Framework, Dataset, and Benchmark for
  Controllable Omni-modal Captioning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12841)

The AnyCap Project introduces a framework, dataset, and evaluation protocol to enhance controllability and reliability in multimodal captioning.

### 7. RiemannLoRA: A Unified Riemannian Framework for Ambiguity-Free LoRA
  Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12142)

RiemannLoRA addresses initialization and overparametrization in LoRA by treating LoRA matrices as a smooth manifold, improving convergence speed and performance in LLMs and diffusion models.

### 8. Voxtral

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.13264)

Voxtral Mini and Voxtral Small are multimodal audio chat models that excel in understanding spoken audio and text, with a 32K context window for extended audio and conversation handling.

### 9. MindJourney: Test-Time Scaling with World Models for Spatial Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12508)

MindJourney enhances vision-language models with 3D reasoning by coupling them with a video diffusion-based world model, achieving improved performance on spatial reasoning tasks without fine-tuning.

### 10. FantasyPortrait: Enhancing Multi-Character Portrait Animation with
  Expression-Augmented Diffusion Transformers

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12956)

FantasyPortrait, a diffusion transformer framework, generates high-fidelity and emotion-rich facial animations for single and multi-character scenarios using implicit representations and a masked cross-attention mechanism.

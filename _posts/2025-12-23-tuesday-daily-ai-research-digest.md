### 1. DataFlow: An LLM-Driven Framework for Unified Data Preparation and Workflow Automation in the Era of Data-Centric AI

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.16676)

DataFlow is an LLM-driven data preparation framework that enhances data quality and reproducibility for various tasks, improving LLM performance with automatically generated pipelines.

### 2. The Prism Hypothesis: Harmonizing Semantic and Pixel Representations via Unified Autoencoding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.19693)

Unified Autoencoding combines semantic and pixel-level information through a frequency-band modulator, resulting in a latent space with state-of-the-art performance on image benchmarks.

### 3. Region-Constraint In-Context Generation for Instructional Video Editing

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.17650)

ReCo is a novel instructional video editing paradigm that enhances accuracy and reduces token interference by incorporating constraint modeling and regularization techniques during in-context generation.

### 4. WorldWarp: Propagating 3D Geometry with Asynchronous Video Diffusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.19678)

WorldWarp addresses the challenge of generating consistent long-range videos by integrating a 3D geometric cache with a spatio-temporal diffusion model, ensuring structural consistency and textural refinement.

### 5. QuCo-RAG: Quantifying Uncertainty from the Pre-training Corpus for Dynamic Retrieval-Augmented Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.19134)

QuCo-RAG uses objective corpus statistics to mitigate hallucinations in large language models during generation, improving accuracy across various benchmarks.

### 6. Infinite-Homography as Robust Conditioning for Camera-Controlled Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.17040)

InfCam generates high-fidelity videos with accurate camera poses by using infinite homography warping and augmenting synthetic datasets with diverse trajectories.

### 7. LoGoPlanner: Localization Grounded Navigation Policy with Metric-aware Visual Geometry

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.19629)

LoGoPlanner is an end-to-end navigation framework that improves trajectory planning in unstructured environments by integrating localization, scene geometry reconstruction, and policy conditioning.

### 8. Can LLMs Estimate Student Struggles? Human-AI Difficulty Alignment with Proficiency Simulation for Item Difficulty Prediction

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.18880)

Large Language Models struggle to accurately estimate human cognitive difficulty due to a misalignment with human perceptions and a lack of introspection regarding their own limitations.

### 9. Is There a Better Source Distribution than Gaussian? Exploring Source Distributions for Image Flow Matching

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.18184)

A novel framework for flow matching combines norm-aligned training with directionally-pruned sampling to improve generation quality and sampling efficiency by addressing mode discrepancy and path entanglement.

### 10. Reasoning Palette: Modulating Reasoning via Latent Contextualization for Controllable Exploration for (V)LMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.17206)

Reasoning Palette enhances large language models by using a latent-modulation framework to guide internal planning and improve both inference and reinforcement learning performance.

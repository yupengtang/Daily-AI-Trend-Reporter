### 1. Penguin-VL: Exploring the Efficiency Limits of VLM with LLM-based Vision Encoders

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.06569)

Penguin-VL demonstrates that text-only initialized vision encoders can achieve superior performance in multimodal understanding tasks compared to traditional contrastive pretraining methods, enabling efficient deployment on resource-constrained devices.

### 2. BandPO: Bridging Trust Regions and Ratio Clipping via Probability-Aware Bounds for LLM Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.04918)

Band-constrained Policy Optimization addresses stability issues in reinforcement learning for large language models by replacing fixed clipping with a dynamic probability-aware projection method that prevents entropy collapse.

### 3. Planning in 8 Tokens: A Compact Discrete Tokenizer for Latent World Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.05438)

CompACT, a discrete tokenizer that reduces observation encoding from hundreds to 8 tokens, enables faster and more efficient world model planning for real-time control applications.

### 4. Reasoning Models Struggle to Control their Chains of Thought

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.05706)

Chain-of-thought controllability measures how effectively models can be constrained to follow reasoning steps, with findings showing significantly lower controllability in reasoning versus output generation, and varying impacts from model size, training methods, and task complexity.

### 5. WildActor: Unconstrained Identity-Preserving Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.00586)

WildActor generates consistent human videos with full-body identity preservation across varying viewpoints and motions using a large-scale dataset and novel attention mechanisms.

### 6. Progressive Residual Warmup for Language Model Pretraining

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.05369)

Progressive Residual Warmup (ProRes) improves transformer language model pretraining by implementing gradual layer activation that stabilizes training and accelerates convergence.

### 7. RoboMME: Benchmarking and Understanding Memory for Robotic Generalist Policies

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.04639)

RoboMME presents a large-scale standardized benchmark for evaluating vision-language-action models in long-horizon robotic manipulation tasks requiring memory mechanisms.

### 8. Dynamic Chunking Diffusion Transformer

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.06351)

Dynamic Chunking Diffusion Transformer adapts token sequence length based on image content and diffusion timestep, improving efficiency and performance over fixed-token approaches.

### 9. Physical Simulator In-the-Loop Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.06408)

PSIVG integrates a physical simulator into video diffusion processes to generate physically consistent videos while maintaining visual quality and diversity.

### 10. Mario: Multimodal Graph Reasoning with Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.05181)

Mario is a unified framework that enables large language model-based reasoning on multimodal graphs by addressing cross-modal consistency and heterogeneous modality preferences through graph-conditioned vision-language modeling and modality-adaptive instruction tuning.

### 1. General Agentic Memory Via Deep Research

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.18423)

GAM, a novel framework that employs JIT compilation principles, improves memory efficiency and task completion by leveraging a lightweight memorizer and researcher in conjunction with reinforcement learning.

### 2. AutoEnv: Automated Environments for Measuring Cross-Environment Agent Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.19304)

AutoEnv and AutoEnv-36 provide a standardized framework and dataset for evaluating cross-environment learning in agents, highlighting the challenges and limitations of existing learning methods.

### 3. DeCo: Frequency-Decoupled Pixel Diffusion for End-to-End Image Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.19365)

The frequency-DeCoupled pixel diffusion framework improves image generation efficiency and quality by separating high-frequency details and low-frequency semantics, achieving superior performance compared to existing pixel diffusion models.

### 4. DR Tulu: Reinforcement Learning with Evolving Rubrics for Deep Research

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.19399)

Reinforcement Learning with Evolving Rubrics (RLER) enables training of deep research models for long-form tasks, outperforming existing models and proprietary systems while being more cost-effective.

### 5. Computer-Use Agents as Judges for Generative User Interface

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.15567)

A framework leveraging Computer-Use Agents as judges to assist coding-oriented language models in designing efficient and functional GUIs.

### 6. UltraFlux: Data-Model Co-Design for High-quality Native 4K Text-to-Image Generation across Diverse Aspect Ratios

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.18050)

UltraFlux, a Flux-based DiT trained on a 4K dataset, addresses failures in diffusion transformers at 4K resolution through enhanced positional encoding, improved VAE compression, gradient rebalancing, and aesthetic curriculum learning, achieving superior performance compared to existing models.

### 7. Budget-Aware Tool-Use Enables Effective Agent Scaling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.17006)

Budget-aware methods improve the scaling of tool-augmented agents by providing continuous budget awareness and adaptive planning, leading to better cost-performance trade-offs.

### 8. In-Video Instructions: Visual Signals as Generative Control

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.19401)

Video generative models can interpret and execute visual instructions embedded within frames, enhancing controllability in image-to-video generation.

### 9. Chain-of-Visual-Thought: Teaching VLMs to See and Think Better with Continuous Visual Tokens

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.19418)

Chain-of-Visual-Thought (COVT) enables Vision-Language Models to reason through visual tokens, improving their performance on perceptual tasks by capturing dense visual information.

### 10. HunyuanVideo 1.5 Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.18870)

HunyuanVideo 1.5 is a lightweight video generation model with state-of-the-art visual quality and motion coherence, using a DiT architecture with SSTA and an efficient video super-resolution network.

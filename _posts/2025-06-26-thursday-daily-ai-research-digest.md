### 1. FineWeb2: One Pipeline to Scale Them All -- Adapting Pre-Training Data
  Processing to Every Language

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.20920)

A new pre-training dataset curation pipeline based on FineWeb supports multilingual LLMs by automatically adapting to any language, improving model performance and balancing dataset quality.

### 2. ShareGPT-4o-Image: Aligning Multimodal Models with GPT-4o-Level Image
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.18095)

ShareGPT-4o-Image and Janus-4o enable open research in photorealistic, instruction-aligned image generation through a large dataset and multimodal model.

### 3. OctoThinker: Mid-training Incentivizes Reinforcement Learning Scaling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.20512)

Investigating mid-training strategies reveals that high-quality mathematical corpora and well-formatted chain-of-thought reasoning examples enhance reinforcement learning performance in language models, leading to the development of OctoThinker.

### 4. Outlier-Safe Pre-Training for Robust 4-Bit Quantization of Large
  Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.19697)

Outlier-Safe Pre-Training improves large language model quantization performance by preventing extreme activation outliers through innovative training techniques.

### 5. Inverse-and-Edit: Effective and Fast Image Editing by Cycle Consistency
  Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.19103)

A new framework using consistency models enhances image inversion and editing efficiency, achieving top performance with fewer steps.

### 6. DualTHOR: A Dual-Arm Humanoid Simulation Platform for Contingency-Aware
  Planning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.16012)

A simulator named DualTHOR for training dual-arm humanoid robots integrates real-world assets and physics to enhance the robustness and generalization of Vision Language Models.

### 7. HiWave: Training-Free High-Resolution Image Generation via Wavelet-Based
  Diffusion Sampling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.20452)

HiWave enhances ultra-high-resolution image synthesis using pretrained diffusion models through a two-stage pipeline involving DDIM inversion and wavelet-based detail enhancement, improving visual fidelity and reducing artifacts.

### 8. RoboTwin 2.0: A Scalable Data Generator and Benchmark with Strong Domain
  Randomization for Robust Bimanual Robotic Manipulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.18088)

RoboTwin 2.0 is a scalable simulation framework for bimanual robotic manipulation that uses expert data synthesis and structured domain randomization to generate diverse and realistic synthetic data, improving sim-to-real transfer and generalization.

### 9. Thought Anchors: Which LLM Reasoning Steps Matter?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.19143)

Sentence-level attribution methods uncover critical thought anchors in large language models' reasoning processes, enhancing interpretability.

### 10. When Life Gives You Samples: The Benefits of Scaling up Inference
  Compute for Multilingual LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.20544)

The study examines and proposes new sampling and selection strategies to enhance inference-time compute for multilingual and multi-task large language models, demonstrating significant improvements in win-rates across various languages and tasks.

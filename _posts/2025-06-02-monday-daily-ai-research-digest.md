### 1. ProRL: Prolonged Reinforcement Learning Expands Reasoning Boundaries in
  Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24864)

Prolonged reinforcement learning training (ProRL) uncovers novel reasoning strategies in language models, outperforming base models and suggesting meaningful expansion of reasoning capabilities.

### 2. AlphaOne: Reasoning Models Thinking Slow and Fast at Test Time

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24863)

AlphaOne dynamically modulates reasoning in large models by introducing $\alpha$ moment and Bernoulli process for slow thinking, improving efficiency and capability across diverse domains.

### 3. Time Blindness: Why Video-Language Models Can't See What Humans Can?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24867)

SpookyBench is a benchmark for temporal pattern recognition in videos that highlights the limitations of vision-language models in processing noise-like frames without spatial information.

### 4. Large Language Models for Data Synthesis

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.14752)

LLMSynthor enhances LLMs for efficient and statistically accurate data synthesis through distributional feedback and proposal sampling.

### 5. HardTests: Synthesizing High-Quality Test Cases for LLM Coding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24098)

HARDTESTGEN creates a large, high-quality competitive programming dataset to enhance the precision and recall of verifiers in evaluating LLM-generated code.

### 6. Don't Look Only Once: Towards Multimodal Interactive Reasoning with
  Selective Visual Revisitation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.18842)

v1 enhances Multimodal Large Language Models by enabling selective and dynamic visual region retrieval during inference, improving performance on multimodal reasoning tasks.

### 7. ViStoryBench: Comprehensive Benchmark Suite for Story Visualization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24862)

ViStoryBench is a comprehensive evaluation benchmark for story visualization frameworks, featuring diverse datasets and metrics to assess model performance across narrative and visual dimensions.

### 8. DINO-R1: Incentivizing Reasoning Capability in Vision Foundation Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24025)

DINO-R1 incorporates reinforcement learning to enhance visual in-context reasoning capabilities in vision foundation models, achieving better performance than supervised fine-tuning across various visual prompting scenarios.

### 9. EXP-Bench: Can AI Conduct AI Research Experiments?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24785)

EXP-Bench evaluates AI agents' end-to-end research experiment capabilities through curated tasks from top AI papers, highlighting current limitations.

### 10. Open CaptchaWorld: A Comprehensive Web-based Platform for Testing and

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24878)

### 1. Z-Image: An Efficient Image Generation Foundation Model with Single-Stream Diffusion Transformer

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.22699)

Z-Image, a 6B-parameter Scalable Single-Stream Diffusion Transformer (S3-DiT) model, achieves high-performance image generation with reduced computational cost, offering sub-second inference and compatibility with consumer hardware.

### 2. DeepSeekMath-V2: Towards Self-Verifiable Mathematical Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.22570)

A self-verifying large language model for theorem proving improves mathematical reasoning by incentivizing rigorous step-by-step derivations and achieves high scores in international competitions.

### 3. REASONEDIT: Towards Reasoning-Enhanced Image Editing Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.22625)

Integrating reasoning mechanisms into image editing models enhances performance by improving instruction understanding and result correction.

### 4. Vision Bridge Transformer at Scale

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.23199)

Bridge Models, instantiated as Vision Bridge Transformer (ViBT), efficiently translate data through direct modeling of input-to-output trajectories, achieving robust performance in image and video editing tasks at large scales.

### 5. AnyTalker: Scaling Multi-Person Talking Video Generation with Interactivity Refinement

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.23475)

The proposed AnyTalker framework generates high-quality multi-person talking videos by extending Diffusion Transformer with identity-aware attention, leveraging single-person videos for training, and using a specialized dataset for evaluation.

### 6. Geometrically-Constrained Agent for Spatial Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.22659)

Geometrically-Constrained Agent (GCA) addresses the semantic-to-geometric gap in vision language models by decoupling semantic analysis and task solving with formal constraints, achieving state-of-the-art performance in spatial reasoning.

### 7. Nemotron-Flash: Towards Latency-Optimal Hybrid Small Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.18890)

The study identifies key architectural factors and efficient operators to optimize small language models for real-device latency, introducing the Nemotron-Flash family for improved accuracy and efficiency.

### 8. Decoupled DMD: CFG Augmentation as the Spear, Distribution Matching as the Shield

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.22677)

The study reveals that in text-to-image generation, CFG Augmentation is the primary driver of few-step distillation in Distribution Matching Distillation (DMD), while the distribution matching term acts as a regularizer.

### 9. Architecture Decoupling Is Not All You Need For Unified Multimodal Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.22663)

The proposed Attention Interaction Alignment (AIA) loss improves cross-modal attention and performance in unified multimodal models for image generation and understanding without decoupling.

### 10. CaptionQA: Is Your Caption as Useful as the Image Itself?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.21025)

CaptionQA evaluates caption utility by measuring their effectiveness in supporting downstream tasks, revealing significant gaps compared to traditional image-QA benchmarks.

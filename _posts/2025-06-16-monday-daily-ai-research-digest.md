### 1. Feedback Friction: LLMs Struggle to Fully Incorporate External Feedback

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.11930)

LLMs show resistance to feedback, termed feedback friction, even under ideal conditions, and sampling-based strategies only partially mitigate this issue.

### 2. Effective Red-Teaming of Policy-Adherent Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.09600)

CRAFT, a multi-agent system using policy-aware persuasive strategies, challenges policy-adherent LLM-based agents in customer service to assess and improve their robustness against adversarial attacks.

### 3. The Diffusion Duality

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10892)

Duo improves uniform-state discrete diffusion models by transferring techniques from Gaussian diffusion, enhancing training speed and enabling fast few-step text generation.

### 4. Aligned Novel View Image and Geometry Synthesis via Cross-modal
  Attention Instillation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.11924)

A diffusion-based framework generates aligned novel views of images and geometry using warping-and-inpainting with cross-modal attention distillation and proximity-based mesh conditioning, achieving high-fidelity synthesis and 3D completion.

### 5. LiveCodeBench Pro: How Do Olympiad Medalists Judge LLMs in Competitive
  Programming?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.11928)

LLMs perform well on implementation-heavy competitive programming problems but struggle with nuanced algorithmic reasoning, as highlighted by LiveCodeBench Pro.

### 6. ViCrit: A Verifiable Reinforcement Learning Proxy Task for Visual
  Perception in VLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10128)

ViCrit, an RL task for fine-tuning VLMs, improves visual perception by training models to detect subtle hallucinations in image captions, with gains transferable to various visual domains.

### 7. Dense Retrievers Can Fail on Simple Queries: Revealing The Granularity
  Dilemma of Embeddings

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.08592)

A new dataset named CapRetrieval is introduced to evaluate the ability of text encoders to recognize fine-grained entities and events, highlighting challenges in dense retrieval tasks.

### 8. Beyond Homogeneous Attention: Memory-Efficient LLMs via
  Fourier-Approximated KV Cache

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.11886)

FourierAttention is a training-free framework that enhances memory efficiency in Large Language Models by compressing long-context-insensitive transformer head dimensions using orthogonal Fourier bases, while maintaining accuracy.

### 9. Med-PRM: Medical Reasoning Models with Stepwise, Guideline-verified
  Process Rewards

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.11474)

Med-PRM enhances clinical decision making by verifying reasoning steps against medical knowledge bases, achieving state-of-the-art performance in medical QA benchmarks with improved accuracy.

### 10. SwS: Self-aware Weakness-driven Problem Synthesis in Reinforcement
  Learning for LLM Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.08989)

A self-aware problem synthesis framework that leverages model weaknesses enhances reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards, improving large language model performance on reasoning tasks.

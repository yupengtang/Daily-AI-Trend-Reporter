### 1. DeepAnalyze: Agentic Large Language Models for Autonomous Data Science

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.16872)

DeepAnalyze-8B, an agentic LLM, autonomously completes the data science pipeline from raw data to research reports using curriculum-based training and data-grounded trajectory synthesis.

### 2. FineVision: Open Data Is All You Need

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.17269)

FineVision, a large-scale and curated dataset, enhances vision-language models through rigorous data collection, de-duplication, and human oversight, leading to improved performance.

### 3. Glyph: Scaling Context Windows via Visual-Text Compression

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.17800)

Glyph compresses long textual inputs into images using vision-language models, achieving significant token compression and improved performance in long-context tasks.

### 4. PICABench: How Far Are We from Physically Realistic Image Editing?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.17681)

PICABench and PICAEval evaluate physical realism in image editing by assessing eight sub-dimensions and using VLM-as-a-judge with human annotations, highlighting the need for physics-based solutions.

### 5. RL makes MLLMs see better than SFT

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.16333)

Reinforcement Learning enhances vision encoders in Multimodal Language Models, leading to better visual representations and performance compared to Supervised Fine-tuning.

### 6. QueST: Incentivizing LLMs to Generate Difficult Problems

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.17715)

QueST, a framework combining difficulty-aware graph sampling and fine-tuning, generates large-scale synthetic coding problems to enhance the performance of large language models in competitive coding and reasoning tasks.

### 7. Towards Mixed-Modal Retrieval for Universal Retrieval-Augmented
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.17354)

Nyx, a unified mixed-modal retriever, enhances vision-language generation by retrieving and reasoning over mixed-modal data, outperforming existing RAG systems in real-world scenarios.

### 8. TrajSelector: Harnessing Latent Representations for Efficient and
  Effective Best-of-N in Large Reasoning Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.16449)

TrajSelector is an efficient Best-of-N framework that leverages hidden states for process-level scoring, improving LLM performance with lower computational costs.

### 9. When to Ensemble: Identifying Token-Level Points for Stable and Fast LLM
  Ensembling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15346)

SAFE, a selective ensembling framework for large language models, improves long-form generation by considering tokenization mismatch and consensus in probability distributions, leading to better accuracy and efficiency.

### 10. AION-1: Omnimodal Foundation Model for Astronomical Sciences

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.17960)

AION-1, a family of large-scale multimodal foundation models, integrates diverse astronomical data using tokenization and transformer-based modeling, achieving strong performance across various downstream tasks.

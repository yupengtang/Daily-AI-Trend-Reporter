### 1. VibeVoice Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.19205)

VibeVoice synthesizes long-form multi-speaker speech using next-token diffusion and a highly efficient continuous speech tokenizer, achieving superior performance and fidelity.

### 2. TreePO: Bridging the Gap of Policy Optimization and Efficacy and
  Inference Efficiency with Heuristic Tree-based Modeling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.17445)

TreePO, a self-guided rollout algorithm for sequence generation, reduces computational cost and enhances exploration diversity in reinforcement learning for large language models.

### 3. CMPhysBench: A Benchmark for Evaluating Large Language Models in
  Condensed Matter Physics

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.18124)

CMPhysBench evaluates LLMs in condensed matter physics using calculation problems and a new SEED score for partial credit assessment, revealing significant capability gaps.

### 4. VoxHammer: Training-Free Precise and Coherent 3D Editing in Native 3D
  Space

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.19247)

VoxHammer is a training-free method that performs precise and coherent 3D editing in latent space, ensuring consistency in preserved regions and high-quality overall results.

### 5. OmniHuman-1.5: Instilling an Active Mind in Avatars via Cognitive
  Simulation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.19209)

A framework using Multimodal Large Language Models and a specialized Multimodal DiT architecture generates semantically coherent and expressive character animations from multimodal inputs.

### 6. Pixie: Fast and Generalizable Supervised Learning of 3D Physics from
  Pixels

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.17437)

PIXIE, a neural network method, predicts physical properties of 3D scenes from visual features, enabling fast and realistic physics simulation using supervised learning and pretrained visual features.

### 7. UltraMemV2: Memory Networks Scaling to 120B Parameters with Superior
  Long-Context Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.18756)

UltraMemV2, a redesigned memory-layer architecture, achieves performance parity with 8-expert MoE models while significantly reducing memory access costs.

### 8. Spacer: Towards Engineered Scientific Inspiration

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.17661)

Spacer, a scientific discovery system, uses deliberate decontextualization to generate creative and factually grounded scientific concepts from keyword sets, achieving high accuracy and similarity to leading publications.

### 9. Autoregressive Universal Video Segmentation Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.19242)

AUSM, an autoregressive universal segmentation model, unifies prompted and unprompted video segmentation by treating it as sequential mask prediction, achieving superior performance and faster training on standard benchmarks.

### 10. Wan-S2V: Audio-Driven Cinematic Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2508.18621)

Wan-S2V, an audio-driven model built on Wan, enhances expressiveness and fidelity in cinematic character animation compared to existing methods.

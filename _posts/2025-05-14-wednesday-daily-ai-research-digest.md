### 1. MiniMax-Speech: Intrinsic Zero-Shot Text-to-Speech with a Learnable
  Speaker Encoder

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07916)

MiniMax-Speech, an autoregressive Transformer-based TTS model, generates high-quality speech with a learnable speaker encoder that extracts reference speaker features without transcription, achieving SOTA results in voice cloning and supporting various extensions.

### 2. Fast Text-to-Audio Generation with Adversarial Post-Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.08175)

Adversarial Relativistic-Contrastive (ARC) post-training optimizes diffusion/flow models for fast text-to-audio generation with minimal latency.

### 3. AM-Thinking-v1: Advancing the Frontier of Reasoning at 32B Scale

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.08311)

AM-Thinking-v1, a 32B dense language model, achieves state-of-the-art performance in mathematical and coding tasks by leveraging supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning, demonstrating the capabilities of mid-scale open-source models.

### 4. Aya Vision: Advancing the Frontier of Multilingual Multimodality

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.08751)

Multi-modal language models are enhanced through synthetic data creation and cross-modal merging techniques, achieving superior performance in multilingual settings.

### 5. A Multi-Dimensional Constraint Framework for Evaluating and Improving
  Instruction Following in Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07591)

An automated pipeline generates diverse, code-verifiable instruction-following test samples, revealing performance variation across constraints in large language models and enhancing instruction following via reinforcement learning.

### 6. Measuring General Intelligence with Generated Games

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.07215)

gg-bench is a dynamic benchmark for evaluating general reasoning in language models by generating new game environments and assessing winrates against reinforcement learning agents.

### 7. Bring Reason to Vision: Understanding Perception and Reasoning through
  Model Merging

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05464)

Merging models across modalities effectively transfers reasoning abilities from LLMs to VLMs without additional training, revealing layer-specific contributions to perception and reasoning.

### 8. Advancing Arabic Reverse Dictionary Systems: A Transformer-Based
  Approach with Dataset Construction Guidelines

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2504.21475)

A transformer-based reverse dictionary system with a semi-encoder architecture achieves superior results for Arabic RD tasks, outperforming multilingual embeddings, and provides quality standards and a Python library for development.

### 9. NavDP: Learning Sim-to-Real Navigation Diffusion Policy with Privileged
  Information Guidance

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.08712)

NavDP, an end-to-end diffusion policy framework trained in simulation, achieves state-of-the-art performance and generalization in diverse real-world environments through efficient data generation and real-to-sim fine-tuning using Gaussian Splatting.

### 10. TRAIL: Trace Reasoning and Agentic Issue Localization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.08638)

Modern long context LLMs struggle with trace debugging of agentic workflows, as evidenced by a new dataset of human-annotated traces.

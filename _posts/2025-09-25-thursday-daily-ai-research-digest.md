### 1. Video models are zero-shot learners and reasoners

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.20328)

Veo 3, a generative video model, exhibits zero-shot capabilities across various visual tasks, suggesting a trajectory towards becoming a unified, generalist vision foundation model.

### 2. EmbeddingGemma: Powerful and Lightweight Text Representations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.20354)

EmbeddingGemma, a lightweight text embedding model based on Gemma 3, achieves state-of-the-art performance with fewer parameters through encoder-decoder initialization, geometric embedding distillation, and spread-out regularization.

### 3. SIM-CoT: Supervised Implicit Chain-of-Thought

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.20317)

SIM-CoT, a plug-and-play training module, introduces step-level supervision to stabilize and enrich the latent reasoning space of implicit Chain-of-Thought methods, enhancing their performance and efficiency.

### 4. Advancing Speech Understanding in Speech-Aware Language Models with GRPO

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.16990)

A Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO)-based method using BLEU as a reward signal outperforms standard SFT for open-format speech understanding tasks like Spoken Question Answering and Automatic Speech Translation.

### 5. EditVerse: Unifying Image and Video Editing and Generation with
  In-Context Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.20360)

EditVerse is a unified framework using self-attention for image and video generation and editing, achieving state-of-the-art performance with a scalable data pipeline and benchmark.

### 6. PhysCtrl: Generative Physics for Controllable and Physics-Grounded Video
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.20358)

PhysCtrl is a physics-grounded framework for generating realistic, controllable videos from images using a diffusion model with spatiotemporal attention and physics-based constraints.

### 7. LLMs4All: A Review on Large Language Models for Research and
  Applications in Academic Disciplines

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.19580)

Large Language Models are transforming various academic disciplines by enabling human-like conversation and enhancing performance in language-related tasks, while also presenting limitations and future challenges.

### 8. Lavida-O: Elastic Large Masked Diffusion Models for Unified Multimodal
  Understanding and Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.19244)

Lavida-O, a unified Masked Diffusion Model, excels in multimodal understanding and generation tasks, including object grounding, image editing, and high-resolution text-to-image synthesis, outperforming existing models with improved efficiency and quality.

### 9. SimpleFold: Folding Proteins is Simpler than You Think

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.18480)

SimpleFold, a flow-matching based protein folding model using general-purpose transformer blocks, achieves competitive performance with reduced complexity and improved efficiency.

### 10. Mixture of Thoughts: Learning to Aggregate What Experts Think, Not Just
  What They Say

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.21164)

Mixture of Thoughts (MoT) is a method for latent-level collaboration among heterogeneous LLMs using a global routing scheme, improving performance over existing routing and aggregation methods.

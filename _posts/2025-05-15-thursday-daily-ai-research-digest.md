### 1. BLIP3-o: A Family of Fully Open Unified Multimodal Models-Architecture,
  Training and Dataset

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09568)

A diffusion transformer is used in a unified multimodal model framework to improve image generation while maintaining image understanding capabilities.

### 2. Insights into DeepSeek-V3: Scaling Challenges and Reflections on
  Hardware for AI Architectures

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09343)

DeepSeek-V3 addresses hardware limitations through MLA, MoE, FP8 training, and Multi-Plane Network Topology, enabling efficient large-scale LLM training and inference.

### 3. MathCoder-VL: Bridging Vision and Code for Enhanced Multimodal
  Mathematical Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.10557)

A new image-to-code model and dataset are developed to enhance multimodal mathematical reasoning, resulting in a model that outperforms current benchmarks in mathematical problem-solving.

### 4. DeCLIP: Decoupled Learning for Open-Vocabulary Dense Perception

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04410)

DeCLIP enhances CLIP by separating content and context features, improving performance on open-vocabulary dense prediction tasks like object detection and semantic segmentation.

### 5. LightLab: Controlling Light Sources in Images with Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09608)

A diffusion-based method fine-tuned on real and synthetic image pairs provides precise control over light sources and ambient illumination in images, offering more effective relighting than existing methods.

### 6. Marigold: Affordable Adaptation of Diffusion-Based Image Generators for
  Image Analysis

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09358)

Marigold, a family of conditional generative models, leverages pretrained latent diffusion models for dense image analysis tasks with minimal modification and minimal data.

### 7. CAST: Component-Aligned 3D Scene Reconstruction from an RGB Image

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2502.12894)

CAST is a method for reconstructing high-quality 3D scenes from single RGB images using GPT models, occlusion-aware 3D generation, and physics-aware correction with Signed Distance Fields.

### 8. UniSkill: Imitating Human Videos via Cross-Embodiment Skill
  Representations

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.08787)

UniSkill learns embodiment-agnostic skill representations from unlabeled cross-embodiment video data, enabling human skill transfer to robots in both simulation and real-world settings.

### 9. WavReward: Spoken Dialogue Models With Generalist Reward Evaluators

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09558)

WavReward, a reward feedback model based on audio language models, evaluates the conversational performance of spoken dialogue systems, addressing the gap in evaluation by incorporating deep reasoning and nonlinear rewards.

### 10. Omni-R1: Do You Really Need Audio to Fine-Tune Your Audio LLM?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.09439)

Omni-R1 fine-tunes Qwen2.5-Omni with GRPO on an audio QA dataset, achieving state-of-the-art performance in sound, music, speech, and average categories.

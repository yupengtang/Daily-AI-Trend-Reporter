### 1. A Theoretical Study on Bridging Internal Probability and
  Self-Consistency for LLM Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15444)

A theoretical framework for sampling-based test-time scaling in large language models reveals limitations in self-consistency and perplexity, and introduces RPC to improve reasoning performance and reduce sampling costs.

### 2. OmniVinci: Enhancing Architecture and Data for Omni-Modal Understanding
  LLM

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15870)

OmniVinci, an open-source omni-modal LLM, enhances cross-modal understanding and performance across audio, vision, and robotics applications with innovative architecture and efficient data curation.

### 3. NANO3D: A Training-Free Approach for Efficient 3D Editing Without Masks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15019)

Nano3D is a training-free framework that integrates FlowEdit and TRELLIS for precise 3D object editing, using front-view renderings and region-aware merging strategies to maintain structural fidelity and visual quality.

### 4. Scaling Instruction-Based Video Editing with a High-Quality Synthetic
  Dataset

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15742)

Ditto framework addresses data scarcity in instruction-based video editing by generating a large dataset and using a curriculum learning strategy to train Editto, achieving superior instruction-following ability.

### 5. Skyfall-GS: Synthesizing Immersive 3D Urban Scenes from Satellite
  Imagery

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15869)

Skyfall-GS creates large-scale, high-quality 3D urban scenes using satellite imagery and diffusion models, offering real-time exploration and improved geometry and texture consistency.

### 6. Latent Diffusion Model without Variational Autoencoder

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15301)

SVG, a novel latent diffusion model without VAEs, uses self-supervised representations to enable efficient training, few-step sampling, and high-quality visual generation with semantic and discriminative capabilities.

### 7. Emergent Misalignment via In-Context Learning: Narrow in-context
  examples can produce broadly misaligned LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.11288)

Emergent misalignment occurs in in-context learning across multiple models and datasets, with misaligned responses increasing with the number of examples provided.

### 8. LightsOut: Diffusion-based Outpainting for Enhanced Lens Flare Removal

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15868)

LightsOut enhances Single Image Flare Removal by reconstructing off-frame light sources using a diffusion-based outpainting framework, improving performance across challenging scenarios.

### 9. Paper2Web: Let's Make Your Paper Alive!

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15842)

Paper2Web is a benchmark and evaluation framework for academic webpage generation, featuring PWAgent, an autonomous pipeline that enhances content and layout through MCP tools, outperforming end-to-end baselines.

### 10. BLIP3o-NEXT: Next Frontier of Native Image Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.15857)

BLIP3o-NEXT, a unified text-to-image generation and image editing model, uses an Autoregressive + Diffusion architecture to achieve superior performance and realism.

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.24533)

A two-stage self-evolving mobile GUI agent named UI-Voyager is proposed, featuring rejection fine-tuning and group relative self-distillation to improve efficiency and performance in GUI automation tasks.

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.14375)

Generative video models suffer from temporal ambiguity due to inconsistent frame rate training, which this work addresses through a Visual Chronometer that estimates physical frames per second from visual dynamics.

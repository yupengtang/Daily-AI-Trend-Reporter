### 1. Efficient Long-context Language Model Training by Core Attention
  Disaggregation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18121)

CAD, a technique for long-context large language model training, improves throughput and balance by decoupling and distributing core attention computations.

### 2. LightMem: Lightweight and Efficient Memory-Augmented Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18866)

LightMem, a memory system inspired by human memory, enhances LLMs by efficiently managing historical interaction information, improving accuracy and reducing computational costs.

### 3. DeepSeek-OCR: Contexts Optical Compression

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18234)

DeepSeek-OCR uses optical 2D mapping to compress long contexts, achieving high OCR precision with reduced vision tokens and demonstrating practical value in document processing.

### 4. World-in-World: World Models in a Closed-Loop World

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18135)

World-in-World evaluates generative world models in closed-loop environments, emphasizing task success over visual quality and revealing insights into controllability, data scaling, and compute allocation.

### 5. Every Step Evolves: Scaling Reinforcement Learning for Trillion-Scale
  Thinking Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18855)

Ring-1T, a trillion-parameter open-source thinking model, addresses training challenges with IcePop, C3PO++, and ASystem, achieving top results across benchmarks and democratizing large-scale reasoning intelligence.

### 6. UniGenBench++: A Unified Semantic Evaluation Benchmark for Text-to-Image
  Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18701)

UniGenBench++ is a comprehensive benchmark for text-to-image generation that evaluates semantic consistency across diverse scenarios and languages using a hierarchical prompt structure and a robust evaluation pipeline.

### 7. Chem-R: Learning to Reason as a Chemist

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.16880)

Chem-R, a three-phase trained Chemical Reasoning model, achieves superior performance on chemical tasks by integrating core knowledge, expert reasoning, and multi-task optimization.

### 8. MoGA: Mixture-of-Groups Attention for End-to-End Long Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18692)

Mixture-of-Groups Attention (MoGA) enables efficient long video generation by addressing the quadratic scaling issue of full attention in Diffusion Transformers.

### 9. Grasp Any Region: Towards Precise, Contextual Pixel Understanding for
  Multimodal LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18876)

Grasp Any Region (GAR) enhances region-level visual understanding by integrating global contexts and modeling interactions, achieving advanced reasoning and outperforming existing models in captioning and video reference tasks.

### 10. IF-VidCap: Can Video Caption Models Follow Instructions?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.18726)

A new benchmark, IF-VidCap, evaluates video captioning models on instruction-following capabilities, revealing that top-tier open-source models are closing the performance gap with proprietary models.

### 1. Scaling RL to Long Videos

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07966)

A framework scales vision-language models for long video reasoning using reinforcement learning, achieving strong performance on benchmarks and demonstrating consistent gains with increased video frames.

### 2. T-LoRA: Single Image Diffusion Model Customization Without Overfitting

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.05964)

T-LoRA, a timestep-dependent low-rank adaptation framework, enhances diffusion model personalization with a dynamic fine-tuning strategy and orthogonal initialization, improving concept fidelity and text alignment in data-limited settings.

### 3. Traceable Evidence Enhanced Visual Grounded Reasoning: Evaluation and
  Methodology

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07999)

TreeBench evaluates visual grounded reasoning with a focus on subtle targets, traceable evidence, and second-order reasoning, while TreeVGR enhances this reasoning using reinforcement learning.

### 4. Multi-Granular Spatio-Temporal Token Merging for Training-Free
  Acceleration of Video LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07990)

A training-free method merges spatio-temporal tokens in video large language models to reduce computational cost while maintaining accuracy.

### 5. OST-Bench: Evaluating the Capabilities of MLLMs in Online
  Spatio-temporal Scene Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07984)

OST-Bench evaluates multimodal large language models on online spatio-temporal reasoning tasks, highlighting challenges in dynamic spatial understanding and long-term memory retrieval.

### 6. LangSplatV2: High-dimensional 3D Language Gaussian Splatting with 450+ FPS

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07136)

LangSplatV2 improves the speed and accuracy of 3D open-vocabulary text querying by eliminating the decoder and using sparse Gaussian splatting.

### 7. Skip a Layer or Loop it? Test-Time Depth Adaptation of Pretrained LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07996)

A method using chain-of-layers (CoLa) and Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) optimizes the architecture of a pretrained large language model for individual samples, improving inference efficiency and performance.

### 8. Geometry Forcing: Marrying Video Diffusion and 3D Representation for
  Consistent World Modeling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07982)

Geometry Forcing enhances video diffusion models by aligning their intermediate representations with geometric features from a pretrained model, improving visual quality and 3D consistency.

### 9. PyVision: Agentic Vision with Dynamic Tooling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07998)

PyVision enables MLLMs to autonomously generate, execute, and refine Python-based tools for visual reasoning, achieving significant performance improvements across benchmarks.

### 10. Dynamic Chunking for End-to-End Hierarchical Sequence Modeling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.07955)

Hierarchical networks (H-Nets) enable end-to-end learning by dynamically segmenting data, outperforming token-based models in various languages and modalities.

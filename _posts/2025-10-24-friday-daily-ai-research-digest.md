  Decoders

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19779)

AdaSPEC enhances speculative decoding by selectively filtering tokens during knowledge distillation, improving token acceptance rates without sacrificing generation quality.

### 3. Open-o3 Video: Grounded Video Reasoning with Explicit Spatio-Temporal
  Evidence

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.20579)

Open-o3 Video integrates spatio-temporal evidence into video reasoning, achieving state-of-the-art performance on multiple benchmarks and providing valuable reasoning traces for test-time scaling.

### 4. HoloCine: Holistic Generation of Cinematic Multi-Shot Long Video
  Narratives

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.20822)

HoloCine generates coherent multi-shot narratives using a Window Cross-Attention mechanism and Sparse Inter-Shot Self-Attention, enabling end-to-end cinematic creation.

### 5. DyPE: Dynamic Position Extrapolation for Ultra High Resolution Diffusion

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.20766)

Dynamic Position Extrapolation (DyPE) enhances ultra-high-resolution image generation by dynamically adjusting positional encodings in pre-trained diffusion transformers, achieving state-of-the-art fidelity without additional sampling cost.

### 6. Loopholing Discrete Diffusion: Deterministic Bypass of the Sampling Wall

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19304)

Loopholing Discrete Diffusion Models (LDDMs) enhance text generation by preserving distributional information through a deterministic latent pathway, reducing perplexity and improving coherence and performance on reasoning tasks.

### 7. Seed3D 1.0: From Images to High-Fidelity Simulation-Ready 3D Assets

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.19944)

Seed3D 1.0 generates scalable, physics-accurate 3D assets from images for use in simulation environments, enhancing both content diversity and real-time physics feedback.

### 8. Every Question Has Its Own Value: Reinforcement Learning with Explicit
  Human Values

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.20187)

RLEV, a reinforcement learning method, aligns LLM optimization with human value signals, improving value-weighted accuracy and learning value-sensitive termination policies.

### 9. SAKE: Towards Editing Auditory Attribute Knowledge of Large
  Audio-Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.16917)

SAKE is a benchmark for editing auditory attribute knowledge in Large Audio-Language Models, addressing challenges in reliability, generality, locality, and portability.

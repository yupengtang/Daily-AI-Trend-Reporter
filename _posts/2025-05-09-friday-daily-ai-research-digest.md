### 1. Perception, Reason, Think, and Plan: A Survey on Large Multimodal
  Reasoning Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04921)

This survey outlines the development of multimodal reasoning models, progressing from task-specific modules to unified frameworks, and discusses emerging capabilities and challenges in integrating reasoning across diverse data types.

### 2. Flow-GRPO: Training Flow Matching Models via Online RL

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05470)

Flow-GRPO combines online reinforcement learning with flow matching models through an ODE-to-SDE conversion and denoising reduction, improving sampling efficiency and performance across text-to-image tasks.

### 3. On Path to Multimodal Generalist: General-Level and General-Bench

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.04620)

A General-Level evaluation framework is introduced to assess multimodal generalist performance in LLMs, using Synergy to measure consistent capabilities across comprehension and generation.

### 4. Sentient Agent as a Judge: Evaluating Higher-Order Social Cognition in
  Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.02847)

SAGE, an automated evaluation framework, assesses the higher-order social cognition and empathy of large language models through simulated human-like emotions and inner thoughts in multi-turn conversations.

### 5. Generating Physically Stable and Buildable LEGO Designs from Text

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05469)

LegoGPT generates physically stable LEGO models from text prompts using a large language model with physics-aware constraints and supports manual and automated assembly.

### 6. Scalable Chain of Thoughts via Elastic Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05315)

Elastic Reasoning is a framework that divides reasoning into thinking and solution phases with separate budgets, improving model reliability and efficiency under resource constraints.

### 7. 3D Scene Generation: A Survey

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05474)

This survey examines the state-of-the-art methods in 3D scene generation, categorizing them into procedural, neural 3D, image-based, and video-based paradigms, and discusses current challenges and future directions.

### 8. FG-CLIP: Fine-Grained Visual and Textual Alignment

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05071)

FG-CLIP enhances fine-grained understanding in multimodal tasks by leveraging large multimodal models, a high-quality dataset with detailed captions, and hard fine-grained negative samples.

### 9. X-Reasoner: Towards Generalizable Reasoning Across Modalities and
  Domains

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.03981)

X-Reasoner, a vision-language model post-trained on general-domain text, achieves strong reasoning capabilities across modalities and domains, with X-Reasoner-Med outperforming existing models on medical benchmarks.

### 10. PlaceIt3D: Language-Guided Object Placement in Real 3D Scenes

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.05288)

A new task for placing 3D assets in real 3D scenes based on textual prompts is introduced, with a benchmark and dataset for evaluating 3D LLMs.

### 1. Lumine: An Open Recipe for Building Generalist Agents in 3D Open Worlds

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.08892)

Lumine, a vision-language model-based agent, completes complex missions in real-time across different 3D open-world environments with human-like efficiency and zero-shot cross-game generalization.

### 2. TiDAR: Think in Diffusion, Talk in Autoregression

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.08923)

TiDAR combines diffusion and autoregressive models to achieve high throughput and quality in language generation.

### 3. MADD: Multi-Agent Drug Discovery Orchestra

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.08217)

MADD, a multi-agent system integrating large language models and specialized models, streamlines hit identification in early drug discovery with superior performance and accessibility.

### 4. Time-to-Move: Training-Free Motion Controlled Video Generation via Dual-Clock Denoising

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.08633)

Time-to-Move (TTM) is a plug-and-play framework for motion- and appearance-controlled video generation using image-to-video (I2V) diffusion models, offering precise control over video content without requiring additional training.

### 5. Motif 2 12.7B technical report

Motif-2-12.7B combines architectural innovations and system optimizations to enhance efficiency and performance in large language models.

### 6. WMPO: World Model-based Policy Optimization for Vision-Language-Action Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.09515)

WMPO, a pixel-based world-model framework for VLA RL, enhances sample efficiency, performance, self-correction, and generalization in robotic manipulation tasks.

### 7. LoopTool: Closing the Data-Training Loop for Robust LLM Tool Calls

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.09148)

A fully automated data evolution framework, LoopTool, enhances tool-use capabilities of Large Language Models by iteratively refining data and model through a closed-loop process.

### 8. WebVIA: A Web-based Vision-Language Agentic Framework for Interactive and Verifiable UI-to-Code Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.06251)

WebVIA is an agentic framework for generating executable and interactive UI code from design mockups, improving stability and accuracy over existing models.

### 9. MathSE: Improving Multimodal Mathematical Reasoning via Self-Evolving Iterative Reflection and Reward-Guided Fine-Tuning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.06805)

A proposed Mathematical Self-Evolving framework iteratively refines multimodal large language models through inference, reflection, and reward-based feedback, achieving superior performance in mathematical reasoning tasks compared to existing models.

### 10. Adapting Web Agents with Synthetic Supervision

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2511.06101)

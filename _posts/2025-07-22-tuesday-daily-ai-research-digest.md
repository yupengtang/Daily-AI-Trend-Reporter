### 1. MiroMind-M1: An Open-Source Advancement in Mathematical Reasoning via
  Context-Aware Multi-Stage Policy Optimization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.14683)

The MiroMind-M1 series, built on Qwen-2.5, introduces fully open-source reasoning language models with state-of-the-art performance in mathematical reasoning through Context-Aware Multi-Stage Policy Optimization.

### 2. GUI-G^2: Gaussian Reward Modeling for GUI Grounding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15846)

A new reward framework, GUI-G$^2$, models GUI elements as continuous Gaussian distributions to improve autonomous interaction through dense gradient signals, outperforming existing methods in spatial reasoning tasks.

### 3. The Invisible Leash: Why RLVR May Not Escape Its Origin

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.14843)

Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) enhances precision but may limit exploration and discovery of new solutions, suggesting potential limits to its effectiveness in expanding reasoning capabilities.

### 4. WebShaper: Agentically Data Synthesizing via Information-Seeking
  Formalization

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15061)

WebShaper, a formalization-driven framework, synthesizes information-seeking datasets using set theory and Knowledge Projections to enhance reasoning structure and achieve top performance in open-sourced benchmarks.

### 5. NoHumansRequired: Autonomous High-Quality Image Editing Triplet Mining

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.14119)

An automated pipeline mines high-fidelity image editing triplets using generative models and a task-tuned validator, enabling large-scale training without human labeling.

### 6. GR-3 Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15493)

GR-3, a large-scale vision-language-action model, demonstrates exceptional generalization and fine-tuning capabilities, excelling in complex tasks and outperforming state-of-the-art methods.

### 7. SeC: Advancing Complex Video Object Segmentation via Progressive Concept
  Construction

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15852)

A concept-driven segmentation framework using Large Vision-Language Models improves video object segmentation by integrating high-level semantic reasoning and adapting to scene complexity.

### 8. Robust 3D-Masked Part-level Editing in 3D Gaussian Splatting with
  Regularized Score Distillation Sampling

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.11061)

RoMaP, a novel framework, enhances precise local 3D Gaussian editing through robust 3D mask generation and regularized Score Distillation Sampling loss, achieving state-of-the-art results.

### 9. Being-H0: Vision-Language-Action Pretraining from Large-Scale Human
  Videos

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15597)

Being-H0, a Vision-Language-Action model, uses human videos and physical instruction tuning to achieve high dexterity in manipulation tasks and scales well with data and model size.

### 10. STITCH: Simultaneous Thinking and Talking with Chunked Reasoning for
  Spoken Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.15375)

Stitch is a novel method that enables simultaneous thinking and talking in spoken language models by alternating between generating unspoken reasoning and spoken response chunks, achieving low latency and improved performance on math reasoning tasks.

### 1. Orca: The World is in Your Mind

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.30534)

Orca establishes a unified world latent space through next-state-prediction modeling using multimodal data and demonstrates superior performance in downstream tasks compared to specialized baselines.

### 2. Dockerless: Environment-Free Program Verifier for Coding Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.28436)

A Dockerless environment-free agentic patch verifier improves code patch evaluation accuracy and enables effective post-training without execution-based verification costs.

### 3. DOPD: Dual On-policy Distillation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.30626)

DOPD addresses privilege illusion in on-policy distillation by dynamically routing token-level supervision between teacher and student policies based on advantage gaps and probabilities, improving capability transfer in large and vision-language models.

### 4. BlockPilot: Instance-Adaptive Policy Learning for Diffusion-based Speculative Decoding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.31315)

Speculative decoding with adaptive block size selection improves inference efficiency by predicting optimal block sizes from prefilling representations, achieving significant speedup with minimal overhead.

### 5. Scenes as Objects, Not Primitives: Instance-Structured 3D Tokenization from Unposed Views

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.29513)

A feed-forward framework decomposes 3D scenes into instance-structured token groups from multi-view images, enabling direct object-level reconstruction, segmentation, and manipulation without 3D annotations.

### 6. GEAR: Guided End-to-End AutoRegression for Image Synthesis

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.32039)

GEAR trains a vector-quantized tokenizer and autoregressive generator jointly end-to-end using representation alignment, overcoming non-differentiability issues through a dual read-out approach that improves convergence speed and feature quality.

### 7. Multi-Block Diffusion Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.29215)

Multi-Block Diffusion Language Models extend single-block diffusion to concurrent block decoding with improved training strategies and optimized decoding algorithms.

### 8. Evolution Fine-Tuning: Learning to Discover Across 371 Optimization Tasks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.29082)

Evolutionary fine-tuning enables large language models to develop cross-task problem-solving capabilities by learning from search trajectories, demonstrating improved performance on mathematical conjectures and optimization tasks.

### 9. SkillHone: A Harness for Continual Agent Skill Evolution Through Persistent Decision History

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.08671)

SkillHone enables continuous evolution of agent skills by maintaining persistent decision histories and incorporating practice feedback for improved performance across research and tool-mediated analysis tasks.

### 10. MemLearner: Learning to Query Context memory for Video World Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2606.31734)

MemLearner improves video world models by using learning-based adaptive context querying with query tokens to enhance scene consistency and memory in long video sequences with occlusions and dynamic objects.

### 1. Memory in the Age of AI Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.13564)

This survey provides an updated overview of agent memory research, distinguishing its forms, functions, and dynamics, and highlights emerging research directions.

### 2. QwenLong-L1.5: Post-Training Recipe for Long-Context Reasoning and Memory Management

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.12967)

QwenLong-L1.5 enhances long-context reasoning through data synthesis, stabilized reinforcement learning, and memory-augmented architecture, achieving superior performance on benchmarks and general domains.

### 3. Towards Scalable Pre-training of Visual Tokenizers for Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.13687)

A unified visual tokenizer pre-training framework (VTP) improves generative performance by optimizing image-text contrastive, self-supervised, and reconstruction losses, leading to better scaling properties and higher zero-shot accuracy and faster convergence.

### 4. ReFusion: A Diffusion Large Language Model with Parallel Autoregressive Decoding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.13586)

ReFusion, a novel masked diffusion model, improves performance and efficiency by using slot-based parallel decoding, achieving superior results compared to autoregressive models and traditional masked diffusion models.

### 5. LongVie 2: Multimodal Controllable Ultra-Long Video World Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.13604)

LongVie 2, an end-to-end autoregressive framework, enhances controllability, visual quality, and temporal consistency in video world models through three progressive training stages.

### 6. Finch: Benchmarking Finance & Accounting across Spreadsheet-Centric Enterprise Workflows

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.13168)

A finance and accounting benchmark named Finch is presented, featuring real-world enterprise workflows with complex, multi-modal tasks derived from authentic corporate environments.

### 7. NL2Repo-Bench: Towards Long-Horizon Repository Generation Evaluation of Coding Agents

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.12730)

NL2Repo Bench evaluates long-horizon software development capabilities of coding agents by assessing their ability to generate complete Python libraries from natural-language requirements.

### 8. KlingAvatar 2.0 Technical Report

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.13313)

KlingAvatar 2.0 addresses inefficiencies in generating long-duration, high-resolution videos by using a spatio-temporal cascade framework with a Co-Reasoning Director and Negative Director for improved multimodal instruction alignment.

### 9. Error-Free Linear Attention is a Free Lunch: Exact Solution from Continuous-Time Dynamics

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.12602)

Error-Free Linear Attention (EFLA) is a stable, parallelizable, and theoretically sound linear-time attention mechanism that outperforms DeltaNet in language modeling and downstream tasks.

### 10. MentraSuite: Post-Training Large Language Models for Mental Health Reasoning and Assessment

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2512.09636)

A unified framework and benchmark called MentraSuite is introduced to enhance reliable mental-health reasoning in large language models, with a post-trained model named Mindora achieving superior performance through a hybrid SFT-RL approach optimized for consistency and faithfulness.

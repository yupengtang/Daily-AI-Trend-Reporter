### 1. Beyond the 80/20 Rule: High-Entropy Minority Tokens Drive Effective
  Reinforcement Learning for LLM Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.01939)

Token entropy patterns are crucial in RLVR, with high-entropy tokens significantly impacting reasoning performance and model optimization.

### 2. SmolVLA: A Vision-Language-Action Model for Affordable and Efficient
  Robotics

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.01844)

SmolVLA is a compact, efficient vision-language-action model that achieves competitive performance at reduced computational costs and can be deployed on consumer-grade hardware.

### 3. REASONING GYM: Reasoning Environments for Reinforcement Learning with
  Verifiable Rewards

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24760)

Reasoning Gym provides a library of reasoning environments with verifiable rewards and procedural data generation for reinforcement learning, enabling the evaluation and training of reasoning models at varying difficulty levels.

### 4. SRPO: Enhancing Multimodal LLM Reasoning via Reflection-Aware
  Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.01713)

A two-stage reinforcement learning framework enhances reasoning and self-reflection in multimodal large language models through reflection-focused datasets and a novel reward mechanism.

### 5. Temporal In-Context Fine-Tuning for Versatile Control of Video Diffusion
  Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.00996)

Temporal In-Context Fine-Tuning (TIC-FT) enhances pretrained video diffusion models for diverse conditional generation tasks with minimal data and without architectural changes.

### 6. Taming LLMs by Scaling Learning Rates with Gradient Grouping

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.01049)

SGG, an optimizer wrapper, enhances adaptive learning rates for large language models by grouping gradients and applying cluster-specific scaling, improving convergence and stability.

### 7. AReaL: A Large-Scale Asynchronous Reinforcement Learning System for
  Language Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2505.24298)

AReaL, a fully asynchronous reinforcement learning system, decouples generation and training to achieve higher GPU utilization and up to 2.57x training speedup for large language models on reasoning tasks.

### 8. ShapeLLM-Omni: A Native Multimodal LLM for 3D Generation and
  Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.01853)

A native 3D large language model, ShapeLLM-Omni, is proposed to understand and generate 3D assets and text, trained using a 3D vector-quantized variational autoencoder and a new 3D-Alpaca dataset.

### 9. LoHoVLA: A Unified Vision-Language-Action Model for Long-Horizon
  Embodied Tasks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.00411)

A unified vision language action framework, LoHoVLA, combines a large pretrained vision language model with hierarchical closed-loop control to improve performance on long-horizon embodied tasks.

### 10. ARIA: Training Language Agents with Intention-Driven Reward Aggregation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.00539)

ARIA aggregates rewards in an intention space to mitigate reward sparsity and improve policy optimization in language-based reinforcement learning tasks.

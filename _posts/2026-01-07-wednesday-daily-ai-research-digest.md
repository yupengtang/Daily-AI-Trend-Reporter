### 1. LTX-2: Efficient Joint Audio-Visual Foundation Model

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.03233)

### 2. InfiniDepth: Arbitrary-Resolution and Fine-Grained Depth Estimation with Neural Implicit Fields

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.03252)

InfiniDepth represents depth as neural implicit fields using a local implicit decoder, enabling continuous 2D coordinate querying for arbitrary-resolution depth estimation and superior performance in fine-detail regions.

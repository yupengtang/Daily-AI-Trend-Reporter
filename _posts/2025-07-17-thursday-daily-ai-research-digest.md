### 1. Towards Agentic RAG with Deep Reasoning: A Survey of RAG-Reasoning
  Systems in LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.09477)

This survey integrates reasoning and retrieval in Large Language Models to improve factuality and multi-step inference, highlighting Synergized RAG-Reasoning frameworks and outlining future research directions.

### 2. PhysX: Physical-Grounded 3D Asset Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12465)

PhysX addresses the gap in physical-grounded 3D asset generation by introducing PhysXNet, a physics-annotated dataset, and PhysXGen, a feed-forward framework that integrates physical knowledge into 3D generation.

### 3. SWE-Perf: Can Language Models Optimize Code Performance on Real-World
  Repositories?

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12415)

SWE-Perf is a benchmark for evaluating LLMs in code performance optimization using real-world repository data, revealing significant gaps compared to expert performance.

### 4. DrafterBench: Benchmarking Large Language Models for Tasks Automation in
  Civil Engineering

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.11527)

DrafterBench is an open-source benchmark for evaluating LLM agents in technical drawing revision, assessing their capabilities in structured data comprehension, function execution, instruction following, and critical reasoning.

### 5. Seq vs Seq: An Open Suite of Paired Encoders and Decoders

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.11412)

The Ettin suite of models demonstrates that encoder-only and decoder-only architectures perform optimally in their respective tasks, with encoder-only models excelling at classification and retrieval, and decoder-only models at generation, and that adapting models to different tasks through continued training is less effective.

### 6. MMHU: A Massive-Scale Multimodal Benchmark for Human Behavior
  Understanding

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12463)

A large-scale benchmark, MMHU, is proposed for human behavior analysis in autonomous driving, featuring rich annotations and diverse data sources, and benchmarking various tasks including motion prediction and behavior question answering.

### 7. MOSPA: Human Motion Generation Driven by Spatial Audio

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.11949)

A diffusion-based generative framework, MOSPA, is introduced to model human motion in response to spatial audio, achieving state-of-the-art performance using the newly created SAM dataset.

### 8. SpatialTrackerV2: 3D Point Tracking Made Easy

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.12462)

SpatialTrackerV2 is a feed-forward 3D point tracking method for monocular videos that integrates scene geometry, camera ego-motion, and object motion into a unified, differentiable architecture, achieving high accuracy and speed.

### 9. Lizard: An Efficient Linearization Framework for Large Language Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.09025)

Lizard is a linearization framework that transforms Transformer-based LLMs into subquadratic architectures for efficient infinite-context generation, using a hybrid attention mechanism and hardware-aware training.

### 10. Replacing thinking with tool usage enables reasoning in small language
  models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2507.05065)

A new approach formats tokens as a multi-turn interaction trace with a stateful tool for training Large Language Models, enabling faster sampling and denser reward signals for tasks like repairing Python code.

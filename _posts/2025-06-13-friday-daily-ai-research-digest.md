### 1. ReasonMed: A 370K Multi-Agent Generated Dataset for Advancing Medical
  Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.09513)

ReasonMed, a large medical reasoning dataset, enhances the accuracy of medical question answering models by combining detailed reasoning paths with concise summaries, setting new benchmarks for model performance.

### 2. Magistral

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10910)

Magistral, a scalable reinforcement learning pipeline, demonstrates that RL can enhance multimodal understanding and instruction following in large language models without requiring existing RL traces.

### 3. SWE-Factory: Your Automated Factory for Issue Resolution Training Data
  and Evaluation Benchmarks

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10954)

A pipeline named SWE-Factory automates the creation and validation of GitHub issue resolution datasets for training and evaluating Large Language Models, using SWE-Builder for environment setup, exit-code-based grading, and automated fail2pass validation.

### 4. Text-Aware Image Restoration with Diffusion Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.09993)

The proposed Text-Aware Image Restoration (TAIR) system integrates a multi-task diffusion framework with a text-spotting module to enhance both image recovery and textual fidelity, outperforming existing diffusion-based methods.

### 5. AniMaker: Automated Multi-Agent Animated Storytelling with MCTS-Driven
  Clip Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10540)

AniMaker, a multi-agent framework using MCTS-Gen and AniEval, generates coherent storytelling videos from text input, outperforming existing models with better quality and efficiency.

### 6. Ming-Omni: A Unified Multimodal Model for Perception and Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.09344)

Ming-Omni is a unified multimodal model with dedicated encoders and modality-specific routers that can process images, text, audio, and video, and performs tasks like speech and image generation, context-aware chatting, and versatile image editing.

### 7. Discrete Audio Tokens: More Than a Survey!

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10274)

A systematic review and benchmark of discrete audio tokenizers across speech, music, and general audio domains is presented, covering their taxonomy, evaluation metrics, and limitations.

### 8. VRBench: A Benchmark for Multi-Step Reasoning in Long Narrative Videos

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10857)

VRBench is a long narrative video benchmark designed to evaluate models' multi-step reasoning and procedural validity through human-labeled question-answering pairs and a human-AI collaborative framework with a multi-phase evaluation pipeline.

### 9. PosterCraft: Rethinking High-Quality Aesthetic Poster Generation in a
  Unified Framework

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.10741)

PosterCraft improves aesthetic poster generation through a unified, modular pipeline with enhanced text rendering, region-aware fine-tuning, aesthetic reinforcement learning, and joint vision-language refinement.

### 10. Comment on The Illusion of Thinking: Understanding the Strengths and
  Limitations of Reasoning Models via the Lens of Problem Complexity

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2506.09250)

Evaluation artifacts, particularly token limits and impractical instances in benchmarks, lead to misreported failures in Large Reasoning Models on planning puzzles.

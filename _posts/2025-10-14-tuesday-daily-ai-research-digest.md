### 1. QeRL: Beyond Efficiency -- Quantization-enhanced Reinforcement Learning
  for LLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.11696)

QeRL, a quantization-enhanced reinforcement learning framework, accelerates RL training for large language models by combining NVFP4 quantization with Low-Rank Adaptation and an Adaptive Quantization Noise mechanism, achieving significant speedups and improved performance.

### 2. Diffusion Transformers with Representation Autoencoders

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.11690)

Replacing VAEs with pretrained representation encoders in Diffusion Transformers enhances generative quality and convergence speed without auxiliary losses.

### 3. Latent Refinement Decoding: Enhancing Diffusion-Based Language Models by
  Refining Belief States

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.11052)

Latent Refinement Decoding (LRD) improves parallel sequence generation by maintaining global consistency and iterative refinement, enhancing accuracy and reducing latency.

### 4. OmniVideoBench: Towards Audio-Visual Understanding Evaluation for Omni
  MLLMs

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.10689)

OmniVideoBench is a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating audio-visual reasoning in multimodal large language models, addressing modality complementarity and logical consistency.

### 5. Spotlight on Token Perception for Multimodal Reinforcement Learning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.09285)

VPPO, a novel policy gradient algorithm, enhances multimodal RLVR by leveraging token perception to refine learning signals and improve reasoning capabilities in Large Vision-Language Models.

### 6. InternSVG: Towards Unified SVG Tasks with Multimodal Large Language
  Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.11341)

A unified multimodal large language model (MLLM) for SVG understanding, editing, and generation leverages a comprehensive dataset and benchmark to achieve superior performance across various tasks.

### 7. RLFR: Extending Reinforcement Learning for LLMs with Flow Environment

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.10201)

RLFR uses flow rewards derived from latent space to improve reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards, demonstrating reliable reward shaping and efficient context comprehension.

### 8. Demystifying Reinforcement Learning in Agentic Reasoning

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.11701)

Agentic reinforcement learning enhances LLMs' reasoning ability through real datasets, exploration techniques, and a deliberative strategy, achieving strong performance with smaller models.

### 9. AVoCaDO: An Audiovisual Video Captioner Driven by Temporal Orchestration

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.10395)

AVoCaDO, an audiovisual video captioner, enhances temporal coherence and dialogue accuracy through a two-stage post-training pipeline, outperforming existing models across multiple benchmarks.

### 10. DiT360: High-Fidelity Panoramic Image Generation via Hybrid Training

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2510.11712)

DiT360 framework enhances panoramic image generation by hybrid training on perspective and panoramic data, incorporating cross-domain knowledge and hybrid supervision to improve boundary consistency and image fidelity.

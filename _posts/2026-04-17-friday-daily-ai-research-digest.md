### 6. Dive into Claude Code: The Design Space of Today's and Future AI Agent Systems

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.14228)

### 7. HiVLA: A Visual-Grounded-Centric Hierarchical Embodied Manipulation System

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.14125)

### 8. ASGuard: Activation-Scaling Guard to Mitigate Targeted Jailbreaking Attack

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2509.25843)

Activation-Scaling Guard (ASGuard) mitigates brittle refusal behaviors in large language models by identifying and recalibrating specific attention heads vulnerable to tense-based jailbreaking attacks through mechanistic circuit analysis and targeted fine-tuning.

### 9. UniDoc-RL: Coarse-to-Fine Visual RAG with Hierarchical Actions and Dense Rewards

### 10. LeapAlign: Post-Training Flow Matching Models at Any Generation Step by Building Two-Step Trajectories

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.15311)

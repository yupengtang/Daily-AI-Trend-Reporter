[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.08763)

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.09667)

Multi-Agent Test-Time Reinforcement Learning (MATTRL) enhances multi-agent reasoning through structured textual experience injection and consensus-based decision making at inference time.

### 5. FlowAct-R1: Towards Interactive Humanoid Video Generation

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.10103)

FlowAct-R1 enables real-time interactive humanoid video generation with high-fidelity synthesis and low-latency responsiveness through MMDiT architecture and chunkwise diffusion forcing strategies.

### 6. VIBE: Visual Instruction Based Editor

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.02242)

A compact image editing system uses a 2B-parameter model for guidance and a 1.6B-parameter diffusion model to achieve high-quality edits with low computational requirements and strict source consistency.

### 7. HeartMuLa: A Family of Open Sourced Music Foundation Models

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.10547)

A suite of open-source music foundation models is introduced, featuring components for audio-text alignment, lyric recognition, music coding, and large language model-based song generation with controllable attributes and scalable parameterization.

[Read Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2601.10402)

### 10. DanQing: An Up-to-Date Large-Scale Chinese Vision-Language Pre-training Dataset
